Snatch Balance + 2 Overhead Squats

2 sets @ 70% of Max Snatch Balance

2 sets @ 80%

1 set @ 85%

1 set @ 90%

 

Notes

Build up with sets of 2 snatch balances + 2 overhead squats until you reach 70%. Focus on technique and control. Go with technical snatch balances and slow, solid overhead squats. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets.

—

 

Hang Squat Snatch

2×2 @ 60% of Max Hang Squat Snatch

2×2 @ 70%

2×2 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

2×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve explosiveness and stability. Focus on a solid, hang position, an explosive pull and a fast drive under the barbell. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Conditioning

For time

4 rounds of

12 chest to bar pull-ups

4 squat cleans 80/52kg – 175/115lb

Then

4 rounds of

12 chest to bar pull-ups

4 shoulders to overhead 80/52kg – 175/115lb

Cap: 13 minutes

 

Notes

Ideally go the chest to bar pull-ups unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. Try to complete the shoulder to overheads unbroken and go the squat cleans with single lifts at a solid, steady tempo. Pace the first couplet, push the second couplet harder.

Options

Chest to bar pull-ups → pull-ups

*Go with chest to bar pull-ups if you can perform at least 12 unbroken reps while fresh.

80/52kg – 175/115lb → 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Front Squat

1×5 @ 65%

1×4 @ 75%

1×3 @ 80%

1×2 @ 85%

4×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Build up to 65% with sets of 3-4 reps. Improve strength. Focus on a proper front rack and a solid bottom position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Gymnastics

5 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

20 GHD sit-ups

100 double-unders

Max reps, Toes to bar

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Go the GHD sit-ups unbroken and the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible, when you break, immediately continue with the remaining reps. On the toes to bar start with a big set and continue with some smaller ones, prefer to break into manageable sets that will allow you to keep moving efficiently

Engine

A. Ski-erg, 10 minutes @ easy pace

 

B. 6 sets of

Ski-erg, 1000 meters @ increasing effort

Rest 90 seconds between sets

 

Notes

Warm up by skiing 10 minutes at an easy pace. In the main session the goal is to start with a fast yet manageable pace and progressively increase with each set until you reach a challenging yet sustainable pace in the last set. Cool down skiing 4 minutes at very easy pace.

Options

Ski-erg → row
