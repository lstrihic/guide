5 snatch pulls + 5 hang power snatches + 3 overhead presses + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Improve pulling power and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Shoulder taps

Goal

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Your upper body will be taxed from the strict exercises so try to be as technical as possible on the kipping exercises.

Movements

Ideally go each exercise unbroken for as long as proper form allows, if you break take short rest and continue with smaller sets. Alternatively break from the start into a big and no more than 2-3 smaller sets that will allow you to move efficiently through the sesion.

Options

Engine

5 sets of

AMRAP 4 Minutes of

Bike-erg, 500/450 meters

Run, 300 meters

Max distance, Bike-erg

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set. The goal is to push efficiently through the couplet and then maintain the fastest sustainable pace on the bike in the remaining time until the 4-minute time frame is completed.

Tempo

Push explosively through the 500/450 meters on the bike-erg, then run at a steady sustainable pace throughout the session. On the run try to breathe well and avoid pace variations. After the run go straight to the bike, reach the intented pace as fast as possible and then try to maintain it until the last second.
