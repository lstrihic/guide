Then,

Goal

Row at a steady pace throughout the session that will allow you to move efficiently on the exercises. Start by pacing and push harder as volume decreases and you reach the finish.

Movements

In the first round break the toes to bar into manageable sets and the deadlifts into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between. Try to go both exercises unbroken in the next rounds.

Warm up

3 sets of

5 deadlifts + 5 front squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 front squats until 70%.

Goal

Improve maximal strength and consistency. Complete 3 consecutive short build-ups increasing each time the starting percentage 5% until you reach a lift at 100%. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a solid front rack position and a strict bottom position (no bounce).

Engine

2 sets of

Bike-erg, 2000 meters

Ski-erg, 1000 meters

Bike-erg, 2000 meters

300 double-unders

Rest 5 minutes between sets

 

Goal

Try to achieve maximum performance in each set. Put high effort and push each set hard, try to keep the score of the second set as equal to the first as possible.

Tempo

Move steadily on the ergs at a sustainable pace with focus on breathing well. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take a short breath and immediately continue with the remaining reps.
