Power Clean & Jerk

1×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

3×1 @ 80%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve power and stability. Focus on a powerful pull, an explosive jerk and solid receiving positions. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Clean Pull

2×3 @ 90%

2×2 @ 100%

2×1 @ 105%

 

Notes

Continue to the clean pulls from where you left the power clean jerks. Improve starting strength. Start with a solid first pull and increase speed once the barbell is over the knees. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 5 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

A. AMRAP 8 Minutes of

8 strict handstand push-ups

8 burpees to target

8 power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb

 

Rest 4 minutes

 

B. AMRAP 8 Minutes of

8 kipping handstand push-ups

8 burpees to target

4 power snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb

 

Notes

This workout consists of 2 parts, try to achieve maximum performance in both of them. Start steadily and increase your pace to max effort as you reach the finish. Ideally, go each exercise in each part unbroken with smooth transitions between them. Alternatively prefer to break the strict handstand push-ups into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between. Mark a target 10cm above your extended arms overhead for the burpees (eg. pull-up bar) , go at a fast pace thaw will allow you to effortlessly touch the targer on every rep.

Options

A. Strict → kipping handstand

Choose to go with kipping if you can’t perform 8 unbroken strict hspu while fresh

B. 61/43kg – 135/95lb → 52/37kg – 115/80lb

Barbell Cycling

For form

3 rounds of

50 double-unders

5 hang power cleans 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Then,

3 rounds of

50 double-unders

5 power cleans 75/52kg – 165/115lb

Then,

3 round of

50 double-unders

5 squat cleans 95/61kg – 210/135lb

 

Notes

This workout consists of 3 consecutive parts with each part the barbell load being increased yet the difficulty of the movement decreased. Go the double-unders unbroken, perform fast transitions from the barbell to the jump rope, catch your breath before you start the lifts. Go the hang power cleans in each round unbroken. Go the power cleans touch & go for as long as you can and, if you need to break, continue without rest with single lifts. Start the squat cleans with single lifts and switch to touch & go as you reach the finish, if you feel comfortable enough.

Gymnastics

For form

2 rounds of

60 GHD sit-ups

45 meters, Handstand walk

Notes

Focus on form and quality of movements. Go the GHD with a big set and continue with some smaller sets, and the single leg squats at a fast, steady tempo. If you are comfortable with handstand walk break the total distance into 15-meter distances to challenge yourself, if not prefer to go with 7,5 meter distances.

Engine

A. 15 minutes @ easy pace

 

B. 14 sets of

2 minutes @ 85% of Vo2Max

Rest 1 minute between sets

Rest 3 minutes after the 7th set

Notes

Run 15 minutes (warm-up) and 10 minutes (cool down) at a comfortable pace of your choice. Be consistent throughout the session, try not to force your pace, instead make sure you maintain around 85% of Vo2Max in all 14 sets. Prefer to walk or easily jog during resting periods (long and shorts).
