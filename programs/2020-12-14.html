AMRAP 4 Minutes of

3.6.9..etc reps of

Toes to bar

Power cleans 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Rest 3 minutes between sets


Notes

This session consists of 2 sets with 3 minutes rest between them, each set is 2 AMRAPs with 1 Minute rest between them. Try to achieve high performance in each set. Go the exercises of the first, low-rep rounds unbroken with smooth transitions between them (unbroken gymnastics, touch and go unbroken lifts). As reps of each round increase start breaking each exercise into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between. On the thrusters prefer to go unbroken for as long as you can before you drop, on the cleans dont hesitate to go with single lifts if you realise that touch and go is no more efficient or forces you to take big rest.

Options

Chest to bar pull-ups → pull-ups

*Go with pull-ups if you can’t perform at least 10 unbroken reps chest to bar pull-ups while fresh.

Gymnastics

For form

10 rounds of

9 strict handstand push-ups

1 leg-less rope climb

 

Notes

Focus on quality of movements and full range of motion. Move steadily through the 10 rounds. Go the strict handstand push-ups unbroken for as long as proper form allows. On the rope climbs work on technical ascents with coordinated arm-leg motion and smooth descents (sliding down or extending and jumping down).

Options

Strict handstand push-ups → pike push-ups (elevated feet)

Leg-less → regular rope climbs

Engine

3 sets of

AMRAP 12 Minutes of

Row, 1500/1250 meters

40 bar-facing burpees over

Max calories, Assault bike

Rest 3 minutes  between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Focus on consistency, maintain a steady pace on the rower, 1:52.0/500m for men | 2:02.0/500m for women, and a fast, steady tempo on the bar-facing burpees over. Once you complete the couplet go straight to the max-cal set, move steadily at a sustainable pace that will allow you keep pushing efficiently until the last second. The more the remaining time the steadier your pace, the less the remaining time, the faster your pace.

Options

Assault bike → row
