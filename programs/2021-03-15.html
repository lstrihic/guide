Chest to bar pull-ups

Handstand walk, 7,5 meters

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and technique. Go the chest to bar pull ups and handstand walk unbroken. Perform smooth transitions between the two exercises that will allow you to move efficiently throughout. On the handstand walk move fast with quick, solid steps and a proper handstand position (straight body, toes pointing upwards.
