Warm up

2 sets of

5 shoulder presses + 4 push presses + 3 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2-3 jerks from blocks until 60%.


Goal

Improve technique and overhead stability. Prefer to go with single lifts of split jerks. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.


Movements

Focus on an explosive push, a fast drive under the barbell and a deep solid receiving position. Receive and drop the barbell on the blocks.

Strongman

5 sets of

60 seconds, Sandbag bear hug carry 70/50kg – 155/115lb.

Rest 60 seconds between sets


Warm up

Build up with sets of 2-3 sandbag deadlifts until you reach 70kg.


Goal

Improve stamina and consistency.


Movements

Establish a comfortable hug position that will allow you to breath. Move constantly with the sandbag by 10 meter back and forth distances for as long as you can in each set.
