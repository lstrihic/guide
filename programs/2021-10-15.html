Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat

3×1+3 @ 70% of Max Snatch Balance

3×1+2 @ 80%

3×1+1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

4 back push presses + 3 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power snatches + 2 overhead squats until 70%.

Goal

Improve confidence and stability. The lifts at 70% are 3 times the consecutive lifts of 1 snatch balance + 3 overhead squats. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a fast drive under the barbell, a solid deep receiving position (back straight, shoulders tight, elbows locked out) and slow controlled overhead squats.

Goal & Movements

Ideally go the chest to bar pull-ups and the wall-balls unbroken and as reps decrease gain confidence and try to go unbroken even harder. Alternatively, break the high rep rounds into a big and no more than 2-3 smaller sets with short to minimum rest between. As reps of double-unders increase, don’t hesitate to break into big manageable sets that will allow you to push through the total volume efficiently.

Options

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

7 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

14 d-ball or sand-bag ground to shoulders 70/50kg – 155/80lb.

5 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

10 d-ball or sand-bag ground to shoulders 70/50kg – 155/80lb.

3 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

6 d-ball or sand-bag ground to shoulders 70/50kg – 155/80lb.

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

7 rope pull-ups

3 d-ball or sand-bag deadlifts

5 d-ball or sand-bag squats (object on shoulders)

Goal

Focus on proper form and d-ball/sandbag mechanics.

Movements

Move fast on the rope, use your legs, kicks or kipping, to assist your upper body when ascending. Descend smoothly by sliding down, extending your body and falling on the ground. On the d-ball or sand-bag ground to shoulders, focus on mechanics, slightly rest the object over your knees before you bring it on your shoulders. Try to maintain a steady sustainable tempo throughout the session.

Options

Leg-less —> regular rope climbs

D-ball cleans —> barbell power cleans 70/52kg – 155/80lb.

Engine – Rowing

2 sets of

Row, 3750 meters @ 18-20 strokes/minute

Rest 2 minutes and 30 seconds

Pace: 2km PR pace + 18 seconds

 

Warm up

6 minutes @ easy pace | free strokes

Goal

Complete two medium distances at the given pace and slow steady strokes. Be technical, this is a low stroke rate that requires powerful pulls but will allow you to maintain form and breathe well throughout.

Cool down

4 minutes @ very light pressure
