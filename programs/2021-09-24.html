Warm up

50 calories, Assault bike

Then,

20 wall-balls

15 toes to bar

10 front squats

Goal & Movements

Maintain a fast pace on the Assault bike on both portions, move explosively and push quickly through the cals. On the couplets as reps decrease with each round push harder. The first couplet is more lighter and more manageable than the second one and will allow you to pace. If needed, break the high-rep rounds of toes to bar into manageable sets and try to go the wall-balls unbroken. In the second couplet, ideally go both exercises unbroken with smooth transitions between, alternatively break the high-rep rounds of muscle-ups into a big and a smaller set.

61/43kg – 135/95lb —> 52/34kg – 115/75lb.

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

5 rounds of

6 devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

10 meters, Double dumbbell front rack lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

2 pegboard ascents

10 meters, Handstand walk

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

10 double dumbbell deadlifts 15/10kg – 35/22,5lb.

5 burpees

10 alternating front rack lunges 15/10kg – 35/22,5lb.

5 pegboard pull-ups

10 double dumbbell snatches 15/10kg – 35/22,5lb.

20 shoulder taps

Goal

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Perform smooth transitions between exercises that will allow you to focus on quality of movements.

Movements

Go the devils presses at a steady manageable tempo throughout the session. Move fast on the pegboard, push your feet against the board to create stability, ascend and descend with swifts arm movements. The less you stay on the board the better. Go the handstand walk and the front rack lunges unbroken. Focus on a proper handstand position (straight body, shoulders tight, feet pointing upwards) and a solid front rack position.

Options

Pegboard ascents —> leg-less rope climbs 4,6m -15ft.

Handstand walk —> 3 wall-walks
