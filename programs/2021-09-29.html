Warm up

3 sets of

1 snatch pull + 2 hang power snatches + 2 hang squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

1 snatch pull + 2” pause below knees + 2 low hang squat snatches until 50%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and stability.

Movements

Start with a controlled pull until the barbell is below the knees, then maintain a solid position for 2 full seconds with the barbell 10cm below the knees and continue with a technical pull and a fast drive under the barbell. Start each rep from the ground including the pull.

Warm up

Sets of 4-5 reps until you reach 65%.

Goal

Improve form. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets. On the high-rep sets make sure you breathe well and keep a steady tempo, on the increasing percentages sets go for a solid successfully made single lift.

Movements

Focus on a straight back, a solid front rack position and slow controlled squats.

Gymnastics Stamina

For form

5 pegboard ascents

75 handstand push-ups

5 pegboard ascents

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

6 pegboard pull-ups

18 shoulder taps

15 seconds, Pegboard climb position hold

12 hand release push-ups

Goal & Movements

Focus on proper form and technique. Move fast and technically on the board, you need to rotate your grip in order to safely insert the stick into the hole and push your legs against the board as a counter power to ensure balance. Break the handstand push-ups into a big set, 15+ reps, and some smaller manageable sets that will allow you to push smoothly through the total volume.

Options

Peg-board ascents —> 15 peg-board pull-ups
