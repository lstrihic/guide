Barbell Cycling

30 clean jerks 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

30 bar-facing burpees over

30 snatches 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

 

Notes

Move fast with the barbell. Go the lifts with fast touch & go unbroken sets of 5-10 reps depending on your comfort. Focus on proper touch and go technique and smooth drive of the barbell to the ground. If you realise that your technique starts to break down or you rest too much during sets switch to drop & go lifts with minimum rest between. Maintain a steady sustainable pace on the bar-facing burpees over.

Engine – Running

A. 15 Minutes @ easy pace

 

B. 8 sets of

60 seconds, Incline sprint @ 80%

Walk back

 

Notes

Start with a 15-minute easy pace run and continue with some sprinting drills. Adjust a treadmill or find a route with incline (5-10%). Work at 80% of Max effort, you will find that the incline will increase the difficulty on your leg work and breathing, changing your common running pace (expect your legs to feel really heavy!). Walk back between sprints and finish the session with a 800 meters jog.
