Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 3 front squats +  2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat clean & jerks until 60%.

Goal

Improve technique and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Prefer to go with split jerks and work on stability.

Then

12.9.6.3 reps of

Ring muscle-ups

Thrusters 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

 

Warm up

3 rounds of

Run 200 meters

3 ring muscle-ups

6 thrusters 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Goal

Run at a steady sustainable pace that will allow you to breath and perform well on the couplet of ring muscle-ups & thrusters.

Movements

Ideally go both exercises of the couplet unbroken with smooth transitions between them, alternatively break the high-rep rounds into a big and a smaller set and push the low-rep rounds for unbroken. In general, it is more efficient to break the ring muscle-ups and try hard for unbroken thrusters.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

61/43kg – 135/95lb. —> 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

Warm up

3 sets of

5 shoulder presses + 5 push presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Max Effort

For time

42 calories, Bike-erg

12 meters, Overhead lunges 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

Cap: 5 minutes

 

Warm up

3-4 sets of

8 calories, Bike-erg @ fast pace

5 meters, Overhead lunges

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal & Movements

Give it all you got from the start, go explosively on the bike to create momentum quickly and try to maintain it for as long as possible, then take a breath and go for an unbroken distance of overhead lunges. The overhead lunges is the psychological challenge, because your cardio and legs will be surely taxed from the bike you have to stay focused and confident, find a solid overhead position and move steadily through the distance at a steady tempo.

Options

70/52kg – 155/115lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Engine – Running

3 sets of

4×300 meters @ 59-68”

Rest 60 seconds between portions

Rest 5 minutes between sets

 

Warm up

15 minutes, Run @ easy pace

Then,

2×10 meters, Front swings, side cross swings, front leg raise toe taps,

3×50 meters, Sprint

Goal

Try to be consistent throughout the session. Make sure you achieve the given time-frame yet avoid pushing too hard and giving it all in the first set. All portions in each set should be almost equal and each set should be at the same or higher effort and performance than the previous one.

Cool down

5 minutes, Run @ very easy pace
