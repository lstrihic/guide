Warm up

2 sets of

5 overhead presses + 4 push presses + 3 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 push presses + 2 jerks until 70%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness. Rest no less than 60 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive push press, then drive the barbell smoothly back to the shoulders and continue with a technical jerk.

—

 

Jerk

5 Reps Max

 

Warm up

Continue with sets of 3 reps < 85% and a few sets of 5 reps > 85%. Increase weight with until you reach the heaviest load with which you can successfully complete 5 unbroken reps.

Goal

Improve stability and confidence. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on explosive pushes and smooth rebounding on the shoulders. Ideally go the lighter weights with unbroken push jerks with touch & go technique and switch to solid split jerks once you reach near the max.

Goal & Movements

This session consists of two consecutive sequences of exercises divided by a farmers carry distance. Maintain a steady pace on the first row that will allow you to breathe and perform well on the exercises that follow. Prefer to break all the exercises of the 1st part (before farmers carry) into a big and some smaller manageable sets with short to minimum rest between. Then go the farmers walk distance by 20 or 10-meter unbroken distances depending on the available space, ideally go unbroken for as long as possible. In the 2nd part go the exercises unbroken for as long as you can, break only 1-2 reps before your feel like reaching muscle failure and push the rower really hard.

The goal in the main session is to efficiently increase your strokes and pace with each part. In part A keep steady 20 strokes and try to achieve a 2km PR pace +16 seconds, in part B keep 22 strokes and 2km PR pace +12 seconds and in part C keep 24 strokes and 2km PR pace +8 seconds.
