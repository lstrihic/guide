Overhead Squat

2×5 @ 60%

1×3 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

5×2 @ 85%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 back presses + 3 back push presses + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 overhead squats until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive back push press, once you have established a solid overhead position continue with slow controlled overhead squats.

—

 

Hang Power Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 80%

5×1 @ 87%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 half squat snatch balances @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power snatches until 60%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

First deadlift the barbell to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to the hip crease and avoid spending much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position. Reposition swiftly and then continue to the next rep.

Max Effort

3 sets of

For time

3 clean & jerks

6 bar facing burpees over

3 squat cleans

5 meters, Sprint to finish line

Cap: 90 seconds

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Warm up

Build up to 102/65kg – 225/145lb. of power clean and jerk + squat clean

Then,

2-3 sets of

1 clean & jerks 84/57kg – 185/125lb.

3 burpees over bar

1 squat clean

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal & Movements

Work on competition routines. Make sure you start fast, push hard and sprint to the finish line jumping over the barbell to get there. Ideally, go the clean & jerks with power cleans and touch & go power jerks, alternatively go with squat cleans and split jerks. Choose your technique as long as you ensure successfully made reps. On the squat cleans prefer to go with fast single lifts. Increase the weight on the barbell as prescribed below.

1st set: 84/57kg – 185/125lb.

2nd set: 93/61kg – 205/135lb

3rd set: 102/65kg – 225/145lb.
