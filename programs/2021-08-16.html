Warm up

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%.

Goal

Improve pulling power and stability. Focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

The lightweight high-rep sets allow you to work on proper angles and quality.

Warm up

2 rounds of

30 double-unders

7 overhead squats 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Goal

The challenge is the overhead squats. Weight increases and reps decrease but effort remains the same, make sure you rest shortly between sets.

Movements

Ideally go the double-unders unbroken and the overhead squats unbroken for as long as possible. Find and establish a solid overhead position and then move steadily at a solid tempo for as long as you can, break 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching failure, take short to minimum rest and continue. Alternatively break both exercises into big manageable sets with short rest between.

Back Squat

2×5 @ 60%

1×5 @ 70%

1×3 @ 75%

1×3 @ 80%

3×3 @ 85%

1×10 @ 70%

 

Warm up

Build up with sets of 3-4 reps until you reach 60%. Improve strength and form (front rack position).

Goal

The high-rep set allows you to better work on your legs by activating your slow twitch muscle fibres. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets.

Gymnastics

For form

6 sets of

2 strict ring muscle-ups

3 strict ring dips

2 kipping ring muscle-ups

3 kipping ring dips

Rest as needed between sets

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

10 shoulder taps

8 push-ups

6 swings on the rings

4 ring pull-ups

Goal

Each set should be performed as a complex. The better you switch and adapt to the technique and speed needed for the kipping reps the better chance you have to complete the complex. Rest as needed between sets in order to complete each one efficiently.

Movements

Complete the strict ring muscle ups and strict ring dips unbroken with focus on proper form and full range of motion, immediately continue with the kipping ring muscle-ups and kipping ring dips with focus on technical and fast repetitions (good kipping technique and swings). Your most important challenge is the transition from strict to kipping reps.

Options

1 strict ring muscle-ups → assisted strict ring muscle-ups (partner or coach)

2 strict ring dips

1 kipping ring muscle-ups → kipping chest to rings ull-ups

2 kipping ring dips

Engine

A. AMRAP 10 Minutes of

63 burpees over rower

Max distance, Row (30+ stokes)

 

Rest 3 minutes

B. AMRAP 10 Minutes of

45 burpees over rower

Max distance, Row (26-28 strokes)

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

C. AMRAP 10 Minutes of

27 burpees over rower

Max distance, Row (22-24 strokes)

 

Goal

This session consists of 3 parts. The goal is to achieve a steady pace on the rower with focus on rate of strokes. Try to be strict on that and breath well throughout.

Movements

Try to achieve high performance in each one. On the burpees maintain a fast, sustainable pace, as reps decrease with each set try to increase your pace (effort) accordingly. Don’t push too hard from the start considering the extra difficulty of the jump over rower.
