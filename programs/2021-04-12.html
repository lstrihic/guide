Notes

This workout consists of 3 portions. The common exercise is the overhead squats and the gymnastic exercises increase in difficulty with each portion. The goal is to be efficient on the overhead squats and go with fast and technical reps on the gymnastics. Ideally go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between. Alternatively try to go the overhead squats unbroken for as long as possible and break the gymnastic exercise into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between.

Front Squat

2×5 @ 60%

1×5 @ 70%

1×3 @ 75%

1×3 @ 80%

3×3 @ 85%

1×10 @ 70%

 

Notes

Build up with sets of 4-5 reps until you reach 60%. Focus on proper form (front rack, full grip on the barbell) and a solid bottom position (no bounce). Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets. Make sure you don’t skip the high-rep set, go this at a steady tempo.

—

 

Gymnastics

5 sets of

AMRAP 2 Minutes of

12 strict handstand push-ups

Max distance, Handstand walk

Rest 2 minutes between

 

Notes

The goal is to move efficiently on the handstand walk throughout the whole session. Go the strict handstand push-ups fast and unbroken, if not, break into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between, then catch a breath and go for 5 or 10-meter unbroken distances depending on your comfort. Move fast on your hands and try to take short equal rest between distances.

Options

Strict handstand push-ups → pike push-ups elevated feet

Handstand walk → wall-walks

*Go with wall-walks if you can’t perform unbroken at least 5 meters of hs walk.

Notes

This session consists of 3 portions. Try to achieve maximum performance in each one. Push through the couplet fast and efficiently, as calories decrease in each portion increase your effort and pace while maintaining a steady tempo on the bar-facing burpees over throughout. On the max cals go at a solid, steady tempo and progressively increase as you feel like your breathing is improving. The more remaining time the steadier the pace, the less remaining time the faster the pace.
