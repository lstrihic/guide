For time

3 rounds of

Row, 500 meters

15 burpee box-jumps over 61/50cm – 24/20”

15 deadlifts 143/91kg – 315/200lb

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

Go the row and the burpee box-jumps over at a fast yet sustainable pace throughout the session. Prefer to break the deadlifts of the first 2 rounds into a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with minimum rest between, go the last round unbroken for as long as possible. The more consistent you are on the row and burpee box-jumps over the better your performance will be.

Max Effort

For time

21 calories, Ski-erg

15 bar-facing burpees over

9 squat snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

 

Notes

Go hard from the start. Push explosively through the cals on the ski, utilise your legs and use your weight to create momentum. Then go straight to the bar facing burpees and maintain a fast sustainable tempo. Briefly stop and catch a breath before you start the lifts. Prefer to start with single lifts with minimum rest between and, if you feel confident enough, finish with a touch & go set, if not, just try to be consistent on the single lifts.

Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb. —> 52/37kg – 115/80lb.
