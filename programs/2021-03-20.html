Squat Snatch

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

3×1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 70%. Improve technique and explosiveness. Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the bar and a solid deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

50 GHD sit-ups

40 wall-ball shot 9/6kg – 20/14lb

30 power cleans 61/43kg – 135/95lb

20 ring muscle-ups

30 power cleans 61/43kg – 135/95lb

40 wall-balls shots 9/6kg – 20/14lb

50 GHD sit-ups

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Notes

This workout has a decreasing volume of GHD sit-ups, wall-balls and power cleans which increases once again after the muscle-ups, so keep that in mind and pace until you finish the muscle-ups, then push harder to a near max effort pace. Ideally go the first GHD sit-ups and wall-ball shots unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. On the power cleans prefer to go with single lifts at a steady, fast tempo on both before and after-muscleup lifts. Go the muscle-ups ideally with a big and a smaller set with short rest between, alternatively break into manageable sets (about 5-7 reps) that will allow you to move efficiently. Go the last GHD sit-ups and wall-ball shots unbroken for as long as possible, break only 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching failure.

Options

GHD sit-ups → weighted sit-ups 15/10kg dumbbell to chest

61/43kg – 135/95lb → 52/37kg – 155/80lb

Ring muscle-ups → chest to rings pull-ups

Notes

Max Effort

For time

30 toes to bar

Row, 30/23 calories

10 sand-bag cleans over shoulders 70/50kg – 155/110lb

Cap: 4 minutes

 

Notes

This is a sprint, move fast from the start and try to push explosively through the exercises. Go the toes to bar unbroken for as long as possible, break 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching failure. On the rower try to maintain a pace 1400+ for men / 1150+ for women. The sandbag cleans is the most difficult part, you will be already gasping for air and you have to establish a steady, fast tempo. Perform fast transitions between exercises and make sure you take minimum rest between sandbag cleans.

Options

Sand-bag cleans → barbell power cleans 82/55kg – 180/120lb
