Push Press

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

1×2 @ 85%

3×2 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

8 strict presses + 5 push presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Improve explosiveness and overhead stability. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets and try to go with touch and go reps on the shoulders.

Movements

Use your legs and hips to create momentum and assist your shoulders send the barbell overhead.

—

 

Hang Power Clean

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 80%

5×1 @ 87%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 3 half squat cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power cleans until 60%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

First deadlift the barbell up to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to the hip crease and avoid spending much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position. Reposition swiftly and then continue to the next rep.

Cap: 25 minutes

 

Warm up

600 meters, Ski-erg @ easy pace

Then,

Build up to 61/43kg – 135/95lb. of hang power clean

Then,

8.9.10 reps of

Lunges

Thrusters 20/10kg – 45/25lb.plate

Rope pull-ups

Goal

This session consists of two consecutive sequences of exercises divided by 10 rope climbs. The goal is to pace the part before the rope climbs and then push to maintain the same or faster pace in the next part (after rope climbs).

Movements

Go the front rack lunges unbroken (5-meter unbroken mandatory section) and break the hang power cleans into a big and 1-2 smaller sets with short rest between, the fewer times you drop the barbell on the hang the better. Push fast through the ski and break the wall-balls into a big and a smaller set. In the second portion (after rope climbs) try to go unbroken or achieve the same sets and transitions as in the first part. On the rope climbs move fast finding solid footing and squat on the rope to assist your upper body. Descend smoothly by sliding down, extending your body and jumping on the ground.
