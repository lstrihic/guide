Squat Clean + Jerk

2×2 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

2×2 @ 80%

1×1+2 @ 90%

2×1+2 @ 95%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2 reps until you reach 60%. Improve stability and confidence. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell and solid receiving positions on both clean and jerk movements. The lifts at 80% is 2 sets of 2 squat clean jerks, at 90% is 1 set of 1 squat clean and 2 jerks. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Clean Pull

2×2 @ 105%

 

Notes

Start the clean pull from where you left the squat clean + jerk. Work on pulling strength. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

Row, 800 meters

Then,

12 rounds of

1 rope climb 4,6m – 15ft.

8 handstand push-ups

12 squats

*Vest 9/7kg – 20/14lb.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Notes

Row 800 meters at a fast explosive pace and go straight to the rope climb. The goal is to be technical on the rope and perform the reps as comfortably as possible and equally important perform fast transitions between exercises. Go the exercises unbroken with focus on breathing well, shorten your transitions as you reach the end. Work with the vest, make sure you adjust it tight to your body so it doesn’t move around excessively.

Barbell Cycling

100 double-unders

10 snatches @ 65%

100 double-unders

10 snatches @ 70%

100 double-unders

10 snatches @ 75%

100 double-unders

10 snatches @ 80%

 

Notes

The goal is to move efficiently with the barbell under metabolic fatigue. Go the double-unders unbroken, if you break take a breath and continue immediately with the remaining reps. Go the snatches either power or squat. It is recommended that you start the low percentages with single reps of power snatches at a steady tempo (minimum rest between lifts) and as the load gets heavier switch to squat snatches by maintaining your tempo as equally similar to that of the power snatches as you can. Load the barbell on your own and make sure you practice fast transitions.

B. 10 sets of

2 minutes @ 85% of Vo2Max

Rest 1 minute between sets

Notes

Warm up by running 15 minutes prior the main session and cool down by running 10 minutes afterwards at a comfortable pace of your choice. Be as consistent as you can throughout the session, try not to force your pace, instead make sure you maintain it around 85% of Vo2Max in all 10 sets.
