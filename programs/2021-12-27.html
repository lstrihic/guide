Power Snatch

2×3 @ 70%

2×2 @ 80%

1×2 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

1×5 @ 75%


Warm up

3 sets of

4 snatch pulls + 3 hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2-3 power snatches until 70%.


Goal

Improve confidence and stability. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.


Movements

Focus on a powerful pull and a steady receiving position. Keep the barbell close to your body’s vertical line. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees, shoulders tight and elbows locked out.

Snatch High Pull

2×3 @ 90%  of Max Power Snatch

2×2 @ 95%

2×2 @ 100%


Warm up

Continue from the percentage that you left the power snatch.

Goal

Improve technique and weightlifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell as high as possible. Do not force a pull with your arms yet make sure you drive the barbell as high as you can, close to your shoulders and your body’s vertical line.

Then,

3 sets of

7 kettlebell goblet squat

8 down ups

9 power cleans 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in both parts. Move steadily for the biggest part of each AMRAP, 4+ minutes, then push harder to go for a strong finish.


Movements

Go the bar-facing burpees over at a steady sustainable pace throughout the session. Perform the power cleans of the low-rep rounds unbroken with touch and go technique. As reps increase with each round break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between yet make sure you keep the transitions even shorter.
