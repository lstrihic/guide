Hang Power Clean

2×2 @ 60% of Max Hang Power Clean

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

2×2 @ 75%

2×2 @ 80%

Warm up

3 sets of

2 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power cleans until 60%.


Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

First pull the barbell to hip height, then drive the barbell to a position over the knees and pull explosively with the barbel close to your body into a solid receiving position. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to the hip crease and avoid spending too much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position. In each set reposition swiftly and continue to the second rep.

4×1 @ 90%


Warm up

2 sets of

1 hang squat clean + 2 front squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang squat cleans until 50%.


Goal

Improve explosiveness and confidence. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.


Movements

Focus on a solid pull until the barbell is at hip height, then drive the barbell to a position over the knees, pull explosively with the barbel close to your body (proper lifting angles) and focus on a fast drive into a deep receiving position.

Goal

First complete 5 rounds of the couplet, in the remaining time go for as many reps as you can of ring muscle-ups.


Movements

Go the wall-balls unbroken and maintain a fast pace on the rower 1350+ for men | 1050+ for women. Once you finish the couplet take a short breath and go for a big set of ring muscle-ups, then continue with smaller sets with short rest between.

Warm up

Goal & Movements

Go the double-unders unbroken, then take a breath before the shoulder to overhead and try to go unbroken for as long as you can. Break 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching failure.
