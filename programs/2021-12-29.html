Goal

C. For time

5 rounds of

3 devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

3 ring muscle-ups

Cap: 7 minutes


Warm up

30 calories, Assault bike @ easy pace

Then,

2-3 sets of

10 hand release push-ups

9 ground to overhead 20/10kg – 45/25lb. plate

10 down-ups

9 thrusters 20/10kg – 45/25lb. plate

10 sit-ups

Goal & Movements

Try to achieve high performance in each part. Move steadily through the rounds, ideally go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between that will allow you to keep moving efficiently. Alternatively, break the challenging exercises into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. As volume decreases with each part make sure you shorten your transitions.


Options

Strict —> regular handstand push-ups

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

*Scale down if you cant perform at least one set of strict handstand push-ups or ring muscle ups unbroken while fresh.

Engine

AMRAP 2 Minutes of

Row, 20/16 calories

Max reps, Lateral burpees over rower

Rest 1 minute between sets


Goal & Movements

Try to achieve high performance in each set, dont go all in from the first set yet try to be consistent throughout. Push fast and explosively through the calories, then go straight to the lateral burpees over rower and try to regulate your breathing. Move at a tempo that you estimate you can sustain for the whole remaining time.
