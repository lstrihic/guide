Gymnastics Conditioning

AMRAP 8 Minutes of

1 pegboard ascent

2.4.6..etc reps of

Strict handstand push-ups

Box jump-overs 76/61cm – 30/24”

 

Warm up

2.4.6 reps of

Pegboard pull-ups

Box step-ups 61/50cm – 24/20”

6.8.10 reps of

Hand release push-ups

Goal & Movements

Move at a steady tempo through the biggest part of the session, 5+ minutes, then start pushing harder to finish strong. Move fast on the board, push your feet against the wall to establish balance and ascend-descend with swift arm movements. The less you stay on the board, the more your preserve your upper body for the next reps. On the strict handstand push-ups go the first rounds unbroken and then start breaking into a big and some smaller manageable sets with short rest between.

Options

Peg-board ascent —> legless rope climb —> regular rope climb

Engine

4 sets of

AMRAP 6 Minutes of

Buy in:

40/30 calories, Assault bike

Then

10.20.30..etc reps of

Double-unders

5.10.15..etc reps of

Burpees to target 15cm – 6”

Rest 2 minutes between sets

 

Goal & Movements

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Start each set with 40 calories for men /30 calories for women on the assault bike and continue with as many reps as you can of the couplet in the remaining time of the 6 minutes. Maintain a steady pace on the assault bike throughout the session, 65+ for men / 58+ for women RPM. Go the double-unders unbroken and maintain a sustainable pace on the burpees that will allow you to hit the target smoothly and efficiently every time.
