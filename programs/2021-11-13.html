Jerk

2×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 65%

1×1 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

4 presses + 3 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 jerks until 60%.

Goal

Use blocks or racks to receive and drop the barbell after each lift in order to explicitly focus on technique and explosiveness. Prefer to work on the technique of split jerk. Rest no less than 60 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive push and a fast drive under the barbell. Work on the position of the feet on the split jerk and focus on a deep receiving position.

Hang Power Clean + Hang Squat Clean

2×1+3 @ 60% of Hang Power Clean

1×1+3 @ 70%

1×1+2 @ 75%

1×1+2 @ 80%

5×1+1 @ 85%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

2 clean pulls + 2 hang power cleans + 2 front squats + 2 hang squat cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power cleans + 2 hang squat cleans until 60%.

Goal

Work on consistency and confidence on the hang position. The lifts at 60% are 2 times 1 hang power clean + 3 hang squat cleans, at 80% are 1 hang power clean + 2 hang squat cleans. Rest no less than 60 seconds between sets.

Movements

First pull the barbell to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Make sure you drive the barbell smoothly and under control back to the hip crease and avoid spending too much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position. In each set reposition swiftly and continue to the next rep.

Strongman

EMOM 12 Minutes of

1st: Max distance, Farmers walk 2×70/50kg – 155/115lb. handles

2nd: Rest

3rd: Max distance, Sand-bag bear hug carry 90/60kg – 200/130lb.

4th: Rest

 

Warm up

2-3 rounds of

10 meters, Farmerswalk

Rest 20 seconds

10 meters, Sand-bag bear hug carry

Rest 20 seconds

*progressively increase weight with each round

Goal

Work by 20 or 10-meter distances depending on the available space. Move constantly in each 1st and 3rd minute (1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11 …etc) covering as much distance as you can and rest in each 2nd and 4th minute (2,4,6,8,10 …etc).

Movements

Progressively increase weight on the handles and carry heavier sandbags as you work in 10-meter distances. Once you reach the given load, start the EMOM carrying the weights for the marked distances (20 or 10 meters) back and forth.

For time

8 bar-facing burpees over

4 power snatches 83/57kg – 185/125lb

4 bar-facing burpees over

2 squat snatches 83/57kg – 185/125lb

Cap: 90 seconds

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Warm up

Build up to 83/57kg – 185/125lb of 2 power snatches + 1 squat snatch

Then,

2-3 sets of

6 down ups

3 power snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

6 jumping squats

1 squat snatch 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal & Movements

Start each set 5 meters away from the barbell, run to the barbell to perform the lifts and burpees and once you complete the set run another 5 meters to a designated finish line. Move fast in each set, push fast and explosively through the bar facing burpees. On the lifts ideally go unbroken with touch and go reps, alternatively go the power snatches unbroken and the squat snatches with 2 solid singe lifts.
