Notes

Conditioning

For time

100 double-unders

21 overhead squats 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

12 bar-facing burpees over

100 double-unders

15 overhead squats 52/37kg – 15/80lb.

12 bar-facing burpees over

100 double-unders

9 overhead squats 52/37kg – 15/80lb.

12 bar-facing burpees over

Cap: 11 minutes

 

Notes

The challenge of the workout is the overhead squats. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can, if you break immediately continue with the remaining reps. On the overhead squats try to go each round unbroken with a solid overhead position and steady tempo, if you manage to do that you will save a lot of time and avoid extra taxing. If not possible, break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. Go the first two rounds of burpees at a fast sustainable tempo and go the last round as fast as you can.

Options

52/37kg – 115/80lb. —> 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

Back Squat

2×2 @ 70% + 8 box-jumps

2×2 @ 75% + 8 box-jumps

2×2 @ 80% + 8 box-jumps

2×2 @ 85% + 8 box-jumps

 

Notes

At each percentage complete 2 sets. Each set is 2 back squats + 8 box-jumps. Ideally, go the box jumps with touch & go technique. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Gymnastics

5 sets of

AMRAP 2 Minutes of

30 GHD sit-ups

Max reps, Ring muscle-ups

Rest 2 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve maximum performance in each set. Go the GHD sit-ups unbroken then take a breath and go for a big and some smaller sets of muscle-ups. The focus is on consistency, this is not a max set of muscle-ups it is an AMRAP so make sure you never reach failure and break into sets that will allow you to keep moving and keep doing reps without having to rest too much between sets.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to rings pull-ups

*Go with chest to rings pull-ups if you can’t perform at least 3 unbroken reps of ring muscle-ups

Engine

3 sets of

Bike-erg, 1000 meters

20 bar-facing burpees over

Bike-erg, 1000 meters

20 bar-facing burpees over

Bike-erg, 1000 meters

Rest 3 minutes between

 

Notes

Try to achieve maximum performance in each set. Increase your pace from distance to distance within each set and maintain a steady tempo on the bar-facing burpees over throughout the session. Try to have equal or better time in each next set.

Options
