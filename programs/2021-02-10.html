Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 50%. Improve explosiveness and stability. Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

11 chest to bar pull-ups

11 overhead squats 43/30kg – 95/65lb

Then,

3 rounds of

13 chest to bar pull-ups

9 overhead squats 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Then,

3 rounds of

15 chest to bar pull-ups

7 overhead squats 83/55kg – 180/120lb

 

Notes

The workout consists of 3 consecutive portions, in each portion chest to bar reps increase while overhead squat reps decrease yet your effort should remain the same. Ideally, go all the chest to bar pull-ups unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. The goal is to move efficiently on the overhead squats. Find that solid overhead position and go unbroken for as long as proper form allows. If you have to break an exercise prefer to break the chest to bar pull-ups and aim at unbroken overhead squats. Transitions will play a major role on your performance, go fast from the barbell to the pull-up bar, go smoothly from the pull-up bar to the barbell.

Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb → 52/37kg – 115/80lb

83/55kg – 180/120lb → 70/50kg – 155/70lb

Gymnastics

For form

18 deficit handstand push-ups 10/5cm – 4/2”

12 bar muscle-ups

40 meters, Handstand walk

12 bar muscle-ups

18 deficit handstand push-ups 10/5cm – 4/2”

 

Notes

Focus on full range of motion and quality of movements. Go the deficit handstand push-ups unbroken for as long as proper form allows, stop 2-3 reps before you feel like reaching muscle failure. If you are not comfortable with handstand prefer to break the first portion into manageable sets. On the bar muscle-ups ideally go unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. Break the handstand walk into unbroken 10-meter distances, move fast with quick steps and proper handstand form.

Options

18 → 16 → 14 reps* of

Deficit handstand push-ups → regular handstand push-ups

12 → 10 → 8 reps* of

Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups

40 → 30 → 20 meters of

Handstand walk → 5 wall-walks

*Choose an option that you can perform unbroken when fresh

Engine

4 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

Assault bike, 20/15 calories

100 double-unders

Max reps, Burpee box-jumps

Rest 1 minute

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

20 burpee box-jumps 60/50cm – 24/20”

100 double-unders

Max calories, Assault bike

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

This session consists of 4 sets with 3 minutes rest between them. Each set is two 3-minute AMRAPs with 1 minute rest between. Try to achieve high performance in each set, your performance between the AMRAPs of each round will drop yet your effort should remain the same. Complete the couplet of each AMRAP fast, push explosively through the Assault bike/Burpee box-jumps and then go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible, if you break immediately continue with the remaining reps. In the max-rep set maintain a fast, sustainable pace that will allow you to move efficiently for the the whole remaining time.

Option

Assault bike → row
