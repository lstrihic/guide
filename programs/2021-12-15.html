Power Snatch

Daily Max

Then

4×1 @ 90% of Daily Max


Warm up

3 sets of

2 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 4 half squat snatch balances @ 20/15kg barbell


Goal

Build up to a heavy lift for the day by progressively increasing weight with sets of 5 reps < 50%, 3 reps < 70%, 2 reps < 80% and a few single lifts until the max. Rest 75 seconds between sets at 90% and focus on solid lifts.


Movements

Focus on an explosive pull and a solid overhead receiving position. Do not be afraid to receive the barbell at a half squat position, keep you feet width narrow when receiving.

Gymnastics Conditioning

For time

5.10.15 reps of

Wall-walks

150 double-unders between

Cap: 15 minutes

Warm up

3 rounds of

10 meters, Bear crawl

15 hand release push-ups

30 double-unders


Goal & Movements

The wall-walk is a demanding exercise and volume in this session increases so go each rep smoothly and steadily with focus on full range of motion. The goal is to maintain the same tempo, same rest, between reps in every round until the finish. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can.
