Squat Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

5×2 @ 80%

2×5 @ 70%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight until you reach 60%, go with sets of 2-3 reps. Work on technique and explosiveness, Focus on a fast drive under the barbell, solid deep squat and overhead positions. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Squat Clean & Jerk

2×2+2 @ 50%

2×2+1 @ 60%

1×2+1 @ 65%

1×2+1 @ 70%

3×3+3 @ 75%

1×5+1 @ 65%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2 squat cleans + 2 jerks until you reach 50%. The lifts at 60% are 2 times of 2 squat cleans + 1 jerk, at 75% are 3 times of 3 squat cleans + 3 jerks. Work on technique and explosiveness. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell, a solid deep squat position and technical split or power jerks (preferable split). Go with single lifts of squat cleans, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Conditioning

For time

8 rounds of

4 ring muscle ups

10 meters, Front rack lunges 43/30kg – 95/65lb

8 box jump-overs 60/50cm – 24/20”

10 meters, Front rack lunges 43/30kg – 95/65lb

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

The goal is to move steadily and efficiently on the lunges. Go the ring muscle-ups unbroken and the box jump-overs at a fast steady pace, the biggest part of the workout is based on the front rack lunges. Go for unbroken 10-meter distances with focus on a solid front rack position. Perform smooth transitions between exercises that will allow you to keep moving steadily.

Options

Ring muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups

Gymnastics

For form

5 rounds of

10 strict pull-ups

20 push-ups

*Vest 9/6kg – 20/14lb

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Ideally go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between that will allow you to achieve that. Alternatively, go unbroken for as long as proper form allows, then take a breath and continue with the remaining reps. *Work with the extra weight and discomfort of the vest.

Options

Engine

8 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

Ski-erg, 300/250 meters

80 double-unders

Max reps, GHD sit-ups

Rest 1 minute between

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set, do not give all you got from the start, go for about equal scores throughout the session. Move fast through the couplet, be explosive on the ski and try to go the double-unders unbroken, if you break immediately continue with the remaining reps. On the GHD move at a steady tempo, go unbroken for as long as possible at a steady manageable tempo.

Options

GHD → toes to rings
