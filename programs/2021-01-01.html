Power Clean

Daily Max

Then

5 touch & go reps @ 85%

 

Notes

Reach a heavy lift for the day. Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with 3-4 reps < 70%, 2 reps < 80% and single reps > 80%. Then drop down to 85% and go for a touch & go set. It’s 1 set so make it worth, focus on touch & go technique and efficiency.

Conditioning

AMRAP 20 Minutes of

4 deadlifts 124/80kg – 275/175lb

8 handstand push-ups

32 double-unders

 

Notes

Focus on consistency. Try to maintain a steady pace for the biggest part of the workout (15+ minutes) and then increase your pace by shortening your transitions. Ideally, go each exercise unbroken, alternatively break the deadlifts into 2 sets or even go with single lifts if necessary. The transitions are very important, make sure you place your equipment close together and have a plan of how to move efficiently from exercise to exercise.

Options

124/80kg – 275/175lb → 102/70kg – 225/155lb

Engine

A1. Row 1000 meters @ 22-24 strokes. 

 

A2. Row 3x250m  @ Time Trial pace

Rest 90 seconds between

 

B. Time trial*

Row, 2000 meters

 

Notes

Start with 1000 meters row at light pressure and 22-24 strokes/minute and then perform 3 sets of 250 meters reaching the pace you intend to follow in the main session as warm up. The main session is a 2000-meter time trial. Give your best to achieve maximum performance. Row 5 minutes at a very light pressure as cool down.

*Note down your score, you will need it as a benchmark for later sessions during the season.
