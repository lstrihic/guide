Squat Clean and Jerk

3×1 @ 70%

2×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

1×1 @ 100%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight until you reach 70%. Improve maximal strength and confidence. There is a total of 10 sets, go with a set every minute in EMOM style. Complete the lift and then wait until the next minute starts to continue with the next lift. Focus on o fast drive under the barbell, a solid deep receiving position and an explosive technical jerk (ideally split).

Notes

In each odd minute continuously perform 10-meter distances back and forth carrying the handles. In every even minute rest, try to recover your breathing and loosen your grips.

Max Effort

For time

4 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

8 overhead squats 83/52kg – 180/115lb.

Cap: 3 minutes

 

Notes

Give everything you got from the start. Move fast on the rope with fast grips and coordinated leg movement (kicks or kipping), descend fast by sliding and jumping down. Lose no time between rope climbs, then take a breath and go for as many overhead squats as possible, if you break take minimum rest and continue. Ideally, If you are comfortable with barbells, attempt to squat snatch the barbell to the overhead. Though, it is recommended that you clean the barbell and place it on the back rack before you jerk it to the overhead position.

Options

83/52kg – 180/115lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Engine – Running

A. 2000 meters @ easy pace

 

B. 2 sets of

3000 meters @ 4’10”-5’05”/km

Rest 7 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Warm up by running 2000 meters prior to the main session and cool down running 400 meters afterwards at a comfortable pace of your choice. The goal is to achieve high performance in both sets, select a pace that will lead to a work intensity over 87% of Vo2Max.
