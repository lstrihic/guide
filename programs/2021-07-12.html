Warm up

3 sets of

5 snatch pulls + 5 hang power snatches + 3 overhead presses + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability at the bottom position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on an explosive  pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position.

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Warm up

Run 400 meters

Then,

3 rounds of

50 double-unders

1 rope climb

6 overhead squats 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

Goal

As volume decreases with each round make sure you put up more effort to sustain an equal or faster tempo.

Movements

Run at a fast yet steady pace throughout the session. On the rope climbs move fast and efficiently finding solid footings and squatting on the rope to assist your upper body, descend smoothly. Ideally go the overhead squats unbroken at a steady tempo with controlled squats, alternatively break the first round into a big and a smaller set with short rest between.

For form

1 strict ring muscle-up

*Vest 9/7kg – 20/14lb

 

Warm up

Shoulder mobility

Then,

2-3 sets

2 strict ring pull-ups + 1 strict ring muscle-up + 2 strict ring dips @ on the low rings

*no vest

Goal

Be strict and focus on proper form and full range of motion.

Movements

Ideally perform each round unbroken, alternatively, complete 1 strict ring muscle-up + 2 strict ring dips and then another 3 strict ring pull-ups in each round.

Options

Strict ring muscle-ups —> kipping ring muscle-ups

Strict ring dips —> kipping ring dips

Strict ring pull-ups —> kipping ring pull-ups

Vest —> without vest *if you cant perform at least one unbroken set with vest

Engine

10 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

Row, 500/450 meters

Max reps, Burpees over rower lateral

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Goal & Tempo

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Push fast and explosively through the distance on the rower, then go straight to the lateral burpees over rower. Pace the first reps as you try to recover your breathing then start pushing faster and increase to a fast yet sustainable pace for the whole 3-minute time window.
