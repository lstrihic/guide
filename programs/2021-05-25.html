High Hang Power Clean

2×5 @ 50% of Max Power Clean

2×3 @ 60%

1×1 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

4×1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 3-4 reps until you reach 50%. Improve explosiveness. Establish a solid high hang position (mid-height of quads) and go for an explosive pull with a high shoulder shrug. Don’t forget to drive the barbell under control to the high hang position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

—

 

Low Hang Clean Pulls

2×2 @ 85% of Max Power Clean

1×2 @ 90%

3×2 @ 95%

 

Notes

Go straight to the low hang clean pulls at the weight you completed the high hang power clean. Work on explosiveness and lifting angles. The low hang position is 10cm below knee height. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

Run, 400 meters

21 deadlifts 102/70kg – 225/155lb.

12 burpee box-jumps over 61/50cm – 24/20”

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

Run at a steady manageable pace throughout the workout that will allow you to move well on the exercises. Break the deadlifts of the 1st and 2nd round into a big and 1-2 smaller sets with short rest between and go the last round unbroken for as long as you can, if you break, take minimum rest and continue with the remaining reps. On the burpees maintain a steady sustainable pace on the first 2 rounds and push the last round as hard as you can.

Options

102/70kg – 225/155lb. —> 83/61kg – 185/135lb.

Barbell Cycling

10 rounds of

3 hang power cleans

3 front squats

3 clean and jerks

83/52kg – 180/115lb.

 

Notes

Work on barbell efficiency. Try to go the hang power cleans and front squats unbroken, then continue with single lifts of clean & jerks. Move steadily with the barbell and focus on maintaining a steady tempo and high performance through the 10 rounds.

Options

83/52kg – 180/115lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Gymnastics Conditioning

5 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

40 double-unders

15 toes to bar

40 double-unders

Max reps, Strict handstand push-ups

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Go the double-unders and the toes to bar unbroken with fast transitions between, then take a breath and go for a big yet manageable set of strict handstand push-ups, continue with smaller sets until the 3 minute cap.

Options

Strict handstand push-ups —> kipping handstand push-ups
