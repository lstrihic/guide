Strength/Gymnastics

AMRAP 14 Minutes of

15 meters, Double dumbbell shoulders rack lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

5 devils press

15 meters, Double dumbbell shoulders rack lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

10 strict ring dips

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and quality of movements. Go the double dumbbell shoulder rack lunges by 2×7,5 meters unbroken distances. Focus on a proper front rack position and solid lunges at a steady tempo. On the devils presses maintain a steady, sustainable tempo and go the ring dips unbroken. Perform smooth transitions that will allow you to keep moving efficiently.

Options

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb → 15/10kg – 35/25lb

Strict ring dips → ring push-ups

*Choose to to go with ring push-ups if you can’t perform 10 unbroken strict ring dips while fresh.

Engine

A. Row 6 minutes @ easy pace

B. 2 sets of

Row, 3750m @ 18-20 strokes/minute

Rest 2 minutes and 30 seconds

Pace: 2km PR pace + 18 seconds

 

Notes

Warm up rowing 6 minutes at light pressure. In the main session try to achieve maximum performance in each set. The goal is to maintain on both sets a  2km PR pace + 18 seconds. Select strokes within the given stroke rate in order to achieve that. Remember that the faster the strokes the more the cardiovascular taxing, the fewer the strokes the more powerful the pulls need to be. This is a low stroke/rate so you gotta need to pull hard. Cool down rowing 4 minutes at super light pressure.
