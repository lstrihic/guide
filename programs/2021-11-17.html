Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat

Build to a heavy combo of

1 Snatch Balance + 2 Overhead Squats

 

Warm up

2 sets of

4 back push presses + 3 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Build up progressively increasing weight on the barbell, to a heavy lift for the day of the combo 1 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a fast drive under the barbell, a solid deep receiving position (back straight, shoulders tight, elbows locked out) and slow controlled overhead squats.

Power Jerk + Split Jerk

Build to a heavy combo of

1 Power Jerk + 2 Split Jerks

 

Warm up

2 sets of

2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Build up progressively increasing weight on the barbell, to a heavy lift for the day of the combo 1 power jerk + 2 split jerks. Use racks to load and unload the barbell. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive push and solid deep receiving positions with proper footwork and positioning.

C. For time

4 rounds of

10 calories, Ski-erg

10 wall-balls 14/9kg – 30/20lb.

Warm up

3 rounds of

12 calories, Ski-erg

8 kipping ring dips

10 thrusters 10/5kg – 25/15lb. plate

Goal & Movements

This session consists of 3 portions, the volume remains the same yet each next portion is broken into sets which makes it easier for you to push harder. In npart A focus on pace, maintain a fast yet manageable pace on the ski that will allow you to move efficiently on the wall-balls and ring muscle-ups. Ideally, go the wall-balls unbroken and break the ring muscle-ups into a big and 1-2 smaller sets with minimum rest between. Alternatively, break the wall-balls into a big and a smaller set as well. In part B & C try to go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between that will allow you to keep moving efficiently.

Options

Goal & Movemtns

Move fast from the first second, start by going the toes to bar unbroken and as reps increase with each round prefer to break into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. Engine

10 sets of

30 heavy double-unders

20/16 calories, Assault bike

10 bar-facing burpees over

Rest 90 seconds between sets

 

Goal & Movements

Move steadily and efficiently through the total volume at a sustainable tempo. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as as possible, then go straight to the assault bike, start explosively in each round and try to maintain the momentum. On the bar-facing burpees over (use a barbell and 10kg – 25lb. plates) move at a steady tempo while you try to regulate your breathing. As you reach the finish increase your pace and shorten your transitions to go for a strong finish.

Options

Heavy —> 60 regular rope double-unders
