Hang Squat Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 80%

5×1 @ 85%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch pulls + 3 hang snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches  + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 hang squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and hang position efficiency. The hang position is with the barbell 10cm above the knees, make sure you are solid on the hang position and perform explosive and technical pulls. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a solid deep receiving position and smooth drive of the barbell to the hang position.

C. AMRAP 4 Minutes of

30 double-unders

5 power snatches 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

Warm up

30 double-unders

10 power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

15 pull-ups

30 double-unders

10 power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

10 chest to bar pull-ups

30 double-unders

5 power snatches 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

5 bar muscle-ups

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Move steadily for the biggest part of each AMRAP and start pushing your pace as you reach the end.

Movements

Go the double-unders unbroken, if you break take a breath and shortly continue with the remaining reps. Ideally go the snatches touch & go and unbroken, alternatively start with a big unbroken set and continue with fast single lifts if you find yourself losing form and efficiency. On the gymnastics, try to go unbroken with fast reps using butterfly technique for pull-ups and chest to bar pull-ups, and kipping technique for bar muscle-ups. In general, competition-wise, prefer to push the exercise you are good at, break and move comfortably on the exercise you struggle.

Gymnastics Conditioning

AMRAP 10 Minutes of

Row, 8 calories

10 meters, Handstand walk

Cap: 10 minutes

 

Goal & Movements

Move steadily for the biggest part of the workout, 6-7 minutes, then start pushing harder to go for the strong finish. Be explosive on the rower, you should perform long and strong pulls that will keep your cardiorespiratory function low (1 pull —> 1 calory is a good tempo), then go straight to an unbroken distance of 10 meters handstand walk. The hardest part of the workout is the transitions, the more consistently you move from one exercise to the other the better. Go straight from handstand walk to the rower, take a breath from the rower to the handstand walk that will allow you to move efficiently.

Options

Handstand walk —> 2 wall-walks

75 calories, Assault bike

25 box-jump overs 61/50cm – 24/20”

 

Rest 5 minutes

 

B. For time

50 calories, Assault bike

50 box-jump overs 61/50cm – 24/20”

 

Rest 5 minutes

 

C. For time

25 calories, Assault bike

75 box-jump overs 61/50cm – 24/20”

 

Goal

Try to achieve maximum performance in each portion. The calories in each portion decrease while the box jump overs increase, this means thatprobably the time it takes to complete each next portion will increase, but your effort should remain the same.

Movements

Move steadily on the Assault bike, ideally 65+ for men/58+ RPM for women and maintain a steady sustainable pace on the box-jump overs that will allow you to move continuously.
