Power Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

3×1 @ 93-97%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

4 snatch pulls + 3 hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 power snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve confidence and stability. Go for solid singles at 93% and increase if you feel confident enough within 93-97%. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull and a steady receiving position. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees, shoulders tight and elbows locked out.

—

 

Snatch High Pull

5×3 @ 100% of Max Power Snatch

 

Warm up

Continue from the percentage that you left the power snatch.

Goal

Improve technique and weightlifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell as high as possible. Do not force a pull with your arms yet make sure you drive the barbell as high as you can, close to your shoulders and your body’s vertical line.

Back Squat

ΕΜΟΜ 10 Minutes of

Odd: 2 reps @ 70% (explosive)

Even: 8 reps @ 60% (pace)

 

Warm up

Sets of 6 reps until you reach 60%.

Goal

Improve consistency. Work for 10 minutes, every odd minute perform 2 lifts at 70% and every even minute remove some weight from the barbell so that you perform 8 reps at 60%. Once you complete each set rest until the next minute starts.

Movements

Go the doubles with focus on an explosive squat from the deep position to the standing position (eccentric phase) and maintain a steady tempo on the 8-rep sets.

Gymnastics Skill

Practice the Front Kip (ring muscle-up from inverted hang)

 

Goal & Movements

Work for 15 minutes on the Front Kip. Establish a solid and straight inverted hang on the rings, bring your legs close to your chest and then explosively kick them. At the same time follow your legs with your upper body to get yourself over the rings using a lever mechanism.

Engine

A. AMRAP 90 Seconds of

Row, 14/10 calories

Max reps, Burpees over rower

Rest 30 seconds between sets

*Follow this sequence until 100 burpees are completed.

 

Rest 5 minutes

 

B. AMRAP 90 Seconds of

Row, 14/10 calories

Max reps, Double-unders

Rest 30 seconds between sets

*Follow this sequence until 500 double-unders are completed.

 

Goal & pace

Perform each part of this session in sets until you complete a total number of 100 burpees in part A and 500 double-unders in part B. Move fast and explosively through the calories on the rower, then go straight to the burpees / double-unders and try to maintain a fast yet sustainable pace that will allow you to move constantly and regulate your breathing as you do that. Once you complete the first set estimate a plausible number of reps in each set and try to achieve that number until you reach the finish.
