Conditioning

For time

10 rounds of

9 deadlifts 102/70kg – 225/155lb.

9 strict handstand push-ups

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

Start strong but make sure you will be able to finish strong as well. Prefer to break each exercise from the start into a big and a smaller set (5-4, 6-3) with fast, efficient reps. The tax is commutative and you don’t want to reach an early muscle failure in either exercise. Go the biggest part of the workout at 90-95% of max effort, then start pushing harder by shortening your transitions and trying more the unbroken on each exercise.

Options

102/70kg – 225/155lb —> 80/55kg – 175/120lb.

Strict handstand push-ups —> kipping handstand push-ups —> hand release push-ups

Barbell Cycling – Gymnastics

AMRAP 14 Minutes of

3.3.6.6.9.9*…etc reps of

Hang power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

Ring dips

 

Notes

The goal is to keep moving efficiently with the barbell despite the taxing combination of snatches-ring dips. Go each exercise unbroken on the first, manageable rounds and start breaking into a big and a smaller set as reps in each round get more than you can handle. Prefer to break the dips rather than the hang power snatches in order to catch your breath or briefly recover. Consider that it’s better to go the snatches unbroken for as long as possible in order to avoid any unnecessary movements (deadlifts etc).

*2 times each set

Options

Ring dips —> ring push-ups
