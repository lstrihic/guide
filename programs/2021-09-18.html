Warm up

2 sets of

3 back push presses + 2 snatch balance + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Improve stability. Use racks to load and unload the barbell. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Deadlifts

EMOM 10 Minutes of

3 reps @ 80-85%

 

Warm up

3×8 deadlifts @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Few sets of 3 reps until 80%.

Goal

Improve strength and consistency. Work for 10 minutes, select a weight that you feel comfortable with within 80-85%. In each minute complete 3 consecutive reps and rest the remaining time until the next minute starts.

Movements

Ideally go touch and go and focus on utilising your legs on each rep so as to to take much effort away from your back. If you go with single reps make sure you don’t rest too much between them.

Max Effort

For time

Run, 400 meters

15 ring muscle-ups

Cap: 4 minutes

 

Warm up

Run, 400 meters @moderate pace

Then,

2 sets of

12/9 cal, Bike-erg

3 ring muscle-ups

Rest as needed between sets

Goal & Movements

This is a sprint, go fast and push yourself to your limits. Run at a fast pace < 1’30”/km that will make you breathe heavily yet leave you with enough gas in the tank for the ring muscle-ups. On the ring muscle-ups ideally go unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take short to minimum rest and continue. Alternatively, if you know that 15 reps is too much, break from the start into a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with minimum rest between.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups
