Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 overhead squats + 3 squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2-3 squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. Perform the heaviest lifts within a range of 93-97% depending on your confidence. The goal is successfully made single lifts. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position.

Snatch Pull

2×2 @ 100% of Max Squat Snatch

2×2 @ 105%

 

Warm up

Continue to the snatch pull from the percentage that you stopped the squat snatch.

Goal

Improve pulling strength. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell over hip height.

Warm up

Then,

3.4.5 reps of

Ground to overhead 20/10kg – 45/25lb. plate

Ring pull-ups

Goal

This session consists of two consecutive sequences of exercises divided by 60 GHD sit-ups. The goal is to pace the part before the GHD and then push to maintain the same or faster pace in the next part (after GHD).

Movements

Break the single arm dumbbell overhead lunges into 2×10-meter unbroken distances, switch arms in each 10-meter distance. Move efficiently on the rope finding solid footings to squat and assist your upper body. Ascend fast, descend smoothly by sliding down, extending your body and jumping on the ground. Break the first handstand push-ups into a big and some smaller manageable sets with short rest between. On the GHD sit-ups go unbroken at a solid sustainable tempo. In the after-GHD part try to achieve the same sets on the handstand push-ups and if this is not possible, tale shorter rest between sets.

Options

30/22,5kg – 70/50lb —> 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.
