Squat Clean and Jerk

1×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

5×1 @ 85%

 

Warm up

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and technique. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a fast drive under the barbell, a solid deep receiving position and an explosive push jerk.

Gymnastics ATP Complex #1

20 toes to bar

15 pull-ups

10 chest to bar pull-ups

5 bar muscle-ups

 

Goal

Focus on technique and transitions. Try to complete all the exercises unbroken without falling of the pull up bar.

Movements

Go the pull-ups and chest to bar pull-ups with butterfly technique considering that the faster you push through the reps the less you will be hanging of the pull up bar and the more chances you will have to complete the complex. Emphasize on smooth transitions between kipping technique (toes to bar) — butterfly technique (pull-ups, CH2B) — keeping technique (muscle-ups). If butterfly is not possible go all the movements with kipping technique and unbroken for as long as possible.

Options

20.15.10.5 → 16.12.8.4 reps

Or

15 toes to bar

10 pull-ups

5 chest to bar pull-ups

Engine

4 sets of

Ski, 500 meters

Row, 500 meters

Bike-erg, 1000 meters

*Start a new set every 12 minutes

 

Goal & Movements

Work for a total volume of about 45-48 minutes. Complete the triplet within 12 minutes and rest until the next 12 minute time frame starts again (each set is 12 minutes). Try to increase your pace with each set, move explosively on each erg and push quickly through the given meters. Transitions will play a big role on your performance, focus on fast transitions that will allow you complete each set fast and efficiently.

Option

Ski → run, 400 meters

Bike-erg → run, 400 meters
