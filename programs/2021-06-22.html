Hang Power Clean

2×3 @ 50%

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

4×1 @ 80%

 

Rest > 90 seconds between sets

 

Notes

Warm up

3 sets of

5 clean pulls + 5 hang power clean + 5 front squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 hang power cleans until 50%

Goal

Improve power and stability on the hang position.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull, a solid front rack position and smooth drive of the barbell down to the hang position.

—

 

Clean High Pull

2×4 @ 90%

2×3 @ 100%

 

Rest > 90 seconds between sets
Rest < 10 seconds between lifts

 

Notes

Warm up

Go to the clean high pulls straight from the last set of hang power cleans.

Goal

Improve pulling strength. Go with single lifts.

Movements

Focus on a slow first pull and a fast second pull once the barbell is over the knees, finish with an explosive shoulder shrug.

Conditioning

For time

Run, 800 meters

66 double kettlebell shoulders to overhead 16/12kg – 44/26lb.

Run, 400 meters

33 double kettlebell snatches 16/12kg – 44/26lb.

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

Warm up

400 meter, Run

Then,

2 rounds of

10 single-arm kettlebell shoulder to overhead

12 alternating single-arm snatches

Goal & Movements

Run at a fast yet steady pace throughout the session. On the kettlebell exercises focus on movement mechanics. Break the shoulders to overhead into a big set and continue with as few manageable sets as possible, on the contrary, go the snatches unbroken for as long as possible.

Barbell Cycling

A. 5 rounds of

3 power cleans 83/61kg – 180/135lb.

2 front squats 83/61kg – 180/135lb.

1 jerk 83/61kg – 180/135lb.

Rest 5 minutes

 

B. 10 rounds of

3 power cleans 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

2 front squats 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

1 jerk 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

 

Notes

Warm up

2 sets

3 power cleans + 3 front squat + 3 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of increasing load until the given weight.

Goal

The goal is to move efficiently with the barbell. Try to maintain a steady tempo throughout each portion, your performance in each round should be as close as possible to the first round.

Movements

Go the cleans with single lifts (drop & go), then continue from the last clean to the front squats and jerk without dropping the barbell to the ground, continue in this way for all rounds.

Options

A. 83/61kg – 180/135lb. —> 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

B. 61/43kg – 135/95lb. —> 52/37kg – 115/80lb.
