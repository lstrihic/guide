Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat

2×2+2 @ 60%

1×2+2 @ 65%

1×1+2 @ 70%

1×1+2 @ 75%

5×1+1 @ 80%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

2 back presses + 3 push presses + 4 back jerks + 3 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats 20/15kg barbell

then

Sets of 2 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats until 60%.

Goal

Improve technique and stability. The lifts at 60% are 2 times of 2 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive push press, a fast drive under the barbell and slow controlled overhead squats. Work on a comfortable snatch width grip.

10 meters, Handstand walk

5 bar muscle-ups

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Warm up

3 set of

10 meters, Handstand walk

3-5 bar muscle-ups

10 meters, Double dumbbell shoulders rack lunges < 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Rest 60 seconds between sets

*go the last set with prescribed weights

Goal

The volume of the exercises decreases with each round. Be consistent, break the handstand walk and double dumbbell shoulders rack lunges into 10-meter unbroken distances and try to move efficiently through each distance.

Movements

Ideally go the bar muscle-ups unbroken, alternatively break into a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with minimum rest between. Focus on a solid front rack position and fast handstand walk with quick steps and a straight vertical handstand position.

Options

5 meters of Handstand walk —> 1 wall walk

Bar muscle ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.

Max Effort

For time

150 double-unders

30 double dumbbell thrusters 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Cap: 4 minutes

 

Warm up

2 sets of

Assault or Echo Bike @ fast pace

Then,

2 rounds of

30 double-unders

10 double dumbbell thrusters

 

Goal & Movements

Push fast and unbroken through the double-unders with focus on breathing well, if you break immediately continue with the remaining reps and then go straight to the double dumbbell thrusters. Go the double dumbbell thrusters unbroken for as long as possible, if you rbeak take minimum rest and continue striving until the last second.
