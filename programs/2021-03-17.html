Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat

3×1+3 @ 75% of Max Snatch Balance

3×1+2 @ 80%

3×1+1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2 snatch balances + 2 overhead squats until you reach 75%. The lifts at 75% are 3 times of 1 snatch balance + 3 overhead squats. Focus on a solid overhead position and slow controlled squats. Rest no less than 90 seconds between lifts.

Notes

In this couplet the weight increases in each round as prescribed below, you can preset numerous barbells or have someone changing the weights on a single barbell. The descending reps allow you to keep your effort level high, prefer to break the high-rep sets into a big and 1-2 smaller sets with minimum rest between and go the low-rep set unbroken for as long as possible. On the front squats push yourself to go each set unbroken for as long as possible as a steady tempo, the less you drop the bar the better.

Gymnastics

For form

15.10.5 reps of

Strict deficit handstand push-ups (10/5cm – 4/2”)

Bar muscle-ups

Box-jumps over 76/60cm – 30/24”

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Ideally go the strict deficit handstand push-ups unbroken for as long as possible, break 2-3 reps before you feel you reach muscle-failure, take a breath and continue. Alternatively break from the start into a big and 1-2 smaller sets with short rest between. Work on touch & go box-jumps, go unbroken for as long as possible.

Options

Strict deficit handstand push-ups  → strict regular handstand push-ups → pike push-ups

Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups
