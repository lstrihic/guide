Gymnastics

A. AMRAP 7 Minutes of

2.4.6.8.. etc reps of

Strict handstand push-ups

Strict ring dips

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. AMRAP 7 Minutes of

4.8.12.16..etc reps of

Kipping handstand push-ups

Kipping ring dips

 

Notes

This session consists of two portions. The goal is to push steadily through both portions with a smooth and efficient transition from strict form to kipping technique. In the first portion focus on proper form and full range of motion. Perform fast reps, the less you stay on the handstand position the less you tax your upper body. Start by going unbroken and break into manageable sets as reps of each round become too many to handle. In the second portion focus on proper kipping technique, balance and whole-body movement. Move consistently for as long as possible at a fast yet steady tempo.

Options

A. Strict handstand push-ups → pike push-ups

Strict ring dips → ring push-ups

B. Practice kipping in both exercises
