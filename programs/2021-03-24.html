Power Snatch

10 reps @ 70%

8 reps @ 75%

6 reps @ 80%

4 reps @ 85%

2 reps @ 90%

 

Notes

Consider this session as For time. Ideally go unbroken with touch and go reps, especially on the light-load rounds (10, 8 reps). On the heavier loads prefer to go with solid, single lifts with short to minimum rest between. Increase weight on the barbell on your own, practice on fast and efficient weight changes.

—

 

Conditioning

AMRAP 15 Minutes of

Row, 14/12 calories

8 shoulders to overhead 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Row, 14/12 calories

8 hang squat cleans 61/43kg – 135/95lb

 

Notes

Maintain a steady pace for the biggest part of the workout and then start pushing harder by shortening your transitions and in-between rest. Row at a fast steady pace, 1350+ for men / 1100+ for women and try to go the shoulders to overhead and hang squat cleans unbroken. Alternatively, break the shoulders to overhead into a big and a smaller set with short rest between and go the hang squat cleans unbroken for as long as possible.

Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb → 52/37kg – 115/80lb

Gymnastics

4 sets of

AMRAP 4 Minutes of

20/15 calories, Assault bike

15 bar muscle-ups

Max calories, Assault bike

Rest 2 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Push fast and efficiently through the calories and the bar muscle-ups, maintaining a fast explosive pace on the rower. Ideally go the bar muscle-ups unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. Once you complete the couplet go straight to the max-cal set. Maintain a fast pace yet manageable enough to sustain for the whole remaining time.

Options

Assault bike → bike-erg → row

15 → 12 → 9 → 6 reps of

Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups

*Choose a number of reps that you can perform unbroken while fresh. Switch to chest to bar pull-ups if you struggle to perform 3 unbroken reps of bar muscle-ups.

Engine

8 sets of

AMRAP 4 Minutes of

Buy in: Row, 300/230 meters

Then,

3.6.9..etc reps of

Burpees to target

Assault bike, Calories

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Start with 300/230 meters on the rower then continue with as many reps as you can of the couplet. Row at a fast yet sustainable pace, ideally same for all sets. On the couplet move explosively on the bike, as cals increase slightly drop down to a more manageable pace. Go the burpees at a steady tempo throughout.

Options

Assault bike → bike-erg
