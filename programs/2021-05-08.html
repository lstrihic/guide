Power Snatch + Overhead Squat

1×2+1 @ 60%

1×2+1 @ 70%

1×2+1 @ 75%

1×1+1 @ 80%

3×1+2 @ 85%

1×3+1 @ 75%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2 power snatches + 2 overhead squats until you reach 60%. Improve power and overhead stability. The lifts at 60% are 2 power snatches + 1 overhead squat, at 85% are 3 times of 1 power snatch + 2 overhead squats. Go with single power snatches, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets (times), no more than 15 seconds between power snatches.

Conditioning

For time

Run, 800 meters

Then,

3 rounds of

20 box-jump overs 61/50cm – 24/20”

10 hang squat cleans 83/61kg – 185/135lb.

Then,

Run, 800 meters

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

Go the first run at a fast yet manageable pace that will allow you to prime and be fresh and go straight to the couplet that follows. Go the box jump overs in each round at a steady manageable tempo, ideally touch and go. On the hang squat cleans go unbroken for as long as possible, when you feel that you cannot continue with technical touch and go lifts switch to single lifts. On the last run start increasing your pace up to a max effort giving all you got as you reach the finish.

Options

83/61kg – 185/135lb. —> 61/35kg – 135/95lb.

Notes

Warm up with sets of 4-5 reps < 55% and 2-3 reps < 70%. Improve strength. Perform the press by using only your shoulders keeping your core, hips and legs still and straight. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Max Effort

For time

30 calories, Assault bike

15 bar muscle-ups

Cap: 3 minutes

 

Notes

Move fast and explosively from the start. Push through the calories on the bike at a really fast pace, then take a short breath and go for the muscle ups. On the muscle-ups ideally go unbroken for as as long as you can, break only 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching muscle failure take minimum rest and continue. Alternatively break into a big and 1-2 smaller sets depending on your comfort. Don’t hesitate to push yourself to your limits.

Options

Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

*Go with chest to bar pull-ups if you can’t perform at least 7 unbroken bar muscle-ups
