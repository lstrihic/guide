Hang Power Snatch

2×3 @ 60% of Max Hang Power Snatch

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

4×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 half squat snatch balances @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power snatches until 60%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

First deadlift the barbell to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to the hip crease and avoid spending much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position. Reposition swiftly and then continue to the next rep.

Conditioning

For time

6 rounds of

Bike-erg, 20/15 calories

15 deadlifts 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

10 bar-facing burpees over

5 shoulders to overhead 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

40 calories, Bike-erg @ easy pace

Then,

9 deadlifts

6 bar-facing burpees over

3 shoulders to overhead

Goal

Move steadily for the first 3-4 rounds and give all you got in the last round. The transitions between the exercises should be that fast as to allow you to go each exercise unbroken and move at a sustainable tempo through the session.

Movements

Push fast and explosively through the cals on the Bike-erg, get the momentum going and then sustain a fast pace until you are done with them. Ideally go the deadlifts and the shoulders to overhead unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between.

Options

70/52kg – 155/115lb. —> 61/43 – 135/95lb.

Deadlift

Daily Max

Then

3×6 @ 75%

Rest 90 seconds between sets

 

Warm up

3x 8 deadlifts @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 reps < 55% and 2 reps < 75%, continue with single reps.

Goal

Improve form and consistency. Build up to the heavy lift of the day with as few sets as possible. Then drop down to the given percentages and work on form and stability.

Movements

Focus on a solid grip on the barbell (mixed grip), and smooth reps utilizing not only your back but also you hips and legs.

Max Effort

For time

35/25 calories, Assault-bike

60 meters, Sandbag or D-ball bear hug carry 70/50kg – 150/110lb.

Cap: 4 minutes

 

Warm up

30 calories, Assault bike @ easy pace

Then,

2 sets of

9/7 calories, Assault bike @ fast pace

10 meters, Sandbag or D-ball bear hug carry

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal & Movements

This is a sprint so you should go fast from the start and push yourself to your limits early. Move explosively on the bike and push swiftly through the given reps, 72+ for men | 63+ for women RPM, then find a comfortable position with the d-ball that will allow you to maintain a solid grip, breathe well and go unbroken for as long as you can by 10-meter distances back and forth.
