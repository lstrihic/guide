Conditioning

AMRAP 15 Minutes of

8 strict handstand push-ups

10 toes to bar

12 single arm dumbbell snatches 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

 

Notes

Move steadily through the biggest part of the workout, 10+ minutes and then start pushing harder by shortening your transitions between exercises. Ideally go each exercise unbroken and perform smooth transitions between in order to achieve that. Alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. The single arm dumbbell snatches are alternating, perform smooth and technical transitions mid-air for extra speed.

Options

Strict → kipping handstand push-ups

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb → 15/10kg – 35/25lb

*Choose kipping if you can’t go 8 unbroken strict reps while fresh.

Max Effort

For time

15/12 calories, Ski-erg

3 leg-less rope climbs

15/12 calories, Ski-erg

3 clean and jerks 102/70kg – 225/155lb

 

Notes

Move fast from the start. Push explosively through the calories on the ski and go straight to the rope climbs. On the rope move quickly and as technically as possible, use your legs to create momentum (kicks or kipping) and try to spend the least time on the rope, descend smoothly by sliding down, extending and jumping to the ground. The clean & jerks are the most difficult part, consider that you should be fast but ensure successfully made lifts.

Options

Leg-less → regular rope climbs

102/70kg 225/155lb → 82/52kg – 180/115lb
