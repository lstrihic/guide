Hang Power Clean

2×3 @ 50%

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

2×1 @ 95%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 50%. Improve power and hang position. Focus on a solid, hang position and a powerful pull with an explosive shoulder shrug. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

—

 

Clean Pull

2×3 @ 95%

2×3 @ 105%

 

Notes

Continue to the clean pulls from where you left the hang power cleans. Improve technique and explosiveness. Focus on a solid first pull and proper weightlifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Conditioning

AMRAP 8 Minutes of

17 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb

3 bar muscle-ups

 

Notes

Move from the start at a fast yet sustainable pace and increase to max effort as you reach the end. The transitions will play the biggest role. Go both exercises unbroken and perform the fastest possible transitions between them that will allow you to achieve that.

Options

Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups

*Go with bar muscle-ups if you can perform at least 3 unbroken bar muscle-up while fresh.

—

Barbell Cycling

5 rounds of

30 double-unders

12 deadlifts 80/52kg – 175/115lb

30 double-unders

7 shoulders to overhead 80/52kg – 175/115lb

 

Notes

The goal is to move efficiently with the barbell under metabolic fatigue. Go the double-unders unbroken throughout. Ideally go both lifting exercises unbroken, alternatively break them (preferably the deadlifts) into a big and a smaller touch and go set with short to minimum rest between.

Options

80/52kg – 175/115lb → 61/43kg – 135/95lb

—

 

Gymnastics

For form

8 rounds of

2 rope climbs 4,7m

5 deficit handstand push-ups 10/5cm – 4/2” (plate)

10 meters, Handstand walk

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between. Be technical on the rope, ascend fast finding solid footings and squatting on the rope, descend by sliding down. Ideally go the handstand walk unbroken, alternatively go by 2×5 meters unbroken distances.

Options

Deficit → regular handstand push-ups

10 → 5 meters of

Handstand walk → 2 wall-walks

*Go with handstand walk if you can perform at least 5 unbroken meters while fresh.

—

 

Engine

A. Run, 15 minutes @ easy pace

 

B. 10 sets of

Run, 500 meters @ 1’40”-2’00” or 3’20”-4’00”/km

Rest 2 minutes between

 

Notes

Jog for 15 minutes (warm-up) prior and 10 minutes (cool down) after the session. Additionally perform before main session: front swings, side cross swings, front leg raise toe taps, 3 sets of 50 meter sprints. Work at a pace within 93-97% of your Vo2max and try to keep it steady throughout the 10 sets.

Options

10 → 8 → 6 sets of

Run, 500 meters @ 1’40”-2’00” or 3’20”-4’00”/km
