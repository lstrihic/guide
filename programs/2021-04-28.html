Power Clean & Jerk

2×3 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

5×1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve power and jerk technique. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid, technical jerk. Prefer to work on split jerk as it is a chance to improve your technique, in any case you can go with either split or power jerks. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Front Squat

ΕΜΟΜ 10 Minutes

Odd: 1×2 @ 80%

Even: 1×8 @ 60%

 

Notes

In every odd minute perform 2 solid squats at 80%, every even minute perform 8 squats at a steady tempo at 60%. Focus on successfully made reps on the heavy lifts and proper form and tempo on the lighter lifts. Put the plates on the barbell in such way so that you load and unload according to the given percentages with ease.

Then,

9.7.5 reps of

Bar muscle-ups

10 meters, Handstand walk

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Ideally go each exercise unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. The more technical you are on the execution of the exercises the more efficiently you will push through the total volume. Give emphasis on kipping technique on the pull-up bar, move fast on the handstand walk with quick steps and try to maintain o proper handstand walk form (toes pointing upwards, straight legs tight, core and shoulders).
