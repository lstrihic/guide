Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat

2×1+2 @ 60% of Max Snatch Balance

1×1+2 @ 65%

1×1+1 @ 70%

1×1+1 @ 75%

1×1+1 @ 80%

3×1+1 @ 85%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 back push presses + 2 snatch balance + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats until 60%.

Goal

Improve stability. Use racks to load and unload the barbell. The lifts at 60% are 2 times 1 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive back jerk and a fast drive under the barbell, once you have established a solid overhead position continue with slow controlled overhead squats.

Hang Power Snatch + Hang Squat Snatch

2×2+2 @ 60% go Max Hang Power Snatch

1×2+2 @ 70%

1×1+2 @ 75%

1×1+2 @ 80%

5×1+2 @ 85%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

2 snatch pulls + 2 hang power snatches + 2 overhead squats + 2 hang squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power snatches + 2 hang squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Work on consistency and confidence on the hang position. The lifts at 60% are 2 times 2 hang power snatches + 2 hang squat snatches, at 80% are 1 hang power snatch + 2 hang squat snatches. Rest no less than 60 seconds between sets.

Movements

First pull the barbell to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Make sure you drive the barbell smoothly and under control back to the hip crease and avoid spending too much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position. In each set reposition swiftly and continue to the next rep.

Cap: 25 minutes

 

Warm up

1-2 rounds of

200 meters, Run

20 calories, Ski

20 power cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

15 box step-ups

10 rope pull-ups

Goal & Movements

This session consists of two consecutive sequences of exercises divided by 10 rope climbs. Maintain a fast yet manageable pace on the first ski, box-jumps over and run that will allow you to breathe and break the power cleans of the 1st part (before farmers carry) into a big and 1-2 smaller touch & go sets with minimum rest between. Then move fast and efficiently on the rope finding solid footings to squat and assist your upper body as much as possible. In the 2nd part move in the same fashion yet make sure you maintain an equal or faster tempo than in the 1st part.

D Row 375 meters @ Max effort

 

Strokes

3000 meters @ 22/min

1500 meters @ 24/min

750 meters @ 28/min

375 meters @ free

Warm up

4 minutes @ easy pace warm up routines (1st minute only arm pulls, 2nd only leg pulls, 3rd and 4th minute regular rowing)

Rest 1 minute

2 sets of

2 minutes @ 2km PR pace + 16 seconds

Goal

The goal is to prime by progressively increasing your pace through 3 decreasing distance portions, and finish with a max effort distance of 375 meters. Row 3000 meters at 2km PR pace + 16 seconds, rest 3 minutes, row 1500 meters at 2km PR pace + 13 seconds, rest 2 minutes, row 750 meters at 2km PR pace + 7 seconds, rest 1 minute and finish with the max effort distance. Stroke rate is free, keep in mind that fewer strokes require more powerful pulls while more strokes cause more cardiovascular tax. Prefer to start with fewer strokes and, if needed, increase throughout the distances.

Cool down

4 minutes @ very light pressure.
