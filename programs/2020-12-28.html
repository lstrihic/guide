Hang power clean

2×2 @ 50%

2×2 @ 60%

1×1 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 50%. Improve pulling power and comfort at the hang position. The hang starting position is with the barbell 10cm over the knees. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid reception. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets.

Conditioning

For time

33.27.21.15.9 reps of

Power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

Box-jumps 60/50cm – 24/20”

 

Notes

Descending reps give you the psychological advantage. Start the high-rep rounds (33, 27, 21) by breaking into a big set and 2-3 smaller manageable sets with short rest between (all touch & go). As reps of each round decrease (15, 9) push harder and attempt to go unbroken for as long as possible. On the box jumps prefer to maintain a fast, sustainable pace from start to finish.

For form

Strict ring muscle-ups

Parallettes handstand push-ups

 

Notes

Focus on quality and full range of motion. Go unbroken for as long as proper form allows. Ideally, try to perform each exercise unbroken and take adequate rest between rounds and exercises in order to achieve that.

Options

Strict ring muscle-ups → kipping ring muscle-ups → strict chest to rings pull-ups

Parallettes handstand push-ups → deficit handstand push-ups (10/5cm plates) → regular handstand push-ups

Engine

4 sets of

Row, 60/45 calories

100 double-unders

20 bar-facing burpees over

Rest 2 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set and a progressively increased pace. Avoid going all out from the first set. Ideally pace the row and maintain an equal or slightly faster pace with each set throughout the session, go the double-unders unbroken and adjust your tempo on the burpees by increasing it with each set.
