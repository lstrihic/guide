Squat Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

3×2 @ 85%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 overhead squats + 3 squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2-3 squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve technique an explosiveness. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Keep your barbell close to your body and avoid rushing to stand up from the squat position.

Squat Clean & Jerk

2×2+2 @ 50% of Max Squat Clean

2×2+2 @ 60%

1×2+2 @ 65%

1×2+2 @ 70%

1×3+3 @ 75%

3×3+3 @ 80%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 2 front squats + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat cleans & 2 jerks until 50%.

Goal

Improve technique and explosiveness. The lifts at 50% are 2 times 2 squat cleans & 2 jerks, at 75% are 3 squat cleans & 3 jerks. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between squat cleans.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Prefer to go with split jerks in order to work on technique and foot positioning.

Gymnastics Conditioning

AMRAP 8 Minutes of

2.4.6..etc reps of

Ring dips

Chest to bar pull-ups

Single leg squats

 

Warm up

3.6.9 reps of

Ring rows

Narrow squats

Kipping swings from the pull-up bar

Ring push-ups

Goal

Work steadily for the biggest part of the workout, 5+ minutes, and then start pushing harder. Transitions make a difference so try to perform equal transitions throughout the session and, if possible, increase speed as you reach the finish.

Movements

Go each exercise unbroken, as reps with each round increase don’t hesitate to break the ring dips and/or chest to bar pull-ups into manageable sets. Maintain a steady tempo on the single leg squats throughout the session.

Options

Ring dips —> ring push-ups

Chest to bar pull-ups —> pull-ups

*Go with scale option if you cant perform at lease 6 unbroken reps of each exercise while fresh.

Engine

4 sets of

Row, 30/23 calories

12 burpees over rower

Assault bike, 30/23 calories

12 burpees over rower

Row, 30/23 calories

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Goal & Movements

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Row at a fast yet sustainable pace for the whole session and maintain a powerful pace on the assault bike, 70+ for men | 63+ for women. The goal is the transitions, make sure you perform smooth transitions that will allow you to move through the session comfortably and efficiently.
