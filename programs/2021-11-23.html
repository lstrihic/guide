Power Clean & Jerk

2×3 @ 60% of Max Power Clean

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

4×1 @ 87-93%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power cleans & jerk until 60%.

Goal

Improve power and stability. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull, a solid front rack receiving position and an explosive jerk. Work on power and stability on the power jerks.

—

 

Clean Pull

5×2 @ 95% of Max Power Clean

 

Warm up

Continue to the clean pull from the percentage that you stopped the power clean & jerk.

Goal

Improve pulling strength. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell to hip height.

For time

4 rounds of

20 toes to bar

10 double dumbbell devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

30/23 calories, Assault bike

20 meters, Handstand walk

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

50 calories, Assault bike

Then,

2 rounds of

10 sit-ups

5 hand release push-ups

10 ground to overhead 20/10kg – 45/25lb. plate

5 knees to chest

10 meters, Bear walk

Goal & Movements

Move steadily on the first three rounds and push the last round hard. Break the toes to bar from the start into a big and 1-2 smaller sets with minimum rest between. Go the double dumbbell devils presses unbroken at a steady manageable pace and push swiftly through the calories on the assault bike at a pace that will allow you to breathe, 70RPM for men / 62 RPM for women. Break the handstand walk into 10-meter unbroken distances (5 meters mandatory) and this is the hardest part, move fast on your hands and take minimum yet adequate rest before and between distances.

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb —> 15/10kg – 50/35lb.

Barbell Cycling

3 sets of

5 hang power snatches

5 power snatches

5 squat snatches

83/57kg – 185/125lb.

Rest as needed between sets

 

Warm up

Sets of 2 hang power snatches + 2 power snatches + 2 squat snatches until you reach 83/57kg – 185/125lb.

Goal

The goal is to move fast and efficiently with the barbell. Select one of the given options that you are confident you can complete 5 hang power snatches unbroken when you are fresh.

Movements

Go the hang power snatches unbroken, then ideally continue with an unbroken set of touch and go power snatches, alternatively, go with fast single lifts with minimum rest between. Prefer to go the squat snatches with fast solid single lifts.

Options

A. 70/52kg – 155/115lb

B. 61/43kg – 135/95lb.
