Conditioning

AMRAP 15 Minutes of

8 double dumbbell hang power cleans 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

7,5 meters, Double dumbbell shoulders rack lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

6 bar muscle-ups / 12 toes to bar

7,5 meters, Double dumbbell shoulders rack lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

 

Notes

Go the 1st round of the AMRAP with bar muscle-ups then continue the 2nd round with toes to bar, the 3rd with bar muscle up and continue in this fashion alternating the two exercises with each next round. Move steadily through the biggest part of the workout. +- 10 minutes with focus on breathing well, then start pushing harder for the strong finish. Go each exercise unbroken and keep in mind that transitions play a major role, make sure you keep them equal or shorten them as you push through the workout.

Options

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb. —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.

Notes

Build up with sets of 5 reps until you reach 65%. Focus on a steady tempo at the low percentages and solid single lifts at the increasing percentages. Rest no less than 90 seconds from low-rep sets to high-rep sets, no less than 2 minutes from high-rep sets to low-rep sets.

For form

Strict pull-ups

Strict ring dips

*Vest 9/7kg – 20/14lb.

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Go each exercise unbroken for as long as proper form allows, stop 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching muscle failure. The vest adds extra weight and offers superb strength benefits, try to adopt as much as you can to its discomfort.

Engine

4 sets of

40/30 calories, Assault bike

30 burpees to target*

20/15 calories, Assault bike

10 burpees to target*

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Try to maintain high performance in each set. Maintain a fast pace on the assault bike, 65+ for men | 58+ for women, and move at a steady tempo on the burpees throughout the session with focus on breathing well and proper reps (efficiently and effortlessly touching the target each time). Mark a target (rope, rings, pull-up bar) 10cm over your extended arms overhead when standing.
