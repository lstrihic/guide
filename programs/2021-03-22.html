Hang Power Clean

2×2 @ 50%

2×2 @ 60%

1×1 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 50%. Improve explosiveness and comfort at the hang position. Focus on a prompt, solid hang (barbell 10cm above knees) and a powerful pull with an explosive shoulder shrug. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Gymnastics

For form

5 rounds of

12 strict pull-ups

30 double-unders

12 strict handstand push-ups

30 double-unders

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible. Ideally go the strict pull-ups and strict handstand push-ups unbroken with smooth transitions between exercises. Alternatively break each gymnastic exercise into a big and a smaller set with short rest between.

Options

12 → 10 → 8 reps of

Strict pull-ups → assisted strict pull-ups

12 → 10 → 8 reps of

Strict handstand push-ups —> pike push-ups

*Choose a number of reps that you can perform unbroken while fresh.

Engine

AMRAP 30 Minutes of

Assault bike, calories

*Every 3 minutes perform 12 burpee box-jump overs 60/50cm – 24/20”

 

Notes

The goal is to be consistent and maintain a fast yet sustainable pace throughout the session. Go the bike steadily at 65+/60+ RPM. every time the timer goes (3:00. 6:00, 9:00, …etc – including 0:00) get off the bike and perform 12 burpee box-jump overs. Focus on working consistently on the bike and pushing hard through the burpee box-jump overs on every break. This is the more efficient way to go.
