Squat snatch

1×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

Notes

Work on technique and explosiveness. Go for solid single lifts, focus on a fast drive under the bar and a steady deep receiving position. Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, mo more than 10 seconds between lifts.

—

Snatch pulls

1×2 @ 80%

1×2 @ 85%

3×2 @ 90%

Notes

Drop down to 80% of Max squat snatch and continue with the snatch pulls. Improve pulling strength. Focus on a slow controlled starting pull and increase speed once the barbell is over knee height, finish with an explosive shoulder shrug. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

30 box-jumps 60/50cm

20 power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb

10 bar muscle-ups

Cap: 13 minutes

Notes

Pace the first 2 rounds, push hard on the last round. Go the box jump-overs at a steady pace with focus on breathing well, ideally touch and go Break the power snatches into no more than 2-3 big touch & go sets, if you feel that you can’t maintain proper t&g technique immediately switch to single lifts. Ideally go the bar muscle-ups unbroken, alternatively prefer to break the first 2 rounds into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between and push the last round for the unbroken.

Options

Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups (*go with bar muscle-ups if you can easily perform 6 unbroken reps)

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

20 thrusters 52/35kg – 115/75lb

5 L-sit rope climbs 4,7 meters

15 thrusters @ 61/43kg – 135/95lb

5 leg-less rope climbs 4,7 meters

10 thrusters @ 70/47,5kg 155/105lb

5 rope climbs 4,7 meters

Notes

Focus on proper form and quality of movements. Ideally go the thrusters unbroken, alternatively break into no more than 2-3 sets with short to minimum rest between. Move fast and efficiently on the rope by effectively switching your technique according to each type, in any case descend technically by smoothly sliding down or extending and jumping down.

Options

L-sit → leg-less → regular rope climbs

Engine

A. 2 sets of 

Row, 500 meters @ 24 strokes/min

Rest 2 minutes between

B1. 4 sets of

Row, 1250 meters @ 24 strokes/min

Rest 2 minutes & 30 seconds 

Pace: 2km PR pace + 12 seconds*

Rest 5 minutes

B2. Row 400 meters, As fast as possible 

C. Row 25 minutes @ easy pace

Notes

Row 2 sets of 500 meters with steady 24 strokes, free pace, as warm-up. In the main session try to achieve high performance in each set. Focus primarily on maintaining 24 strokes/min and secondarily achieving an approximately  2km PR pace + 12 seconds. The 400-meter sprint is free strokes, free pace. Row 25 minutes at very light pressure to cool down.

*This is a relative pace of your 2000-meter PR pace. It is recommended that you test the distance so you can find your ideal pace.
