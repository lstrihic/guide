Deadlifts

EMOM 10 Minutes of

1 rep @ 90-95%

 

Warm up

3×6-8 deadlifts @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Few sets of 2 reps until 90%.

Goal

Improve strength and consistency. Work for 10 minutes, select a weight that you feel comfortable with within the given percentages. Complete 1 rep in each minute and rest the remaining time until the next minute starts.

Movements

Focus on utilising your legs on each rep so as to take much effort away from your back.
