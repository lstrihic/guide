Warm up

3 sets of

3 strict presses + 3 push presses + 3 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

then

Sets of 2 push presses + 2 jerks until 70%.

Goal

Improve technique and stability. The lifts at 70% are 2 times of 2 push presses + 2 jerks. Prefer to work on split jerk. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive push press using your legs and hips and a technical jerk with a solid deep receiving position.

10 ring muscle-ups

20 box-jumps over 61/50cm – 24/20”

10 meters, Front rack lunges 61/43kg – 135/95lb

5 ring muscle-ups

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Warm up

2-3 rounds of

9 box-jumps over

6 front rack lunges

3 rings muscle-ups

Goal

There are two consecutive sequences of the same exercises the second sequence is half the volume of the first one. Pace the higher volume and push the lower volume harder to go for the strong finish.

Movements

Go the box jump over at a steady sustainable pace throughout the session. Ideally go the ring muscle-ups unbroken, alternatively break the 10 reps from the start into manageable sets. Move steadily on the front rack lunges by 5 meter unbroken distances.

Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb —> 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to rings pull-ups

Strongman

5 sets of

AMRAP 60 seconds of

Heavy sled push

Rest 60 seconds between sets

 

Warm up

30-50 meters, Empty sled push

then

Smooth built up to a load equal to your bodyweight.

Goal

Consider 2 meters as 1 rep. Move continuously in each 60-minute time frame pushing the sled back and forth by 10-meters distances.

Movement

Work on your body position. Focus on being close to the ground and pushing the sled by touching your hands and shoulders or shoulders on the bars, avoid using your arms fully extended.

Goals & Movements

Push fast and unbroken through the GHD sit-ups at a steady tempo, then go straight to the sandbag. Move steadily with focus on technical and efficient lifts, take the minimum rest between lifts that will alow you to move constantly and sustain a steady or increased tempo until the last second.
