Hang Power Snatch

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

2×2 @ 75%

2×2 @ 80%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

2 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 4 half squat snatch balances @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power snatches until 65%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

First pull the barbell to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to the hip crease and avoid spending too much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position. In each set reposition swiftly and continue to the second rep.

Try to achieve high performance in each part. Front Squat

2×2 @ 70% + 8 touch and go box jumps 50cm – 20”

2×2 @ 75% + 8 touch and go box jumps 50cm – 20”

2×2 @ 80% + 8 touch and go box jumps 50cm – 20”

2×2 @ 85% + 8 touch and go box jumps 50cm – 20”

 

Warm up

Sets of 5 reps < 50%, 3 reps < 70%.

Goal

Improve form and efficiency. Once you finish the 2nd set of each percentage continue straight with a touch & go set of box jumps. Rest no less than 60 seconds between sets, no less than 90 seconds between percentages.

Movements

Focus on proper form. Maintain a steady straight back, a solid front rack position and strict movement at the bottom position (no bounce). On the box jumps focus on landing smoothly on the ground and under control so that you balance proper and prepare for each next rep.

Gymnastics Skill

Practice handstand walk ramp

 

Goal & Movements

Work for 10-15 minutes handstand walk on a ramp. If you have the official handstand walk ramp, mark a distance of 10 meters and place the ramp equally within the 10-meter distance. Work on that distance back and forth over the ramp. If you don’t have the ramp practice on a similar construction, if any.

Warm up

3 sets of

10 shoulder taps

30 seconds, High plank hold

Goal

Focus on proper form. Keep your body straight, your shoulders tight and try to avoid an arch position.

Movements

Move fast on the strict handstand push-ups, try to keep a triangle base on the ground and go for full range of motion.
