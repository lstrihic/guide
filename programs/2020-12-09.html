Conditioning

A. For time

4 rounds of

15 power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb

15 bar facing burpees over

Cap: 11 minutes

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. For time

4 rounds of

15 overhead squats 43/30kg – 95/65lb

9 ring muscle-ups

Cap: 13 minutes

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each one of the two portions.

A. On the first 3 rounds maintain a steady pace and push the last round as hard as you can. Go the power snatches with no more than 2-3 big sets with short rest between and the burpees at a fast yet sustainable pace. In the last round go the power snatches unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take minimum rest and continue, give all you got on the burpees.

B. Perform in the same fashion as A. Go the overhead squats unbroken at a steady, solid pace and break the ring muscle-up into no more than 2-3 sets with short to minimum rest between (ideally a big and a smaller set). In the last round, if possible increase your pace on the overhead squats and go the muscle-ups unbroken for as long as can.

Options

B. 9 → 7 → 5 reps of

Ring muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups → pull-ups

Back squat

2×3 @ 60%

3×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 80%

5×1 @ 85%*

1×8 @ 55%

 

Notes

Improve form and stability. Build up with sets of 3 reps < 60% and 2 reps < 85%. Move in each set at a steady tempo and with proper form. *Go the 85% lifts with 1 set in each minute, rest the remaining time until the next minute start (EMOM style).

Gymnastics

For form

5 rounds of

20 GHD sit-ups

15 ring push-ups

10 strict ring pull-ups

5 deficit handstand push-ups* (20/15cm)

 

Notes

Focus on form and full range of motion. Ideally go each exercise unbroken with adequate rest between sets and exercises. Alternatively break each exercise into no more than 2-3 sets with short rest between. *Use plates to create elevation.

Options

GHD sit-ups → toes to rings → weighted sit-ups

Ring push-ups → parallettes push-ups → regular push-ups

Strict ring pull-ups → ring rows (elevated feet)

Deficit handstand push-ups → regular handstand push-ups
