Hang power clean

2×2 @ 60%

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

2×2 @ 75%

 

Notes

Improve pulling power and hang position technique. The starting position is with the barbell about 10cm above the knees. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid receiving position. Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets.

—

 

Squat clean

1×2 @ 50%

1×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

2×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Work on explosiveness and confidence. Focus on a fast drive under the bar and a solid deep receiving position. Start lifting at a comfortable weight lighter than 50% and continue with the given percentages. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Notes

Push steadily and efficiently through the total volume. Ideally go each exercise unbroken, with smooth yet short transitions between them, alternatively break each muscle-up and chest to bar pull-up round into a big and a smaller set, further shortening your transitions. In any case, go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible, if you break immediately continue with the remaining reps.

Barbell Cycling – Gymnastics

A. For form

10 rounds of

7 power snatches 43/30kg -95/65lb

3 ring muscle-ups

 

Rest 5 minutes

 

B. For form

10 rounds of

3 power snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb

7 toes to bar

 

Notes

This session consists of two portions, each portion differs in style. The goal is technical solid lifts and efficient gymnastics. Go the first portion with unbroken power snatches, touch & go, with focus on proper rebounding on the ground and full range of motion. Go the second portion with solid, single lifts. Maintain a steady performance on the barbell throughout the 10 rounds (tempo, rest between lifts and exercises) and go the gymnastics unbroken. Perform smooth transitions that will allow you to move efficiently.

Options

Ring muscle-ups → chest to rings pull-ups

B. 61/43kg – 135/95kg → 52/35kg – 115/80lb

Engine

Run, 8km @ 4’35”-5’15”/km

 

Notes

Run a total distance of 8000 meters. Start smoothly and progressively increase your pace (in the first km) until you reach a pace within the given time-frame that will accord to about 80% of VO2Max. Jog for 5 minutes after the session as cool down.
