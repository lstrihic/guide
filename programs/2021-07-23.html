Warm up

Sets of 2 squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Conditioning

For time

75 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

50/38 calories, Row

35 GHD sit-ups

5 leg-less rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

35 GHD sit-ups

50/38 calories, Row

75 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

Cap: 25 minutes

 

Warm up

60 calories, Row

Then,

2-3 sets of

15 wall-balls

2 rope climbs

15 wall-balls

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal

This workout consists of two similar patterns in reverse order divided by 5 leg-less rope climbs. The goal is to slightly pace the first pattern and once you have efficiently completed the rope climbs increase your tempo to a near max effort.

Movements

Ideally go the first wall-balls unbroken, alternatively break into a big and no more than 2 smaller sets with short rest between, go the last wall-balls unbroken for as long as you can. On the rower and the GHD sit-ups move steadily at a solid sustainable tempo throughout the session, yet make sure your pace on the second rower is same or faster than your pace on the first rower. Move fast on the rope climbs using your legs and hips (kicks or keeping technique), to assist your upper body on your ascent, descent smoothly by sliding down

Options

Leg-less —> regular rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

Warm up

3 rounds of

8 double kettlebell deadlifts

4 strict handstand push-ups

8 double kettlebell snatches

4 handstand push-ups

Goal

Focus on proper form and movement quality. Go unbroken for as long as proper form allows, then break into sets that will allow you to keep moving efficiently.

Movements

Ideally go the kettlebell exercises unbroken, alternatively break into a big and no more than 2 smaller sets with short rest between. On the handstand push-ups, strict and kipping, prefer to break from the start into a big and a few smaller manageable sets with minimum rest between.

Engine – Rowing

5 sets of

Row, 1000 meters @ 26 strokes/minute

Rest 2 minutes betweens sets

Pace : 2km PR pace + 7 seconds

 

Warm up

4 minutes @ easy pace using the warm up routines (1st minute only arm pulls, 2nd only leg pulls, 3rd and 4th minute regular rowing)

Rest 1 minute

300 meters @ 2km PR pace + 2 seconds

Goal

Maintain high performance in each set efficiently keeping the given pace (2km PR pace + 7 seconds) and most importantly steady 26 strokes per minute. The given stroke rate is a moderate tempo ideal for focus on technique.

Cool down

4 minutes @ very light pressure.
