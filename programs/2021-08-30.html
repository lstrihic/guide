Gymnastics Skill

Practice free standing handstand push-ups

 

Goal

Draw 2 parallel lines on the ground, one longer where your hands should touch and one shorter where your head should touch, the distance between the two lines should create a triangle base for your hands and head. Work for approximately 10 minutes.

Movements

First establish a handstand position with your hands on the long line, then drive your head smoothly to the ground near the seconds line and then kicks with your legs so that you go into a handstand position with your arms and body straight and fully extended.

Options

Kipping handstand push-ups against wall

Rest 30 seconds

4 toes to bar

3 pull-ups

2 chest to bar pull-ups

Goal

The goal is to complete each set unbroken without falling off the pull up bar. Focus on fast technical reps with smooth transitions from kipping to butterfly and then back to kipping technique that will allow you to spend as less time as possible hanging from the pull-up bar.

Movements

Ideally go the toes to bar with kipping technique, then switch to butterfly technique for the pull-ups and chest to bar pull-ups and then once again to kipping for the muscle ups so that you push as quickly as possible through each set. Alternatively go all movements with kipping technique at a steady tempo.

Options

Engine

4 sets of

AMRAP 4 Minutes of

Buy in:

3 rounds of

12 bar-facing burpees over

36 double-unders

Then

Max calories, Assault or Echo bike

Rest 2 minute between sets

 

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set, avoid pushing to hard from the start and prefer to maintain a pace that will allow you to be consistent throughout. Each set is a 4-minute time frame, first complete 3 rounds of the couplet and then, in the remaining time, go for as many reps as you can of calories on the Assault or Echo bike.

Movements

Go the bar-facing burpees over at a fast pace and the double-unders unbroken, the tempo on the burpees should make you feel uncomfortable but still allow you to breathe well and maintain the pace for all 3 rounds, then go straight to the ergometer. Work on the Assault or Echo bike at a pace that you will are able to sustain or even increase as you reach the cap of each set.
