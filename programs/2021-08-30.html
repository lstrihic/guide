Warm up

500 meters, Ski-erg @ easy pace

Then,

2 rounds of

4 thrusters

6 toes to bar

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Start each set with the couplet of thrusters and toes to bar and once you complete the couplet continue with as many meters as you can on the ski-erg until the time cap of the set.

Movements

Ideally go each round of the couplet unbroken, alternatively break the high rep round into a big and a smaller set but by reducing the time of  your transitions, and push harder as reps decrease. On the ski maintain a steady sustainable pace until the 7-minute time cap, push harder as you reach the end.

Gymnastics

For form

10 rounds of

1 legless rope climb 4,6m – 15ft

10 meters, Handstand walk

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

5 rope pull-ups

20 shoulder taps

Goal

Focus on fast rope climbs and unbroken 10-meter distances with smooth transitions between the exercises that will allow you to be efficient.

Movements

On the rope climbs make sure you use your legs by kicks or kipping in coordination with your upper body to help your arms muscle their way up the rope. Go the 10-meter distances of handstand walk fast taking solid long step with your hands.

Options

Leg-less —> regular rope climbs

Handstand walk —> 2 wall-walks

Engine

10 sets of

AMRAP 2 Minutes of

Row, 10/8 calories

7 burpees over row – lateral

Row, 10/8 calories

Max burpees over rower – lateral

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set, avoid pushing to hard from the start and prefer to maintain a pace that will allow you to be consistent throughout.

Movements

The calories are few so push fast and explosively through them each time, then go for a steady pace on the burpees, one that you estimate you can sustain not only for 1 set but for the biggest part of the session.
