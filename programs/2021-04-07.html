Power Snatch

1×1 @ 70%

1×1 @ 80%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

We are going for a short built-up. Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 3 reps < 50% and  a few sets of 2 reps < 70%.. Improve power and confidence. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid overhead position. Go for solid single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between.

—

 

Power Clean + Jerk

1×1+2 @ 65% of Max Power

1×1+2 @ 75%

3×1+2 @ 85%

 

Notes

Same as power snatch. Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 3 reps < 50% and a few sets of 2 reps < 65%.. Improve power and efficiency. Focus on a powerful pull and solid jerks. We recommend you work on technical split jerks, though choose to work on either split or push jerks depending on your needs. Go for solid single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between.

Conditioning

For time

30 meters, Single arm dumbbell overhead lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

60 single arm dumbbell hang clean and press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

30 meters, Handstand walk

60 kipping handstand push-ups

Cap: 12 minutes

 

Notes

Break the dumbbell overhead lunges and the handstand walk into 7,5 unbroken meter distances. On the single arm dumbbell overhead lunges move steadily with focus on proper reps and a solid overhead position (elbow lockout, shoulders tight), switch arm every 7,5 meters. The single arm dumbbell hang clean and presses are alternating, go with mid-air transitions and make sure you do the presses properly without leg/hip assistance. Ideally go the handstand push-ups unbroken for as long as you can breaking 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching failure and then continue with smaller manageable sets with minimum rest between. Alternatively break from the start into manageable sets that will allow you to push efficiently through the 60 reps.

*2,5 meters = 1 rep

Options

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb → 15/10kg – 35/25lb

Handstand walk → 6 wall-walks

Gymnastics

For form

5 legless rope climbs

Then,

21.18.15.12.9.6.3 reps of

Ring push-ups

30 double-unders

Then,

5 regular rope climbs

*Vest 9/6kg – 20/14lb

 

Notes

Go the legless rope climbs fast with proper kicks or kipping and descend smoothly by sliding, extending your body and jumping down. On the ring push ups prefer to break from the start into manageable sets that will allow you to move efficiently through the total volume, only attempt to go unbroken, if possible, at the low-rep sets. Go the double-unders fast and unbroken. Perform fast transitions from push-ups to double-unders, smooth transitions from double-unders to push-ups. On the regular rope climbs focus on being extra technical and always finding solid footings to squat on the rope and assist your ascents. *Work with the extra load and adapt to discomfort of the vest.

Options

Leg-less → regular rope climbs
