Warm up

2 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 overhead squats + 3 squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve strength and confidence. Go for a lift at 100%, warm up properly with an empty barbell and continue with solid single lifts until the Max. Start a new set every 60 seconds.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position.

Warm up

1500 meters, Bike erg @ easy pace

Then,

6.8.10 reps of

Burpees

Narrow squats

ring rows

Ground to overhead

Box step overs

Rng pull-ups

Goal & Movements

Pace the exercises of the first portion, before the ring muscle-ups, and push harder the 2nd portion to go for a strong finish. Go the 1st bike distance at a fast yet sustainable pace that will allow you to prime and continue strong. Move constantly and steadily on the burpee box-jumps over, alternating single leg squats and single arm devils presses at a tempo that will allow you push through the exercises efficiently with having to do big breaks for rest. Break the ring muscle-ups into a big and no more than 2-3 smaller sets with short rest between.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

*Go with chest to bar if you cant perform at least 5 unbroken reps of ring muscle-ups while fresh.

Engine – Rowing

A. Row 2500 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 16 seconds

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. Row 2500 meters @ 22 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 14 seconds

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

C. Row 2500 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 12 seconds

 

Warm up

1000 meters @ light pressure warm up routines

(0-250 meters only arm pulls | 250-500 only leg pulls | 500-1000 regular rowing).

Goal

Be consistent in each set maintaining steady strokes and pace for each 2500-meter distance. In part A, keep steady 24 strokes and try to achieve a 2km PR pace +16 seconds, in part B, 22 strokes and 2km PR pace + 14 seconds and in part C, 20 strokes and 2km PR pace + 12 seconds.

Cool down

4 minutes @ very light pressure.
