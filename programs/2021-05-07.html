Power Clean + Push Press

1×2+1 @ 60%

1×1+1 @ 70%

1×1+1 @ 75%

2×2+1 @ 80%

2×3+1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2 power cleans + 2 push presses until you reach 60%. Improve power. The lifts at 60% are 2 power cleans + 1 push press, at 80% are 2 times of 2 power cleans + 1 push press. Go with single power cleans, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets (times), no more than 15 seconds between power cleans.

Conditioning

For time

5 rounds of

60 double-unders

12 deadlifts 83/57kg – 185/125lb.

9 hang power cleans 83/57kg – 185/125lb.

6 shoulders to overhead 83/57kg – 185/125lb.

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

Try to go each round equally fast. Go the double-unders unbroken and try to be as efficient as you can with the barbell. A good way to go is by 11 deadlifts, 1 deadlift + 8 hang power cleans, 1 hang power clean + 6 shoulders to overhead. Consider that the most difficult part will be the hang power cleans. Perform fast reps and try to remain as less as possible on the hang position and avoid taxing your grips and upper body more than needed.

Options

83/57kg – 185/125lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

45 meters, Handstand walk

18 double dumbbell step-ups over 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb. , 61/50cm – 24/20”

30 strict handstand push-ups

45 meters, Shoulders rack lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Ideally, break the handstand walk and the shoulders rack lunges into 7,5-meter distances. Go the step-ups unbroken for as long as proper form allows, if you break take short to minimum rest and continue with the remaining reps. Break the handstand push ups form the start into small manageable sets with short rest between that will allow you to keep moving efficiently. Move steadily on the lunges with focus on a solid front rack position.

Options

Handstand walk —> 9 wall-walks

Strict —> kipping handstand push-ups

*Go with kipping if you can’t perform at least 10 unbroken strict handstand push-ups

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb. —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.

Engine

A1. Row 4 Minutes @ easy pace

 

A2. 3 sets of

1 Minute @ fast pace

Rest 2 minutes between

 

B. Time Trial 750 meters

Rate: Over 28 strokes/minute

Pace: 1km PR pace – 3 seconds

 

Notes

Warm up rowing 4 minutes at an easy pace and continue with 3 times of 1 minute at a really fast pace to prime your cardio. In the main session go for a 750-meters time trial. Follow the given time/distance pace and make sure you keep a high stroke rate, get the heart pumping and the blood flowing. Row 4 minutes at super light pressure as cool down.
