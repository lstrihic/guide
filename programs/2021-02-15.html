Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 75%. Improve explosiveness and stability. Conditioning

For time

10 rounds of

12 single arm dumbbell snatches 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

35 double-unders

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

Maintain a fast, steady tempo for the biggest part of the workout. 7+ rounds, and increase as you reach the end. The single arm dumbbell snatches are alternating. Go at a fast tempo, make sure you are technical and perform the arm switches in mid-air. On the double-unders go unbroken for as long as possible, when you break immediately continue without rest performing the remaining reps. The goal is to perform fast transitions, the more you are able to keep your transitions fast and equal throughout, the better your performance will be.

Options

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb → 15/10kg – 35/25lb
