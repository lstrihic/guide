Squat Snatch

1×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve technique and explosiveness. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell and a solid overhead position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Snatch High Pull

3×2 @ 90%

 

Notes

Start the snatch high pulls from the percentage you stopped the squat snatches. Improve pulling power and lifting angles. Focus on a slow pull until the barbell is over the knees and continue with a faster second pull with the barbell reaching as high as possible. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

18 pull-ups

9 thrusters 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

*Then

3 rounds of

12 chest to bar pull-ups

6 thrusters 70/48kg – 155/105lb.

*Then

3 rounds of

6 bar muscle-ups

3 thrusters 83/54kg – 185/120lb.

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

This workout consists of 3 consecutive portions, gymnastics difficulty and barbell load increases. The goal is to go each exercise unbroken. The difficulty and load increases but reps decrease so each exercise is manageable enough. Try to be technical on the pull-up bar using (preferably) butterfly or kipping technique. If you need to break prefer to break the gymnastics as these are exercises that you can reposition yourself with less effort.

*Note your split times

Options

52/37kg – 115/80lb.

61/43kg – 135/95lb

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

36 GHD sit-ups

36 single-leg squats

12 shoulders to overhead 83/54kg – 185/120lb.

42 GHD sit-ups

42 single-leg squats

8 shoulders to overhead 83/54kg – 185/120lb.

48 GHD sit-ups

48 single-leg squats

4 shoulders to overhead 83/54kg – 185/120lb.

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and steady tempo. Go the GHD sit ups unbroken for as long as possible, if you break take a short breath and continue with the rest of the reps. On the singe-unders maintain a steady tempo throughout the session. Ideally go the shoulders to overhead unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between, focus on smooth rebounding on the shoulders.

Options

83/54kg – 185/120lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Engine

A. 2 sets of 

Row, 500 meters @ 24 strokes/minute

Rest 2 minutes between

 

B. 5 sets of

Row, 1250 meters @ 24 strokes/minute

Rest 2 minutes & 30 seconds 

Pace: 2km PR pace + 12 seconds

 

Notes

Warm up by rowing 2 sets at light pressure and 24 strokes before you start the main session. In the main session try to achieve high and equal performance in each set, maintain a 2km PR pace + 12 seconds and make sure you keep 24 steady strokes. Cool down rowing 500 meters at very light pressure.
