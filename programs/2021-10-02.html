Warm up

2 sets of

2 clean pulls + 2 hang power cleans + 2 front squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat cleans until 70%.

Goal

Improve strength and confidence. Go for a lift at 100%, warm up properly with an empty barbell and continue with solid single lifts until the Max. Start a new set every 60 seconds.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

Row, 21 calories

7 thrusters 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Rest 3 minutes

3 rounds of

Row, 21 calories

7 clean and jerks 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Cap: 15/17 minutes – including rest

 

Warm up

1000 meters, Row

Then,

Build up to 61/43kg – 135/95lb. of

2 cleans & jerks + 2 thrusters

Goal & Movements

Maintain  a steady pace on the row throughout the session that will allow you to breathe. Ideally go the thrusters unbroken and perform smooth transitions in order to achieve that, alternatively break into no more than 2-3 sets but keep your mind on performing shorter transitions.  On the clean jerks go unbroken with efficient touch and go technique, if this is not possible because you realise you can not sustain technique and pace, immediately switch to fast single lifts.

Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb. —> 52/37kg – 115/80lb. —> 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

Deadlifts

EMOM 10 Minutes of

1 rep @ 90-95%

 

Warm up

3×6-8 deadlifts @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Few sets of 2 reps until 90%.

Goal

Improve strength and consistency. Further to the deadlift sessions of the previous weeks you now increase percentages up to 90-95%. Work for 10 minutes, select a weight that you feel comfortable with within 90-95%. In each minute complete 1 rep and rest the remaining time until the next minute starts.

Movements

Focus on utilising your legs on each rep so as to take much effort away from your back.

Max Effort

For time

20 meters, Handstand walk

5 leg-less rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

20 meters, Handstand walk

5 jerks 102/70kg – 225/155lb

Cap: 4 minutes

 

Warm up

Build up to 102/70kg – 225/155lb of jerk

Then,

2 sets of

13 hand release push-ups

11 ring rows

9 handstand push-ups

7 rope pull-ups

5 meters, Handstand walk

Goal & Movements

These are all technical and demanding exercises so you have to focus on finding the fastest possible pace that will lead to efficient and successfully made attempts. It all comes down to the jerks, you should plan and manage the previous exercises so that you achieve successful jerks. Go the handstand walk by 10-meters unbroken distances. On the leg-less rope climb use your legs by kick or kipping in order to assist your upper body. Lift the barbell from the ground to go for the jerks.

Options

Handstand walk —> 4 wall-walks

Leg-less rope climbs —> regular rope climbs

102/70kg – 225/155lb —> 83/61kg – 185/135lb.
