1×2+1 @ 85%

3×1+1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

1 clean pull + 2 hang power cleans + 1 presses + 2 push presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power cleans + 2 push presses until 60%.

Goal

Improve power and stability. The lifts at 60% are 2 times 2 power cleans + 2 push presses, at 80% are 2 power cleans + 1 push press.Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between power cleans.

Movements

Focus on a controlled first pull until the knees, a powerful second pull and a solid receiving position. Work on touch & go technique on the shoulders after each push press, use your hips and legs to push the barbell overhead, try to stabilise with your shoulders.

—

 

Clean High Pull

3×2 @ 95% of Max Power Clean

 

Warm up

Continue to the clean pull from the percentage that you stopped the power clean + push press.

Goal

Improve pulling strength and angles.Go with single lifts, rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell over hip height. Perform your pulls based on proper mechanics, do not force Max height attempts, let the barbell reach as high as your explosiveness allows.

Barbell Cycling

Built to a heavy unbroken round of DT

 

Warm up

Sets of 9 deadlifts + 6 hang power cleans + 3 push jerks until you reach 70/52kg – 155/115lb. then continue with 12+9+3 reps (DT).

Goal & Movements

Start from 70/52kg – 155/115lb. and add 5/2,5kg – 10/5lb. in every round until you fail to complete the round unbroken. Rest 3 minutes between attempts.
