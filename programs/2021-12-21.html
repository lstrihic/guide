1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 3 half squat cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power cleans until 60%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

First deadlift the barbell to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Avoid spending much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position.

Shoulders Press

Daily Max

Then

2×2 @ 90%

2×3 @ 85%

2×4 @ 80%

 

Warm up

3×8 shoulders presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Build up to a comfortable heavy lift for the day with sets of 5 reps < 60%, 3 reps < 75% and single lifts > 75%. Then drop down to the given percentages and work on strength, focus on smooth rebounding on the shoulders.

Movements

Focus on keeping your legs straight and stable using solely your shoulders to push the barbell overhead.

Warm up

50 calories @ bike-erg @ easy pace

Then,

2 rounds of 10 reps of

Ground to overhead 20/10kg – 45/25lb. plate

Sit-ups

Thrusters 20/10kg – 45/25lb. plate

V sit-ups

Alternating goblet lunges 20/10kg – 45/25lb. plate

Goal

Make a plan to break the exercises into manageable sets that will allow you to move steadily with minimum rest between breaks and transitions.

Movements

Break the power cleans, toes to bar and shoulders to overhead into a big and 2-3 smaller sets with minimum rest between. Go the GHD sit-ups unbroken at a steady tempo and the shoulder rack lunges by 10-meter unbroken distances. The hardest part will be the transitions between distances, make sure you rest shortly.

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb. —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.
