Push Press + Jerk    

2×2+2 @ 70% of Push Press    

2×2+1 @ 75%

2×2+1 @ 80%

1×1+2 @ 85%

1×1+2 @ 90%

1×1+1 @ 100%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2 push press + 2 jerks until you reach 70%. The lifts at 70% are 2 times of 2 push press + 2 jerks, at 85% 1 time of 1 push press + 2 jerks. Improve explosiveness (push presses) and technique (jerk). Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets.

Conditioning

AMRAP 15 Minutes of

Buy in:

3 rounds of

21 toes to bar

21 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb

Then,

3 rounds of

7 power cleans 82/52 – 180/115lb

7 bar muscle-ups

Then,

Max distance, Bike-erg

 

Notes

Start the workout with the 2 couplets and then go for as much distance as possible on the bike. In the first couplet ideally go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between, alternatively break the toes to bar into a big and a smaller set (e.g 11-10) with short to minimum rest between. In the second couplet go the power cleans with single lifts at a fast, steady tempo, ideally go the bar muscle-ups unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set (e.g 4-3). On the bike find a fast yet sustainable pace, keep in mind that the more the remaining time the steadier your pace should be, the less the remaining time the faster.

Options

82/52 – 180/115lb → 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

13 deficit handstand push-ups 20/10cm – 8/4” (plates)

Then,

42.30.18 reps of

Double kettlebell deadlifts 32/24kg – 70/53lb

Box jump-overs 60/50cm – 24/20”

Then,

30 meters, Handstand walk

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and quality of movements. Ideally go the deficit handstand push-ups unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between then go straight to the deadlifts. On the deadlifts make a plan to break into a big and no more than 2-3 smaller sets with short to minimum rest between. Go the box jump-overs at a steady manageable pace and push harder as reps decrease with each round. Break the handstand walk into 7,5 meters unbroken distances, move fast with short yet adequate rest between distances that will allow you to achieve the unbroken.

Options

Deficit → regular handstand push-ups

Handstand walk → 8 wall-walks

A2. 2 sets of

Row,  500 meters @ 20 strokes

Rest 2 minutes between sets

 

B1. Row, 3000 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B2. Row, 2000 meters @ 22 strokes/minute

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B3. Row, 1000 meters @ 24 strokes/minute

 

Pace

B1: 2km PR pace +18 seconds

B2: 2km PR pace +16 seconds

B3: 2km PR pace + 10 seconds

 

Notes

Row 4 minutes at light pressure using the warm up routines (1st minute only arm pulls, 2nd only leg pulls, 3rd and 4th minute regular rowing) and continue with 2 sets at low-stroke rate as warm up. The goal in the main session is to efficiently increase your strokes and pace with each part. In the B1 part keep steady 20 strokes and try to achieve a 2km PR pace +18 seconds, in the B2 part 22 strokes and 2km PR pace +16 seconds and in the B3 part 24 strokes and 2km PR pace + 10 seconds. Cool down by rowing 4 minutes at very light pressure.
