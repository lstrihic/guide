Conditioning

2 sets of

AMRAP 7 Minutes of

7 toes to bar

7 bar-facing burpees over

7 thrusters 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve maximum performance between sets. Ideally go each exercise unbroken at a fast pace and focus on the transitions. The more you succeed in keeping your transitions short and equal throughout the 7 minutes, the better you will perform. Alternatively, break the exercises into a big and a smaller set but make sure you keep your transitions even shorter (in any case prefer to break the toes to bars, not the thrusters).

Options

52/37kg – 115/80lb.—> 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

Engine

2 sets of (80-85%)

AMRAP 15 Minutes of

Row, 19 calories

Bike-erg, 19 calories

Rest 5 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Spend 5 minutes on each erg working at an easy pace as warm-up. Try to achieve maximum performance in each set. The goal is to find and maintain the fastest yet sustainable pace for the whole duration of 15 minutes. That doesn’t mean max effort, ideally use a heart monitor and work constantly within 80-85% of VO2MAX, perform smooth transitions between the two ergs and focus on breathing well throughout.

Options

Bike-erg → Assault bike
