Power Clean & Jerk

2×3 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

2×1 @ 95%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve power and stability. Focus on a strong pull, an explosive shoulder shrug and a solid jerk (work on split jerk technique). Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

21.15.9 reps of

Deadlifts 102/70kg – 225/155lb

Box-jumps over 60/50cm

Then,

21.15.9 reps of

Deadlifts 143/90kg – 315/200lb

Box-jumps over 76/60cm

Cap: 11 minutes

 

Notes

The descending reps give you the psychological advantage. Avoid pushing the first part of the workout too hard considering that the second part is the real challenge. Ideally go the deadlifts of the first part unbroken, alternatively break the rounds of 21, 15 reps into a big and a smaller set with short rest between and go the round of 9 reps unbroken. On the box jumps maintain a fast sustainable pace, ideally go with touch & go technique. On the second part in which the difficulty increases, break all rounds of deadlifts into small manageable sets yet make sure you take minimum rest between lifts. Go the box-jump over at a manageable pace that will allow you to move efficiently.

Options

Masters 40+

143/90kg – 315/200lb → 125/80kg – 275/175lb

Scaled

102/70kg – 225/155lb → 80/55kg – 175/120lb

143/90kg – 315/200lb → 102/70kg – 225/155lb

Front Squat

ΕΜΟΜ 10 Minutes of

Odd 1×2 @ 70%

Even 1×8 @ 60%

 

Notes

Build up with sets of 5 reps until you reach 60%. Alternate a high-rep set and a low-rep set for a total duration of 10 minutes, 5 minutes each. Complete each set within 20 seconds in each minute and rest the remaining time until the  next minute starts. Focus on solid doubles at the low-rep sets and a fast tempo at the high-rep sets.

3 sets of

AMRAP 10 Minutes of

Buy in: Ski-erg, 1000/800 meters

Then,

Row, Calories

Assault bike, Calories

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

This session consists of 3 10-minutes AMRAPs with 3 minutes rest between them. In each set start with 1000/800 meters on the ski and continue with as many reps as you can of calories on the row and the bike. As shown below go the first set with 16 calories on each erg, the second set with 18 calories on each erg and the third set with 20 calories on each erg. Your score is the total number of cals of each set. Maintain a fast steady, tempo and focus on fast transitions between ergometers.

1st set: 16 calories

2nd set: 18 calories

3rd set: 20 calories
