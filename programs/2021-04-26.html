Power Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve power and stability. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid overhead receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Snatch high pull

3×3 @ 95% of Max Power Snatch

 

Notes

Continue to the snatch high pulls from where you left the power snatches. Improve pulling strength and weightlifting angles. Start slowly and move explosively once the barbell is over the knees, bring the barbell as high as possible. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

24/18 calories, Row

12 clean and jerks 61/43kg – 135/95lb

12 box jump-overs 60/50cm – 24/20”

Cap: 13 minutes

 

Notes

Move steadily in the first 2 rounds, push the last round hard. Row at 1350+ for men / 1000+ for women and go for drop & go clean and jerks at a fast tempo with minimum rest between lifts. On the box jump-overs maintain a fast yet sustainable pace throughout. If you are comfortable enough attempt the last reps of clean & jerks touch and go.

Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb —> 52/37kg – 115/82lb.

Front Squat

2×5 @ 60%

2×3 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

5×2 @ 85%

 

Notes

Build up with sets of 4-5 reps until you reach 60%. Focus on proper form (straight back, front rack) and a steady tempo. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Gymnastics

For form

8 rounds of

3 strict ring muscle ups

7 strict handstand push-ups

12 air squats

*Vest 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Ideally go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between, alternatively don’t hesitate to break into single reps of muscle-ups or a big and a smaller set of strict handstand push-ups with short to minimum rest between. Work with the vest, wear it tight so it doesn’t move around and adapt to the extra weight and difficulty.

Options

Vest —> without vest

Strict ring muscle-ups —> kipping ring muscle-ups

Strict handstand push-ups —> pike push-ups (elevated feet)

Engine

8 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

Buy in: 100 double unders

Then

5.10.15..etc reps of

Assault Bike, calories

Burpees

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Notes

Each set is a 3-minute window. Start with 100 double-unders and continue, in the remaining time, with as many reps of calories on the assault bike and burpees until the 3-minute cap. Move explosively on the bike to create momentum and then try to sustain it efficiently, perform the  burpees steadily. In the next set start the couplet after the double-Unders from the rep you stopped in the previous set. Focus on consistency throughout the session, don’t destroy yourself too early.
