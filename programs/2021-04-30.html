Squat Snatch

1×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

5×2 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve explosiveness and stability. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Snatch Pull

5×2 @ 95% of Max Squat Snatch

 

Notes

Continue to the snatch pulls from where you left the squat snatches. Improve pulling strength. Start slowly and move explosively once the barbell is over the knees. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

Row, 2000 meters

100 single-leg squats

50 single-arm dumbbell hang clean & press 30/20kg – 65/45lb

Cap: 17 minutes

 

Notes

Row 2000 meters at a steady pace 1’50”- 1’58”/500m for men ⎮2’00”-2’10”/500m for women and continue with the single-leg squats moving at a sustainable tempo. The single arm dumbbell hang clean & presses are alternating, be technical and focus on movement mechanics to drive the dumbbell overhead and to the hang position. Try to move steadily and constantly with the dumbbells at a steady tempo since it is the final exercise, perform mid-air transitions preferably at shoulder height.

Options

Single-leg squats —> goblet squats with dumbbell

30/20kg – 65/45lb —> 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

40.30.20.10 reps of

Double kettlebell Russian swings 24/16kg – 53/35lb

20 meters, Handstand walk

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and quality of movements. Ideally go the Russian swing unbroken for as long as proper form allows with focus on movement mechanics and full hip activation. Alternatively break the high-rep sets from the start into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. Break the handstand walk into 2×10 meters unbroken distances, move fast on your hands with quick steps and focus on a proper handstand position (toes pointing upwards, straight legs, tight core and shoulders).

Options

24/16kg – 53/35lb —> 20/12kg – 45/26lb.

A2. Row 300 meters @ 2km PR pace + 2 seconds

 

B. 5 sets of

Row, 1000 meters @ 26 strokes/minute

Rest 2 minutes betweens sets

Pace : 2km PR pace + 10 seconds

 

Notes

Warm up rowing 4 minutes prior to the main session at an easy pace and continue with 300 meters at 2km PR pace + 2 seconds as warm up. In the main session try to maintain high performance (given pace) and most importantly equal strokes in each set. 26 strokes per minute is a moderate pace ideal for focus on technique. Cool down rowing at very light pressure after the session.
