Hip Power Snatch

2×2 @ 60% of Max Power Snatch

1×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

3×1 @ 80%

1×3 @ 75%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve explosiveness. Establish a solid hip height hang position and go for an explosive pull with a high shoulder shrug. Don’t forget to drive the barbell under control to the hip hang position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

—

 

Low Hang Snatch High Pull

2×3 @ 70% of Max Power Snatch

1×2 @ 80%

1×2 @ 90%

3×2 @ 95%

 

Notes

Straight after the hip power snatch drop down to 70% and continue with the given percentages. Work on explosiveness and lifting angles. The low hang position is 10cm below knee height. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Conditioning

AMRAP 20 Minutes of

4 ring muscle-ups

8 single arm dumbbell snatches 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

12 handstand push-ups

32 double-unders

 

Notes

Move at a fast yet sustainable pace for the biggest part of the workout, +-15 minutes, then push harder and go for the strong finish. Go the ring muscle ups unbroken, and the single arm dumbbell snatches at a fast steady pace with focus on dumbbell mechanics (reps are alternating). On the handstand push ups ideally go unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. Go the double-unders fast and unbroken throughout the workout. As you reach the finish increase your pace and shorten your transitions between exercises.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb. —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.

Notes

This workout consists of two consecutive portions. Complete the first portion and then wait until the timer goes 20:00 to start the next portion.
