Hang Power Snatch

3×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

 

Rest > 90 seconds between sets

 

Notes

Warm up

3 sets of

5 snatch pulls + 5 hang power snatch + 5 overhead presses + 5 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 hang power snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve power and stability on the hang position.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull, a solid overhead receiving position and smooth drive of the barbell down to the hang position.

—

 

Snatch High Pulls

2×3 @ 90%

2×2 @ 100%

 

Rest > 90 seconds between sets
Rest < 10 seconds between lifts

 

Notes

Warm up

Go to the snatch high pulls straight from the last set of hang power snatches.

Goal

Improve pulling strength. Go with single lifts.

Movements

Focus on a slow first pull and an explosive second pull once the barbell is over the knees, drive the barbell as close to the body and as high as you can.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

Ski-erg, 600 meters

27 GHD sit-ups

9 ring muscle-ups

Cap: 20 Minutes

 

Notes

Warm up

2 rounds of

300 meters, Ski

12 GHD sit-ups

6 swings on the rings

3 ring muscle-ups

Goal

Move steadily through the first 2 rounds and push the last round hard.

Movements

Go the ski at a fast yet sustainable pace throughout the session. Ideally go the other exercises unbroken with smooth transitions between them, alternatively break the ring muscle ups into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. In any case push the last round for unbroken.

3 reps @ 60%

 

Notes

Warm up

3 sets of

5 front squat + 5 good morning @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 front squats until 60%.

Goal

Improve consistency and front squat form. Complete 3 front squats in each minute and rest until the next minute starts.

Movements

Gymnastics Strength

For form

15.13.11.9.7.5 reps of

Strict ring dips

1 peg-board ascent

 

Notes

Warm up

3 rounds of

10 push-ups

5 peg board pull-ups

Goal

Focus on proper form and full range of motion.

Movements

Ideally go the strict ring dips with a big set of as many reps as proper form allow, then continue with a few smaller sets. Alternatively break from the start into manageable sets that will allow you to move efficiently. On the peg-board focus on fast efficient ascents and descents.

Options

Ring dips —> ring push-ups

*Go with ring push-ups if you can’t perform at least 9 strict ring dips while fresh

Pegboard ascent —> 5 pegboard pull-ups

Engine

4 sets of

Row, 500 meters

12 burpee box-jumps over 61/50cm – 24/20”

Ski-erg, 500 meters

12 burpee box-jumps over 61/50cm – 24/20”

Rest 3 minute between sets

 

Notes

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Focus on consistency, each next set should ideally be faster or equal to the last one.

Tempo

Row at a fast explosive pace on the ergs, start explosively and try to maintain the momentum for the whole distance 500-meter distances, then go straight to the burpee box-jumps over. Pace the first burpees of each portion, go the last burpees faster.
