LiftingA

EMOM 10 Minutes of

1 Power Snatch + 2 Overhead Squats @ 80% of Max Power Snatch

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 2 power snatches + 1 overhead squat @ 20/15kgbarbell

Then,

Sets of 1 power snatch + 1-2 overhead squats until 80%.

Goal

Improve power and overhead stability. Go with 1 complex every minute for 10 minutes, rest in the remaining time until the next minute starts.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull keeping the barbell close to your body’s vertical line and slow controlled overhead squats.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

Row, 50 calories @ easy pace

Then,

8.9.10 reps of

Plate presses 20/10kg -45/25lb.

Kipping swings

Plate bent over rows 20/10kg -45/25lb.

Kipping parallel dips

Goal & Movements

This session consists of two consecutive sequences of exercises with the seconds sequence having half the volume of the first. The goal is to move steadily on the first part pacing the row and breaking the exercises into a big and a few smaller sets and then push the seconds part harder. The difficulty of the gymnastics increases but that shouldn’t discourage you, focus on technique and push hard.
