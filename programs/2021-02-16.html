Power Snatch

3×2 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

1×3 @ 80%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 75%. Improve explosiveness. Focus on a powerful pull, an explosive shoulder shrug and a solid overhead position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Power Clean & Jerk

2×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 75%. Improve explosiveness and stability. Focus on a powerful pull, a solid reception and an explosive push/split jerk. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Thrusters

Chest to bar pull-ups

Rest 3 minutes between sets

1st set: 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

2nd set: 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

3rd set: 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Start the low-rep rounds by going both exercises unbroken and as reps increase go unbroken for as long as possible, break 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching exhaustion. Try to breath well throughout, especially on the thrusters. You want to avoid dropping the barbell on the thrusters so prefer to briefly rest, if needed, at the top position with the barbell overhead. Maintain a steadier tempo on the heavy load and a faster tempo at the lighter loads.

Gymnastics

For form

60 calories, Assault bike

30 ring muscle-ups

 

Notes

Focus on quality and technique. Maintain a steady tempo on the Assault bike, 65+ for men / 59+ for women that will allow you to prime for the ring muscle ups. On the ring muscle ups go with a first set of more than 50% of your Max-rep set when fresh and continue with sets of 3-5 reps until you complete the total reps. Consider that the volume is significantly big so make sure you don’t force a max effort attempt too soon, push harder only once you have reached near the finish. The more technical you are, the more comfortably and efficiently you will move on the rings.

Options

Assault bike → bike-erg or row

30 → 20* → 10** reps of

Ring muscle-ups → chest to rings pull-ups

*Go with 20 reps if your Max set is 4-8 reps

**Go with 10 reps if your Max set is less than 4 reps

Barbell Cycling – Power Clusters

10 reps @ 65% of Max Power Clean

8 reps @ 70%

6 reps @ 75%

4 reps @ 80%

2 reps @ 85%

 

Notes

Start with touch & go sets and if you find that you can’t sustain proper t&g technique switch to single lifts. The goal is to move efficiently with the barbell, focus on a powerful push press after the clean and good drive and rebounding of the barbell on the ground in the touch & go sets, make sure you take minimum rest between lifts if/when you go with singles. Consider this session slightly as a For time workout, work on the weight increases and make sure you load the bar fast and effectively, use the time to rest but don’t overdo it, take just as much rest as needed.

A2. 5 sets of

Run, 800 meters @ 3:55-4:45/km

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Run 20 minutes at an easy pace and continue with some running drills (2 x 10 meters; butt kicks, power skips, high knees, side shuffles – 10 each arm & leg swings …etc). Each set is an 800-meter sprint. Select a pace within the given time frame that you can efficiently sustain in each set and throughout the session. Cool down with a 5 minute jog.
