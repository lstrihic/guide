Warm up

2 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 3 hang squat cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat cleans & jerk until 50%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between squat cleans.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and solid receiving positions. Work and build confidence on split jerks since this is the most appropriate jerk technique when it comes to heavy weights.

3 rounds of

Warm up

Then,

7.9.11 reps of

Kipping swings

Thrusters 20/15kg empty barbell

Knees to chest

Power cleans 20/15kg empty barbell

Kipping pull-ups

Goal & Movements

Ideally, go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between them that will allow you to move efficiently. Alternatively, in the 1st and 2nd round break the toes to bar into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between and go the thrusters unbroken. On the chest to bar pull-up/power clean portion try to go unbroken, if this is not possible break the 1st & 2nd round into a big and a smaller set and push the last round as hard as you can.

Max Effort

For time

30 calories, Assault bike

8 single arm dumbbell squat snatches 30/22,5kg – 70/50lb

Cap: 3 minutes

 

Warm up

30 calories, Assault bike @ easy pace

Then,

Practice single arm dumbbell squat snatches

Then,

3 sets of

6/4 calories, Assault bike @ fast pace

2 single arm dumbbell squat snatches

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal & Movements

Start fast and explosively on the assault bike pushing swiftly through the first calories and then continue by maintaining a fast momentum until you complete the total calories. Then go straight to the single arm dumbbell squat snatches, take a breath and start without rushing, try to move efficiently and technically at a steady tempo with alternating reps. Switch arms mid-air after each rep.
