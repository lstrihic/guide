Squat Snatch

1×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×2 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 overhead squats + 3 squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2-3 squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and confidence. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position.

—

 

Squat Clean + Jerk

1×3+2 @ 60%

2×2+2 @ 65%

2×2+1 @ 70%

1×2+1 @ 80%

1×1+1 @ 85%

3×1+1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 3 hang squat cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat cleans + 2 jerks until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. The lifts at 60% are 3 squat cleans + 2 jerks, at 65% are 2 times of 2 squat cleans + 2 jerks. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between squat cleans.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and solid receiving positions. Work and build confidence on split jerks since this is the most appropriate jerk technique when it comes to heavy weights.

C. AMRAP 5 Minutes of

1.3.5..etc reps of

Wall-walks

3 deadlifts 143/91kg – 315/200lb.

21 double-unders

 

Warm up

Build up to 143/91kg – 315/200lb. of deadlift

Then,

2 rounds of

30 double-unders

10 hand release push-ups

15 double-unders

2 wall-walks

Goal & Movements

Move steadily on the wall-walks at a sustainable tempo with focus on full range of motion and proper reps (use the official guidelines for proper reps). Ideally, go the deadlifts unbroken with touch & go technique at a steady tempo, alternatively, break into a big and a smaller set that will allow you to keep moving efficiently through rounds but make sure you make even faster transitions. Go the double-unders fast and unbroken throughout.

Warm up

Gymnastics Stamina

A. AMRAP 7 Minutes of

7 ring muscle-ups

14 GHD sit-ups

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. AMRAP 5 Minutes of

5 ring muscle-ups

12 GHD sit-ups

 

Rest 3 Minute

 

B. AMRAP 3 Minutes of

3 ring muscle-ups

10 GHD sit-ups

 

Warm up

2-3 rounds of

8 kipping ring dips

8 sit-ups

4 kipping ring pull-ups

4 V-ups

Goal & Movements

Move steadily for the biggest part of each AMRAP, then start pushing harder by going unbroken and shortening the transitions between exercises. As duration of each part decreases push harder and focus more on faster transitions. Ideally, go each exercise unbroken (steady tempo on GHD sit-ups), alternatively, break the ring muscle-ups into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> ring dips
