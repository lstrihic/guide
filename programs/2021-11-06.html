Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat

2×2+1 @ 60% of Max Snatch Balance

2×2+1 @ 70%

1×2+1 @ 75%

1×1+2 @ 80%

1×1+2 @ 85%

3×1+2 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

2 back squats + 2 back presses + 2 snatch balances + 2 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 snatch balances + 1 overhead squat until you reach 60%.

Goal

The lifts at 60% are 2 sets of 2 reps of 2 snatch balances + 1 overhead squat, at 80% are 1 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between reps.

Movements

Use racks or high blocks. Focus on an explosive drive under the barbell and a solid overhead position.

—

 

Overhead Squat

5 Reps Max

 

Warm up

Continue with sets of 3 reps < 85% and a few sets of 5 reps > 85%. Increase weight with a few sets until you reach the heaviest load with which you can successfully complete 5 unbroken reps.

Goal

Improve stability and confidence. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on solid overhead position and controlled squats at a steady tempo.

Strongman

EMOM 10 Minutes of

Odd: Max distance, Sled Push 90/60kg – 200/135lb.

Even: Rest

 

Warm up

2×20-meter distances @ empty sled

Goal

Work by 20 or 10-meter distances depending on the available space. Move constantly in each odd minute covering as much distance as you can and rest in each even minute.

Movements

Progressively increase weight on the sled as you work in 5-meter distances. Once you reach the given load, start the EMOM pushing the sled through the marked distances (20 or 10 meters) back and forth.

Max Effort

For time

21.15.9 reps of

Double dumbbell hang power cleans 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

Handstand push-ups

Cap: 4 minutes

 

Warm up

9.6.3

Ground to overhead with plate 10/5kg – 25/15lb.

Hand release push-ups

Goal & Movements

The descending reps give you the psychological advantage. Go the double dumbbell hang power cleans unbroken for as long as you can at a steady sustainable pace, if you break catch a short breath and immediately continue with the remaining reps. On the handstand push-ups focus on proper kipping technique, ideally, go each round fast and unbroken, alternatively, break from the start into a big and smaller set with minimum rest between.
