Hang power snatch

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

2×2 @ 75%

2×2 @ 80%

 

Notes

Improve pulling power and hang position technique. The starting position is with the barbell about 10cm above the knees. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid overhead position. Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 65%. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets.

1×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

2×2 @ 90%

 

Notes

Work on explosiveness and confidence. Focus on a fast drive under the bar and a solid deep receiving position. Start lifting at a comfortable weight lighter than 65% and continue with the given percentages. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

STELLA

For time

33.27.21.15.9 reps of

Row, calories

Handstand push-ups

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

This is our benchmark workout STELLA. It’s a pull-push format with high volume on each movement. What you should do is maintain a steady pace on the row (1500cal/hr for men and 1200cal/hr for women) and break the handstand push-ups smartly (e.g. 15.10.8 – 12.9.6 – 12.9). The last 2 sets is where you give all you got, sprinting the row and attempting unbroken HSPUs. Goal: finishing in the time cap.

Engine

6 sets of

AMRAP 4 Minutes of

Ski-erg, 20/15 calories

20 bar-facing burpees over

Assault bike, 20/15 calories

Max reps, Bar-facing burpees over

Rest 1 Minute between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high and equal performance in each set. Move fast on the triplet: Push efficiently on the Ski-erg and Assault bike, start explosively gaining the first calories and then maintain the momentum going as much as you can, go the burpees at a fast yet sustainable pace. Perform fast transitions between exercises and go straight to the Max-rep attempt. On the max-rep set move at a steady pace from first until the last second of the set, the more remaining time the steadier the pace the less remaining time the faster the pace.

Options

Ski-erg → row

Assault bike → 80 double-unders
