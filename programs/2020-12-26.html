Conditioning

Open 20.0a

For time

10 rounds of

3 power snatches 70/47,5kg – 155/105lb.

35 double-unders

 

Into

 

Open 20.0b

1 Rep Max of

Squat snatch

Cap: 15 Minutes for both 20.0a & 20.0b

 

Notes

Last week we tested lightweight endurance, this week is about heavyweight stamina.

This Open style workout consists of 2 parts. You are given 15 minutes in which you have to complete 10 rounds of 3 power snatches + 35 double-unders and in the remaining time find a 1 Rep Max Squat Snatch. You have to perform your best in both parts. Ideally, go the couplet with unbroken snatches and double-unders, focus on the transitions, the better you manage to keep your rounds fast and equal the better you will perform. Alternatively, go with single lifts with minimum rest between them. On the squat snatch have in mind that you should get some rest before you start lifting so the faster you finish part 1 the better it is even from that perspective. The starting load will be decided during the warm up, the constant power snatching of part 1 may affect your squat snatch efficiency so we recommend that you start at 80-85% of your Pr, secure a lift and continue from then on as you feel.

Engine

10 rounds of

Bike-erg, 1000m @ easy to moderate

Bike-erg, 1000m @ moderate to fast

 

Notes

Work for 1000 meters on the bike at a progressively increasing pace as warm-up. Each round is a distance of 2000 meters, try to be consistent throughout the 10 rounds. In each round: Go the first 1000 meters at a pace that will allow you to breath well and regulate your cardio back to almost normal (work high in your aerobic zone but still within it); In the second 1000 meters progressively increase to a fast challenging pace of 1:45-1:55/1000m for men | 1:57-2:07 for women.

Options

Bike-erg → Ski-erg, 500 meters
