Deadlift

Daily Max

Then,

3×3 @ 75%

 

Notes

Reach a heavy lift for the day, then drop down to 75% and perform the given percentages. Focus on proper form (straight back, proper use and range of motion on the legs). Build up with sets of 4 reps < 60%, 2 reps < 80% and 1 rep >80%. On the percentages go with single lifts, rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Push press

Daily Max

Then

3×3 @ 85%

 

Notes

Reach a heavy lift for the day, then drop down to 85% and perform the given percentages. Use your legs to explosively push the barbell overhead. Build up with sets of 4 reps < 60%, 3 reps < 70%, 2 reps < 85% and 1 rep > 85%. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets.

Into

 

21.0b

6 Minutes to find

1 Rep Max of

Clean and Jerk

 

Notes

This workout consists of two parts. In each part you have to achieve maximum performance. The challenge in the AMRAP is to move fast and constantly, go each exercise unbroken and the box jumps at a fast steady pace. Consider your performance will depend on the transitions, the more you succeed in keeping short transitions between exercises the better your score will be. Next you will be given 6 minutes to find a 1rep max. Spend about 2 minutes to rest and then start your lifts at about 87% of your Max, you will have time for 3 or 4 lifts at best. So make sure there are successful made.
