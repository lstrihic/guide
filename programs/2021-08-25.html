Hang Power Snatch + Power Snatch

Daily Max

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Go with sets of 2 hang power snatches + 2 power snatches < 75% and continue with a few sets of 1 hang power snatch + 1 power snatch until your reach the heavy lift of the day. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

First deadlift the barbell up to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Once you complete the hang power snatch drive the barbell under control back to the ground and immediately go for a touch and go power snatch.

Warm up

Clean & jerk starting with an empty barbell and build up to 83/61kg – 185/135lb.

Then,

200 meters, Run

Then,

12 GHD sit-ups

5 clean & jerks 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Goal

This session has a total duration of 30 minutes and you are given 3 consecutive 10-minute windows. In each window you have to run 600 meters, then complete 3 rounds of a couplet of GHD Sit-ups

and clean & jerks and once you complete both of them rest until the next 10-minute window starts.

Movements

Run at a steady manageable pace that will allow you to perform the couplet efficiently. Move steadily on the GHD sit-ups, on the clean & jerk ideally start each round with a touch & go set and then switch to fast single lifts. Alternatively go with single lifts from the start and push the last reps of the last round for a touch and go set.

Engine

A. AMRAP 20 Minutes of

Assault or Echo bike, Calories

Rest 10 minutes

 

B. For time

Calories performed @ A part

 

Goal

In the first portion of the session complete as many calories on the assault bike as you can in 20 minutes. Once you do this, take a 10-minute rest and then try to complete the total reps you achieved in the first portion as fast as you can.

Movements

Push at a steady pace through the first portion 65+ for men |58+ for women RPM and try to sustain it or even increase it as you reach the finish, in the second portion notice your progress as you push through the cals and try to complete them in less than 20 minutes.
