Warm up

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Snatch Pull

3×3 @ 95% of Max Squat Snatch

Warm up

Continue from the percentage you left the squat snatch.

Goal

Work on strength. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a slow pull with proper angles and finish with an explosive shoulder shrug.

Conditioning

AMRAP 13 Minutes of

1 rope climb 4,6m – 15ft.

4 devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

12 calories, Ski-erg

Warm up

16 calories,  Ski-erg

8 rope pull-ups

4 devils press

2 rope climbs

Goal

Move steadily through the biggest part of the workout, 8 minutes, then start pushing harder to go for the strong finish.

Movements

Move fast and efficiently on the rope with solid footings that will assist your upper body, descent smoothly. On the devils presses go with a steady tempo with focus on breathing well. Maintain a fast yet sustainable pace on the ski for the whole 13-minute volume.

Options

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb. —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.
