Power Snatch

1×2 @ 55%

1×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 65%

1×1 @ 70%

2×1 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 55%. Improve power and lifting angles. Focus on a powerful pull and proper course of the bar from the ground to the overhead position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

1×2 @ 60%

1×1 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

3×1 @ 80%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 50%. Improve power and lifting angles. Focus on a powerful pull and proper course of the bar from the ground to the front rack. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Engine

A. Row 1000m

*using the warm up routines. Change every 250 meters

 

B. Main session

B1. Row 2500 meters @ 24 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 16 seconds

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B2. Row 2500 meters @ 22 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 14 seconds

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B3. Row 2500 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 12 seconds

 

Notes

Row 1000 meters at light pressure using the warm up routines as warm up (0-250 meters only arm pulls, 250-500 only leg pulls, 500-1000 regular rowing). The goal in the main session is to be consistent in each set maintaining steady strokes and pace for each 2500-meter distance. In part B1 keep steady 24 strokes and try to achieve a 2km PR pace +16 seconds, in the B2 part 22 strokes and 2km PR pace + 14 seconds and in the B3 part 20 strokes and 2km PR pace + 12 seconds. Cool down by rowing 4 minutes at very light pressure.
