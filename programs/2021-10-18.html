Hang Squat Snatch

2×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

4×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

2 snatch pulls + 2 hang power snatches + 2 hang squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and confidence. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a solid pull until the barbell is at hip height, then drive the barbell to a position over the knees, pull explosively with the barbel close to your body (proper lifting angles) and focus on a fast drive into a deep receiving position.

—

 

Snatch Pull from blocks

2×3 @ 90% of Max Hang Squat Snatch

2×2 @ 95%

2×1 @ 100%

 

Warm up

Continue to the snatch pull from blocks at the percentage that you stopped the hang squat snatch.

Goal

Improve pulling power and lifting angles. The starting position of the barbell should be approximately 10cm below the knees.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull until the barbell is at hip height and finish with an explosive shoulder shrug.

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each part. The volume in each next part decreases but the weight and the difficulty increase so make sure you put equal effort throughout the session.

Movements

Ideally go the deadlifts and the handstand push-ups unbroken and each handstand walk by 2×7,5 unbroken distances. Alternatively, break the exercise of the 1st & 2nd round of each part into a big and no more than 2-3 smaller sets and push hard to go the 3rd round unbroken.

2×2 @ 80%

1×2 @ 85%

1×2 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

1×1 @ 97%

1×6 @ 70%

 

Warm up

Sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 70%.

Goal

Improve strength and confidence. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets. In the last set improve consistency, try to breathe well and keep a steady tempo.

Movements

Focus on a straight back, a solid front rack position and slow controlled squats.

Gymnastics Skill

Practice handstand walk – Ramp

 

Goal & Movements

Work for 10-15 minutes on handstand walk on a ramp. Mark a 10-meter handstand walk distance and place the official ramp in the middle (one side ramp, other side stairs). Alternatively use plates to create a pyramid to walk over back and forth.

Options

Practice free handstand walk

Warm up

Shoulder mobility

Then,

2 rounds of

10 push ups

8 ring pull-ups

6 kipping ring dips

Goal & Movements

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Ideally go each round unbroken with adequate rest between rounds in order to be efficient. If this is not possible break before the last strict muscle up, then perform the muscle-up and all the ring dips unbroken. On the second part focus on technique and and try to move smoothly and unbroken.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> assisted ring muscle-ups (coach or partner)

Buy in:

Row, 1500/1300 meters

Then

4.8.12..etc reps of

Assault bike, Calories

Bar-facing burpees over

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Goal & pace

Try to achieve high performance in both parts. Row at a steady pace throughout the session, < 1’55”/500m for men | 2’05”/500m for women. On the couplet be explosive on the bike, push fast through the low-cal rounds. As cals increase start explosively and then try to maintain the momentum until you complete all the cals. Go straight to the burpees and try to maintain a sustainable pace throughout that will allow you to breathe well.
