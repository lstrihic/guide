Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve power and hang position stability. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid reception. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Conditioning – Friendly Fran

3 rounds of

21 thrusters 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

21 chest to bar pull-ups

Cap: 10 Minutes

 

Notes

Go the first 2 rounds fast yet steadily and give everything you got in the last round. The volume of each exercise is demanding. Ideally go each exercise unbroken with fast transitions, if you feel the need to break prefer to swiftly break the chest to bar pull-ups as it is easier and less time consuming to reposition. Alternatively, plan your breaks, if you are good at gymnastics push the chest to bar pull-ups for unbroken, if you are strong with the barbell, push the thrusters for unbroken, in both cases break the other exercise into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between.

Options

52/37kg – 115/80lb. —> 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

Gymnastics Strength

For form

10 sets of

3 strict full deficit handstand push-ups (plates)

3 strict weighted pull-ups *ahap

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Go each exercise unbroken, try to maintain a steady tempo throughout the session with smooth transitions between them. Use a belt and a kettlebell on the pull-ups. Select a kettlebell that will be heavy but still allow you to complete each round of pull-ups unbroken.

Options

Strict full deficit handstand push-ups —> strict regular handstand push-ups

Strict weighted pull-ups —> strict bodyweight chest to bar pul-ups

Bike-erg, 2km

Ski-erg, 1km

Notes

There are two sequences of the same ergs yet the volume of the second one is half that of the first. Pace the first part at 75-80% of max effort and push the second and smaller one harder, 85-90% of max effort. Try to breathe well throughout the session, perform smooth transitions and focus on movement technique and mechanics, the more technical you are the better you will breathe and the smoother you will push through the total volume.

Options

Bike-erg —> air-bike

Ski-erg —> run
