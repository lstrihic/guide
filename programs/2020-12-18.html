Notes

Improve pulling power and hang snatch technique. Focus on a solid hang position and an explosive pull. Go either with hang power snatches or hang squat snatches, the challenge is to be able to switch from power to squat snatches efficiently if you need to. Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2 power snatches + 1 hang power snatch + 1 hang squat snatch until you reach 55%. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets.

Strength/Gymnastics

“Naughty DT”

For form

5 rounds of

12 deadlifts

9 hang power cleans

6 shoulders to overhead

15 meters, Handstand walk

70/50kg – 155/115lb

 

Notes

The goal is to push through the barbell exercises efficiently and then move fast and properly on the handstand walk. Ideally go with 11 deadlifts, 1 deadlift + 9 hang power cleans + 6 shoulders to overhead and then attempt an unbroken 15-meter handstand walk. Alternatively break into 11 deadlifts, 1 deadlift + 8 hang power cleans, 1 hang power clean + 6 shoulders to overhead and go the handstand walk with 7,5 meter unbroken distances. Rest as needed between exercises.

Options

70/50kg – 155/115lb → 61/43kg – 135/95lb

15 → 10 → 7,5 meters of

Handstand walk → 2 wall-walks

*Go with wall-walks if you can’t perform at least 7,5 unbroken meters of handstand walk while fresh

Engine

A1. Row 1000 meters as a warm-up

 

Rest 4 minutes

 

A2. 2 sets of

125 meters @ high rate

Rest 1 minute between sets  

 

B. Main session

7 sets of

500 meters @ free rate

Rest 1:30 between sets

Pace: 2km PR pace + 4 seconds

 

Notes

Row 1000 meters at low pressure and perform 2 high-intensity sets, 26+ stroke/minute, as priming for the main session. In the main session try to achieve high and equal performance throughout. Focus on achieving a 2km PR pace + 4 seconds in each set. Strokes are free, if you choose to go with fewer strokes you rely on power, if you choose to go with more strokes you rely on cardio. Row 5 minutes at very low pressure as cool-down.
