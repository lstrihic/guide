2 deadlifts 165/102kg – 365/225lb.

8 box-jumps 76/61cm – 30/24”

B. AMRAP 8 Minutes of

4 deadlifts 143/90kg – 315/200lb.

8 strict handstand push-ups

12 chest to bar pull-ups

16 box-jumps 61/50cm – 24/20”

 

Warm up

1000 meters, Bike-erg @ easy pace

Then,

Build up to 165/102kg – 365/225lb. of deadlift

Then,

2 rounds of

14 push ups

12 kipping swings

10 kettlebell deadlifts 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

8 box step-ups

6 parallel dips

4 kipping pull-ups

Goal & Movements

Try to achieve high performance in each portion. Move steadily for the biggest part of each AMRAP, 5+ minutes, then start pushing harder to go for a strong finish. Ideally, go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between that will allow you to keep moving efficiently. Alternatively, break the handstand push ups and/or chest to bar pull-ups into manageable sets with minimum rest between. Shorten your transitions as you reach the finish.

Options

Strict deficit hspu 15/10cm – 6/4” —> regular strict hspu

Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

B. 143/90kg – 315/200lb. —> 125/83kg – 275/185lb.

Strict hspu —> kipping hspu

Chest to bar pull-ups —> pull-ups

*Scale down if u cant perform at least one unbroken set of each exercise while fresh.

Sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 80%.

Goal

Improve confidence. Go with 1 heavy lift every minute for 10 minutes (10 reps total). Start from 85% and if you feel confident enough progressively increase to 90%

Movements

Gymnastics Skill

Practice free handstand push-ups

 

Goal & Movements

Work for 10-15 minutes on free handstand push-ups. Draw 2 parallel lines on the floor, place your hands on the first line and your head on the other, your hands and head should form a triangle that will allow you to establish balance. First find balance and then perform the handstand push-ups returning each time to the same position.
