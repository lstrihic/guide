Jerk

Daily Max

Then

3×1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Reach a heavy jerk for the day. Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 3-4 reps < 60%, 2 reps < 85% and single reps > 85%. Then drop down to 85% and work on explosiveness and stability. Ideally go with split jerks, but if you are not that comfortable with the technique yet, prefer to go with power jerks. Use racks or high blocks and rest 90 seconds between sets.

Conditioning

For time

75 wall-ball shots 9/6kg – 20/14lb

150 double-unders

30 meters, Handstand walk

150 double-unders

60/45 calories, Row

150 double unders

30 meters, Handstand walk

150 double-unders

75 wall-ball shots 9/6kg – 20/14lb

 

Notes

This workout contains 2 tempos, move steadily until you complete the calories on the rower and then push harder to go for the strong finish. Break the first wall-ball shots into a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with short to minimum rest between and go the double-unders unbroken. Break the handstand walk into 10-meter distances and row at a steady pace 1300+ for men /1050+ for women. Push the last wall-ball shots really hard going unbroken for as long as possible.

Options

Handstand walk → 5 wall-walks

Bench Press

Daily Max

Then,

3×8 @ 75%

 

Notes

Reach a heavy bench press for the day. Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 5 reps < 60%, 2-3 reps < 85% and single reps > 85%. Then drop down to 75% and work on consistency and muscle hypertrophy.  Rest 2 minutes between the 8-rep sets.

Max Effort

For time

20/15 calories, Assault bike

15 bar muscle-ups

10 overhead squats 75/52kg – 165/115lb

 

Notes

Move fast from the start. Push explosively through the calories on the assault bike and go the bar muscle-ups unbroken for as long as possible, break 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching muscle failure. Take a breath before you start with the overhead squats and go for a solid unbroken set.

Options

15 → 10 → 5 reps of

Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups

*Choose a number of reps that you can easily perform unbroken while fresh.

Notes

Run 2000 meters (warm-up) and 400 meters (cool down) at a comfortable pace of your choice. The combination of the prescribed paces and the 6000 meter distance is demanding, so select manageable paces within the given time frames in order to be able to progressively increase and successfully reach the intended max effort pace finish.
