Power Clean + Jerk

2×2+2 @ 70% of Max Power Clean

2×2+1 @ 75%

2×2+1 @ 80%

2×2+2 @ 85%

2×2+1 @ 90%


Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then, 

Sets of 2 power cleans + 2 jerks until 70%.


Goal

Improve power and stability. The lifts at 70% are 2 times of 2 power cleans + 2 jerks. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between power cleans.


Movements

Focus on a controlled first pull until the knees, a powerful second pull and solid receiving positions. Work and build confidence on split jerks since this is the most appropriate jerk technique when it comes to heavy weights.

—

 

Shoulder Press 

5×5 @ 70%


Warm up

3×5 shoulder presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Then, 

Sets of 2-3 shoulder presses until 70%.


Goal

Improve pushing strength. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.


Movements

Focus on a strong push using only your shoulder and arms, hips and legs should remain straight and still.

Conditioning

For time

Row, 66 calories

44 burpees over rower 

22 clean and jerks 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Cap: 15 minutes 

 

Warm up

20 calories, Row

15 burpees over rower

Goal & Movements

Go the row and the burpees over rower at a fast yet sustainable pace. On the clean & jerks start with 1-2 touch and go sets and then continue with single lifts with short to minimum rest between. Ideally finish strong with a touch and go set of clean and jerks.


Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb. —> 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

Strength/Gymnastics

AMRAP 11 Minutes of

3 strict ring muscle-ups

6 double kettlebell front squats 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

9 box-jumps 76/61cm – 30/24”


Warm up

3 strict ring pull-ups

6 double kettlebell front squats

3 strict ring dips

3 box-jumps

1 strict ring muscle-up


Goal

Focus on full range of motion and kettlebell mechanics. 


Movements

The most difficult part is the strict ring muscle-ups, take a breath before you start and try to go unbroken with focus on quality of movement. Go the double kettlebell front squats and the box-jumps unbroken with focus on breathing well throughout.


Options

Strict ring muscle-ups —> strict ring pull-ups

24/16kg – 53/35lb. —> 20/12kg – 44/26lb.

Engine

A. Row, 3000 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

Rest 3 minutes

B. Row, 2000 meters @ 22 strokes/minute

Rest 3 minutes 

C. Row, 1000 meters @ 24 strokes/minute 


Pace

A: 2km PR pace + 16 seconds

B: 2km PR pace + 12 seconds

C: 2km PR pace + 8 seconds


Warm up 

Row 4 minutes using the warm up routines

(1st minute only arm pulls, 2nd only leg pulls, 3rd and 4th minute regular rowing)

then

2 sets of

Row,  500 meters @ 20 strokes

Rest 2 minutes between sets  


Goal

The goal in the main session is to efficiently increase your strokes and pace with each part. In the B1 part keep steady 20 strokes and try to achieve a 2km PR pace +16 seconds, in the B2 part 22 strokes and 2km PR pace +12 seconds and in the B3 part 24 strokes and 2km PR pace + 8 seconds.


Cool down

Row 4 minutes at very light pressure.
