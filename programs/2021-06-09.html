Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat

3×1+3 @ 70% of Max Snatch Balance

3×1+2 @ 80%

3×1+1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2 snatch balances + 2 overhead squats until you reach 70%. The lifts at 70% are 3 times of 1 snatch balance + 3 overhead squats. Work on stability. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell and slow squats with a solid overhead position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

—

 

Power Snatch

2×2 @ 50%

2×2 @ 60%

1×1 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

3×1 @ 80%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 50%. Work on explosiveness. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

150 double-unders

21 deadlifts 102/70kg – 225/155lb.

9 ring muscle-ups

Cap: 16 minutes

 

Notes

Slightly pace the 1st and 2nd round and push the last round really hard. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take minimum rest and immediately continue with the remaining reps. Ideally, go each exercise unbroken with a possible fast break on the deadlifts and fast transitions between exercises. If this is not possible, break from the start each round of either deadlifts or ring muscle-ups into no more than 2-3 manageable sets with minimum rest between depending on which exercise you struggle most. In any case push the last round for unbroken.

Options

102/70kg – 225/155lb. —> 83/61kg – 180/135lb.

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

*Go with chest to bar pull-ups if you can’t perform at least 5 unbroken ring muscle-ups while fresh.

Back Squat

1×2 @ 50%

1×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

5×1 @ 80% (EMOM)

1×5 @ 65% fast pace

 

Notes

Build up with set of 3 reps until you reach 50%. From then on continue in EMOM style, perform a set in every minute for 10 minutes, go with a set of 2 reps for 5 consecutive minutes and a single rep for another consecutive 5 minutes. Rest for 1 minute and finish the session with a fast set of 5 reps at 65%.

Gymnastics Skill

5 sets of

Max time, Handstand hold into 1,5×1,5m – 5x5ft. box

Rest no more than 90 seconds between attempts

 

Notes

Practice handstand hold. Go smoothly and under control into a handstand position and then try to maintain it for as long as you can with focus on a straight body with toes pointing upwards, a tight midline and shoulder-arms tight and extended. The game winner is the fingers, if you achieve the previous position then it’s the fingers that will assist you to balance efficiently.

Gymnastics Strength

5 Reps Max of

Weighted strict pull-ups

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Use a lifting belt and a kettlebell < 24kg-53lb for men / 16kg-35lb for women. Perform the pull-ups with any grip position you like, switch grips in each set if you find that needed.

Engine

8 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

40 double-unders

10/8 calories, Assault bike

12 bar-facing burpees over

Max calories, Assault bike

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Move fast through the triplet. Go the double-unders unbroken, push explosively through the calories on the assault bike and go straight to the bar-facing burpees over. Make sure you pace the burpees at a steady sustainable tempo throughout the session. On the max calories start powerfully and then maintain the momentum for as long as you can. Don’t push each set too hard, aim at a consistent performance.
