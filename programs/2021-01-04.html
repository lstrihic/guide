Hang Power Snatch

3×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 75%

3×1 @ 80%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve technique and explosiveness. Focus on a solid hang position and an explosive pull with proper angles. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

—

 

Snatch High Pulls

2×5 @ 80%

2×3 @ 90%

2×2 @ 100%

 

Notes

Continue from where you left the hang power snatches. Improve pulling power and angles. Pull high with the barbell on a vertical trajectory and your elbows pointing upwards. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 5 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

10 rounds of

7 power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

11 chest to bar pull-ups

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

The goal is to move steadily and continuously throughout the 10 rounds. Ideally go both exercises unbroken (touch & go snatches), alternatively break an exercise into a big and a smaller set. Transitions make the difference, push steadily through the biggest part of the workout moving fast and equally between exercises and rounds. As you reach the end push harder to go unbroken and shorten your transitions to go for the strong finish. If you break make sure you move from exercise to exercise even faster.

Front squat

ΕΜΟΜ 10 Minutes

3 reps @ 70%

 

Notes

Build up with sets of 3-4 reps < 60% and 2 reps until you reach 70%. Improve stamina and consistency. Focus on a solid tempo and a powerful return from the bottom position to the standing position. You should complete the squats within 20 seconds in each minute.

Gymnastics

Ring muscle-ups

Max unbroken reps

Rest 3 minutes

Max unbroken reps

Rest 3 minutes

Max unbroken reps

 

Notes

This workout consists of 3 Max-rep attempts. Make sure you put maximum effort in each portion. This doesn’t mean you should not be technical. The more technically you perform the exercises the more you will continue moving efficiently on the rings and the fresher you will be for each next attempt. Proper swings and kipping dips is a must.

Options

Ring muscle-ups → chest to rings pull-ups

*Choose the option if you can’t perform at least 5 unbroken reps while fresh.
