Power Snatch

Daily Max

Then

3×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

2 snatch high pulls + 3 power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Build up to a heavy lift for the day with sets of 3 reps < 70%, 2 reps < 82% and single reps > 82%. Your heaviest lift should be comfortable, avoid forcing a heavy attempt too much. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts. Once you find the daily max drop down to 90% and work on consistency on heavy lifts.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull maintaining the barbell close to your body and a solid overhead receiving position.
