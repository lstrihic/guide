Conditioning

For time

10 rounds of

12 single arm dumbbell snatches 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

35 double-unders

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Goal

The goal is to perform fast transitions, the more you are able to keep your transitions fast and equal throughout, the better your performance will be. Maintain a fast, steady tempo for the biggest part of the workout. 7+ rounds, and increase as you reach the end.

Movements

The single arm dumbbell snatches are alternating. Go at a fast tempo, make sure you are technical and perform the arm switches in mid-air. On the double-unders go unbroken for as long as possible, when you break immediately continue without rest performing the remaining reps.

Options

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb → 15/10kg – 35/25lb
