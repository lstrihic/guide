Power Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

1×2 @ 85%

5×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

4 snatch pulls + 3 hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve confidence and stability. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull and a steady receiving position. Keep the barbell close to your body’s vertical line. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees, shoulders tight and elbows locked out.

—

 

Snatch High Pull

3×2 @ 95% of Max Power Snatch

 

Warm up

Continue to the snatch high pull from the percentage that you left the power snatch.

Goal

Improve technique and weightlifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled pull until the barbell is over the knees, then increase speed and drive the barbell as high as possible pulling your elbow high and keeping your chest out.

2 reps @ 80%

 

Warm up

Sets 2-3 reps until you reach 80%.

Goal

Improve form and consistency. Go with a set of 2 repetitions every minute for 10 minutes (20 reps total).

Movements

Focus on a straight back, slow controlled descents and fast explosive ascents at a steady tempo.

Gymnastics Skill

Practice handstand walk ramp

 

Goal & Movements

Work for 10-15 minutes on handstand walk over ramp. Mark a 10-meter distance and place an official handstand walk or other (one side ramp- other side stairs) in the middle of the distance. Try to complete unbroken 10-meter distances back and forth.
