Conditioning

3 sets of

For time

120 double-unders

60 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

12 hang power cleans 83/61kg – 185/135lb.

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Warm up

Build up to 83/61kg – 185/135lb. of hang power clean

Then,

30 double-unders

15 wall-balls

5 hang power cleans

Goal & Movements

Try to achieve high performance in each part. Move steadily through the first 2 rounds and push the last round hard. Go the double-unders unbroken, if you break take a breath and immediately continue with the remaining reps. On the wall-balls prefer to break into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between. Ideally, go the hang power cleans unbroken, if not break into a big and a smaller set.

Options

83/61kg – 185/135lb. —> 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

Gymnastics Conditioning

AMRAP 10 Minutes of

4 ring muscle-ups

12 single-leg squats

2 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft

6 box-jumps 76/61cm – 30/24”

 

Warm up

5.6.7 reps of

Ring pull-ups

Narrow squats

Ring dips

Box step-ups

Goal & Movements

Move at a steady tempo through the biggest part of the session, 7+ minutes, then start pushing harder to finish strong. Go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between that will allow you to move efficiently through each round and keep an equal tempo in each round from start to finish. On the rope climbs find solid footings and squat on the rope to assist your upper body when ascending.

Options

4 —> 3 —> 2 reps* of

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to ring pull-ups

*Choose a number of reps you can easily perform unbroken while fresh

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Maintain a pace on the assault bike, 65+/58+ RPM for men on the 1st cals and 75+/65+ RPM on the 2nd cals of each set. On the box jump-overs maintain a steady sustainable tempo throughout the session. Perform smooth transitions on and off the assault bike.
