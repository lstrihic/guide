2×1 @ 95%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power cleans until 50%.

Goal

Improve power. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between power cleans.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees and the barbell shoulder rest at a solid front rack position.

Clean Pull

2×3 @ 95% of Max Power Clean

2×2 @ 100%

2×1 @ 105%

 

Warm up

Continue to the clean pull from the percentage that you stopped the power clean.

Goal

Improve pulling strength.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell over hip height.

Front Squat

1×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

2×4 @ 75%

 

Warm up

Sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%.

Goal

Improve strength and confidence. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets. On the last percentages breathe well and keep a steady tempo.

Movements

Focus on a straight back, a solid front rack position and slow controlled squats.

Gymnastics Skill

Practice free handstand push-ups

 

Goal

Work for 10-15 minutes on free handstand push ups. Draw 3 marks on the ground where your head and hands should touch as they form a triangle base and try to work using these marks as a guide to position your body during reps.

Movements

Establish a solid base (form of triangle) with your hands and head on the ground and then use your hips and legs to push your body upwards, once you establish the handstand position with your body straight and extended return to the triangle base on the ground.

Gymnastics Pull Stamina

For form

8 sets of

5 strict pull-ups

10 seconds, Dead hang from the pull-up bar

5 strict pull-ups

10 seconds, Chin up pull-up bar hold

Rest as needed between sets

 

Warm up

Shoulder mobility

Then,

2 rounds of

15 inverted rows

10 scap pull-ups

5 kipping pull-ups

Goal & Movements

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Ideally go each set unbroken with adequate rest between in order to be efficient. Go with slow controlled pulls and maintain a solid hollow position when holding high or low from the pull-up bar. Alternatively, break each set into 5 strict pull-ups + 10 seconds, Dead hang from the pull-up bar, short rest (< 15 seconds),  5 strict pull-ups + 10 seconds, Chin up pull-up bar hold.

Engine

8 sets of

21/16 calories, Row

21/16 calories, Ski-erg

21/16 calories, Bike-erg

Rest 90 seconds between sets

 

Goal & pace

Try to achieve high performance in each part. Be explosive and push fast through the calories on each ergometer. Focus on the different mechanics (both legs & arms for row, more arms for ski, solely legs for bike erg) and practice smooth and efficient transition from ergometer to ergometer.
