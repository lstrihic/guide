Warm up

2 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Improve power and stability. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Warm up

Continue from the percentage that you left the power clean & jerk.

Improve strength. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Strength Stamina

For form

3 rounds of

15 calories, Assault bike

5 snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Then,

2 rounds of

15 calories, Assault bike

5 snatches 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

Then,

1 round of

15 calories, Assault bike

5 snatches 83/61kg – 185/95lb.


Warm up

Build up to 83/61kg – 185/95lb. of snatch

Then,

3 sets of

9/7 calories, Assault bike @ fast pace

3 snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Rest 60 seconds between sets


Goal & Movements

As volume decreases with each next round, slightly increase your pace on the assault bike. On the snatches go each round of the lightweight lifts unbroken with touch and go technique, once you feel like your technique breaks down immediately switch to single lifts. Same for the moderate weights. On the heavyweight barbell prefer to go with single lifts from the start at a solid tempo, yet make sure you take minimum rest between lifts.


Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb.

65/47kg – 145/105lb.

70/52kg – 155/115lb.
