Power Snatch + Overhead Squat

1×3+1 @ 60% of Max Power Snatch

1×2+1 @ 70%

1×1+2 @ 75%

1×1+2 @ 80%

3×1+2 @ 85%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 hang squat snatches + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat snatches + 2 overhead squats until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. The lifts at 60% are 3 squat snatches + 1 overhead squat, at 85% are 3 times of 1 squat snatch + 2 overhead squats. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position, once you have established a slow overhead position continue with slow controlled overhead squats.

Snatch High Pull

2×5 @ 90% of Max Power Snatch

1×3 @ 95%

1×2 @ 100%

1×1 @ 105%

 

Warm up

Continue to the snatch high pull from the percentage that you stopped the power snatch + overhead squat.

Goal

Improve pulling strength and angles.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell as high as possible. Do not force a pull with your arms yet make sure you drive the barbell as high as close as you can to your shoulders and as near as you can to your body’s vertical line.

50 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

150 double-unders

30 meters, Single arm dumbbell overhead lunges 32,5/22,5kg – 70/50lb.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

30 double-unders

15 wall-balls

1 rope climb

10 meters, Single arm dumbbell overhead lunges

1 rope climb

15 wall-balls

30 double-unders

Goal & Movements

This workout consists of 2 portions of consecutive exercises in reverse order divided by 10 rope climbs. Pace the exercises until you complete the 10 rope climbs and then start pushing harder up to a near max effort pace. Break the single arm dumbbell overhead lunges into 10-meter unbroken distances and go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible, if you break catch a really short breath and immediately continue with the remaining reps. If you are comfortable with wall-balls, try to go the first portion unbroken and the second portion unbroken for as long as possible, if not, prefer to break the first portion into a big and 1-2 smaller sets and try to go the second portion unbroken for as long as possible. On the rope climbs maintain a tempo that will allow fast an efficient ascents, make sure you use your legs to squat on the rope and descent smoothly by sliding down, extending your body and jumping on the ground.

Options

32,5/22,5kg – 70/50lb —> 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Goal

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Perform smooth transitions between exercises that will allow you to focus on quality of movements.

Movements

Warm up

Goal & Pace

Go for a 750-meters time trial (A). Follow the given time/distance pace and make sure you keep a high stroke rate, get the heart pumping and the blood flowing. Rest for 5-7 minutes and then go for 3 750-meter distance (B) based on the time trial yet at a slower pace and lower stroke rate in order to work on consistency.
