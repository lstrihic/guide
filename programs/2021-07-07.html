Squat Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

5×2 @ 80%

2×5 @ 70%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

5 snatch pulls + 5 hang power snatches + 5 front squats + 3 hang squat cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 squat snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve technique and explosiveness. The low percentages will allow you to improve your technique and lifting angles. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast transition under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position.

Squat Clean + Jerk

2×2+2 @ 50%

2×2+1 @ 60%

1×2+1 @ 65%

1×2+1 @ 70%

3×3+3 @ 75%

1×5+1 @ 65%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

5 clean pulls + 4 hang power cleans + 3 front squats + 2 push press + 1 split jerk 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat cleans + 2  jerks until 50%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and technique. The lifts at 60% are 2 times of  2 squat cleans + 1 jerk. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between squat cleans. Prefer to work on the split jerk movement and foot work.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell, a technical jerk and solid deep receiving positions.

Conditioning

For time

75 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

50 GHD sit-ups

30 ring muscle-ups

50 GHD sit-ups

75 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

3 sets of

15 wall-balls

10 GHD sit-ups

5 ring muscle ups

Rest 60 seconds between

Goal

This workout conists of 2 consecutive sequences of the same exercises with a 30-rep set of muscle-ups between. Pace until you complete the muscle-ups and then start pushing harder to go for the strong finish.

Movements

Ideally, go the wall-balls and GHD unbroken for as long as you can and break the muscle-ups into a a big (> 10 reps) and a few smaller sets (> 5 reps) with short rest between. Alternatively, break the 1st sequence into a big and smaller manageable sets with minimum rest between and go the 2nd sequence (after ring muscle-ups) unbroken for as long as you can.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

Gymnastics Conditioning

AMRAP 10 Minutes of

20 pull-ups

15 push-ups

10 single-leg squats

5 meters, Handstand walk

 

Warm up

10 push-ups

15 ring rows

10 narrow stance squats

20 seconds, Handstand hold on the wall

Then,

14  pull-ups

11 push-ups

8 single-leg squats

5 meters, Handstand walk

Goal

Move steadily for the biggest part of the workout (about 7 minutes) and then start increasing you pace to a max effort finish.

Movements

Ideally go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between them that will allow you to move efficiently through the session. Alternatively, break the pull-ups or push-ups into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between.

Options

Handstand walk —> 2 wall-walks

Ski-erg, 600 meters

60 double-unders

Ski-erg, 600 meters

40 double-unders

8 bar-facing burpees over

Ski-erg, 600 meters

20 double-unders

4 bar-facing burpees over

 

Goal

Try to be consistent, the goal is to go the whole session at a fast steady pace with smooth transitions between exercises and if possible push harder as you reach the finish.

Movements & Tempo

Maintain a steady pace on the ski-erg and move steadily on the bar-facing burpees over for the whole session. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take a short breath and immediately continue with the remainaing exercises. If you feel comfortable enough increase you tempo on the burpees in the last rounds.
