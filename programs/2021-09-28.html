Shoulders Press

5×2 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3×8 shoulders presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 reps until 90%.

Goal

Improve strength. Start a new set at 90% every 90 seconds.

Movements

Keep your legs and hips straight and still and focus on using only your shoulders to push the barbell overhead.

Hang Power Clean

2×5 @ 50%

2×3 @ 60%

1×1 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

4×1 @ 85%

1×3 @ 65%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

2 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3-4 hang power cleans until 50%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

First pull the barbell to hip height, then slightly drive the barbell downwards to create some tension and explosively pull to the overhead position. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to the hip crease and avoid spending too much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position.

Conditioning

For time

4 rounds of

Run, 400 meters

21 handstand push-ups

9 deadlifts 143/91kg – 315/200lb

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

600 meters, Run

Then,

Build up to 143/91kg – 315/200lb deadlift.

Then,

20 shoulder taps

10 bodyweight kang squats

20 push ups

40 seconds, Handstand hold on the wall

Goal

Push steadily through the total volume. Make a plan to break the deadlifts so as you keep moving efficiently.

Movements

Run at a steady tempo that will be hard on your cardio but still allow you to breathe. Ideally go the handstand push-ups unbroken and break the deadlifts into no more than 2-3 sets with short to minimum rest between. Alternatively, break the handstand push-ups into a big and 1-2 smaller sets and don’t hesitate to go the deadlifts with single lifts if you find that needed.

Options

143/91kg – 315/200lb —> 124/83kg – 245/185lb
