Squat Snatch

1×3 @ 50%

2×2 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 50%. Improve explosiveness and stability. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

A. For time

150 double-unders

75 wall-ball shots9/6kg – 20/14lb.

50 chest to bar pull-ups

25 devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Cap: 12 minutes

 

Rest 5 minutes

 

B. For time

25 devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

50 chest to bar pull-ups

75 wall-ball shots 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

150 double-unders

Cap: 13 minutes

 

Notes

Try to achieve maximum performance in each portion. The first portion has a decreasing volume of several exercises which inversely increase in the second portion. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can, if you break continue immediately with the remaining reps. Ideally go the wall-ball shots unbroken, alternatively break into a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with short rest between. Do the same for the chest to bar pull-ups and go the devils presses at the fastest sustainable tempo in the first portion, at a steady sustainable tempo in the second portion.

Options

Chest to bar pull-ups —> pull-ups

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb. —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.

Gymnastics

For form

13.11.9 reps of

Strict ring dips

Strict handstand push-ups

Then,

13.11.9 reps of

Strict pull-ups

3 rope climbs 4,6m -15ft.

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Go each exercise unbroken for as long as proper form allows, break 2-3 reps before you feel like reaching failure, take a breath and continue. Be technical on the rope climbs, create solid footings to squat on the rope and descend smoothly by sliding down and jumping.

Options

Strict —> kipping ring dips – handstand push-ups – pull-ups
