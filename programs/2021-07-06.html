Barbell Cycling

3 sets of

12 power cleans

6 front squats

12 shoulders to overhead

6 front squats

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

83/52kg – 180/115lb.

Warm up

3 sets of

5 deadlifts + 4 power cleans + 3 presses + 2 power jerks 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 6 power cleans + 6 front squats + 6 shoulders to overhead until 70/43lg – 155/95lb.

Goal

The goal is to move efficiently with the barbell. Ideally go the power cleans unbroken and touch and go, alternatively start with a touch and go set and continue with fast single reps. From then on continue with unbroken front squats and shoulder to overhead. Be patient and breathe well through each set. If you struggle, prefer to go unbroken until the shoulders to overhead and break before the second squats.

Movements

Maintain a steady tempo on the squats and focus on smooth rebounding (on shoulders) on the shoulder to overheads

Options

83/52kg – 180/115lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.
