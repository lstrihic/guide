1×1 @ 75%

3×1 @ 80%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

1 snatch pull + 2 hang power snatches + 2 hang squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

1 snatch pull + 2” pause below knees + 2 low hang squat snatches until 50%.

Goal

Work on pulling strength and stability. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Start with a controlled pull until the barbell is 10cm below the knees, then maintain a solid position for 2 full seconds and continue with a technical pull and a fast drive under the barbell. Start each rep from the ground.

Front Squat

ΕΜΟΜ 10 Minutes of

2 reps @ 80-83%

 

Warm up

Sets 2-3 reps until you reach 80%.

Goal

Improve form and consistency. Go with a set of 2 repetitions every minute for 10 minutes (20 reps total). Select a percentage withing the given that will allow you to confidently complete the EMOM.

Movements

Focus on a proper front rack position, slow controlled descents and fast explosive ascents at a steady tempo.

Gymnastics Stamina

AMRAP 11 Minutes of

2 rope climbs 4,6 meters – 15ft.

4 wall-walks

2 rope climbs 4,6 meters – 15ft.

8 deficit handstand push-ups 10/5cm – 4/2” (plates)

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

12 ring rows

6 rope pull-ups

12 handstand shoulder taps (on the wall)

6 meters, Bear crawl

12 hand release push-ups

Goal & Movements

Move steadily for the biggest part of the workout and then start pushing harder by shortening your transitions. Ascend steadily on the rope finding solid footings to squat and assist your upper body. Go the wall-walks at a steady sustainable tempo with focus on full range of motion and proper reps, rest < 3”, and the deficit handstand push-ups unbroken.

Options

Deficit —> regular handstand push-ups

*Scale down to regular hspu if you cant perform at least 8 unbroken deficit reps while fresh.
