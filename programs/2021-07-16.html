B. For time

Run, 800 meters

30 bar muscle-ups

Cap: 10 minutes

 

Warm up

800 meters, Run

Then,

Build up to 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Then,

400 meters, Run

Then,

2 rounds of

5 power snatches

5 bar muscle-ups

Goal

Try to achieve maximum performance in each one of these two portions. Run at a fast yet sustainable pace that will allow you breathe and push efficiently through the power snatches and the bar muscle-ups.

Movements

Start the power snatches with a big unbroken touch and go set, then continue with fast single lifts with minimum rest between and, if possible finish with a touch and go set. Go the bar muscle-ups with big yet manageable sets, ideally as few as possible.

Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb. —> 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

3 rounds of

7 devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

1 pegboard ascent

Then

3 rounds of

7 full deficit handstand push-ups 20/15cm – 8/6”

10 meters, Double kettlebell overhead lunges 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

 

Warm up

2.4.6.8.10

Devils presses

Peg board pull-ups

Strict handstand push-ups

5 meters, Double kettlebell overhead lunges

Goal

Focus on proper form and movement quality. Move steadily through the total volume taking the necessary rest when you need to, in order to keep moving efficiently.

Movements

Push through the devils presses at a steady sustainable tempo. Ideally go the full deficit handstand push-ups unbroken, alternatively break into as few manageable sets as possible. Be technical and fast on the pegboard, the less you hang from the board, the better, make good use of your legs to create traction. On the double kettlebell overhead lunges first establish a solid overhead position and then try to go the 10-meter distances unbroken.

Options

Pegboard ascent —> rope climb

20/15cm – 8/6” —> 10/5cm – 4/2”

24/16kg – 50/35lb. —> 20/12kg – 44/26lb.

Warm up

2 minutes @ 2km PR pace + 10 seconds

Goal

The goal is to row the given distance smoothly with proper form and technique maintaining exactly the given stroke rate and pace with priority on the stroke rate. Go with slow full and powerful pulls.
