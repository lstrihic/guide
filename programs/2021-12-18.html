Warm up

3 sets of

2 back squats + 2 back presses + 2 snatch balances + 2 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 snatch balances + 1 overhead squat until you reach 60%.

Goal

The lifts at 60% are 2 snatch balances + 1 overhead squat, at 85% are 3 times 1 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.


Movements

Use racks or high blocks. Focus on an explosive drive under the barbell and a solid overhead position.

Deadlift

EMOM 10 Minutes of

2 reps @ 85-90%


Warm up

3×8 deadlifts @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Few sets of 2-3 reps until 85%.


Goal

Improve strength and consistency. Work for 10 minutes, select a weight that you feel comfortable with within 85-90%. In each minute complete 2 consecutive reps and rest the remaining time until the next minute starts.


Movements

Ideally go touch and go and focus on utilizing your legs on each rep so as to take pressure away from your back. If you go with single reps make sure you do not rest too much between them.

Warm up

1000 meters @ easy pace


Goal & Pace

The main session is a total distance of 7000 meters. When competing against others it is important to be able to finish strong after a long run and that is the goal in this one. Increase pace in each 1000 meter distance (select a pace depending on your level), progressively increase your heart and respiratory rate and when you reach the last 1000 meters push your limits (near the VO2 max) and go for the strong finish.

Cool down

1000 meters @ very easy pace
