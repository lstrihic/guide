Squat Snatch

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

3×1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 70%. Work on explosiveness and technique. Focus on fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep overhead position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Snatch Pull

3×2 @ 90% of Max Squat Snatch

 

Notes

Continue to the snatch pull from where you left the squat snatch. Work on pulling strength and lifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

10 rounds of

Ski-erg, 200 meters

1 leg-less rope climb 4,6m – 15ft.

3 overhead squats 83/61kg – 180/135lb.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Notes

Be consistent, the goal is to maintain the first round pace for as long as you can throughout the session. Push fast through the ski meters but surely not at max effort, then go straight to the legless rope climb. Use your legs (kicks or kipping) to assist your upper body and descend smoothly by sliding down, extending your body and jumping on the ground. Take a breath each time before the overhead squats, prefer to clean and swifty position on the back rack and jerk. Focus on unbroken sets and controlled squats with a solid overhead position .

Options

Leg-less —> regular rope climbs

83/61kg – 180/135lb. —> 70/52kg – 155/115lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Strongman Stamina

EMOM 20 Minutes of

Odd: 3 bench press @ 85%

Even: 10 meters, Heavy farmers walk

 

Notes

In every odd minute complete 3 bench presses, in every even minute complete a 10-meter unbroken distance farmer walk. Load two handles as heavy as to struggle as you reach the finish yet efficiently go the distance unbroken each time.

—

 

Max Effort

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

30/23 calories Assault bike

12 bar muscle-ups

Max reps, Burpee box-jumps over 76/61cm – 30/24”

 

Notes

Give everything you got from the start. Be super explosive on the assault bike and push swiftly through the calories, 72+/63+ RPM for women. Ideally go the bar muscle ups unbroken, alternatively if you are not comfortable with the exercise prefer to break from the start into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. In this way you will plan how much time you need without over-trying and risking losing additional time. Then go straight to the burpees, the goal is to maintain the fastest possible pace. Go for an attempt of 1 minute max-rep burpees prior to the session and then try to maintain 90% of that pace in the session.

Options

Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

*Go with chest to bar pull-ups if you can’t perform at least 8 unbroken bar muscle-ups while fresh.

Engine – Running

B. 2 sets of

4000 meters @ 4’15”-5’20”/km

Notes

Warm up by running 2000 meters prior to the main session and cool down for 1000 meters afterwards at a comfortable pace of your choice. Be as consistent as you can in each set, select a pace that will lead to 82-85% of Vo2Max.
