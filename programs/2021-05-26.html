Notes

Warm up with sets of 2 snatch pulls + 2 low hang squat snatches until you reach 50%. The lifts at 50% are 2 sets of 2 consecutive reps of 1 snatch pull + 2” pause below knees + 1 low hang squat snatch, at 55% are 2 consecutive reps of 1 snatch pull + 2” pause below knees + 1 low hang squat snatch. Complete a full snatch pull, then drive the barbell 10cm below knees, count 2 seconds at that position and finish with a squat snatch.

Conditioning

A. For time

45 thrusters 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

45 pull-ups

Cap: 7 minutes

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. For time

2 rounds of

15 thrusters 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

15 chest to bar pull-ups

Cap: 5 minutes

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

C. For time

15 thrusters 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

15 bar muscle-ups

Cap: 3 minutes

 

Notes

Volume decreases with each round yet difficulty of exercises increases. In each portion, ideally, go the thrusters unbroken for as long as proper form allows, if you feel that you struggle break, take a breath and continue with smaller sets. Alternatively, break from the start into a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with short rest between. Go the gymnastics unbroken, if not possible, break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. Try to move as much as you can with the barbell on the thrusters, if you need to rest do it with the barbell overhead.

Options

Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

Notes

Build up with sets of 5 reps until you reach 65%. Alternate between 5 reps at 65% and single heavy lifts at increasing percentages. Focus on steady tempo on the high-rep sets and solid lifts on the heavy singles. Rest no less than 60 seconds from heavy lifts to light lifts, no less than 90 seconds from light lifts to heavy lifts.
