Hang Power Clean

2×2 @ 60%

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

2×2 @ 75%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

2 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power cleans until 60%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

First pull the barbell to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to the hip crease and avoid spending too much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position. In each set reposition swiftly and continue to the second rep.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

Row, 600 meters

40 GHD sit-ups

20 meters, Handstand walk

Cap: 20/22 minutes

 

Warm up

1000 meters, Row @ easy pace

Then,

2 rounds of

30 seconds, Hollow hold

20 shoulder taps

15 GHD sit-ups

10 meters, Handstand walk

Goal

The more you are able to maintain a steady performance throughout (all rounds approximately equal) the better your performance will be. Perform smooth transitions between exercises that will allow you to follow a steady tempo.

Movements

Row at a steady sustainable pace and maintain a steady solid tempo on the GHD sit-ups, you should be able to keep it and even increase it in the last round where you push harder. Break the handstand walk into 10-meter unbroken distances, move fast on your hands with quick long steps. The last handstand walk will be the challenge, try to remain composed and confident and finish with efficient unbroken distances.

Options

Handstand walk —> 5 wall-walks (go with handstand walk if you perform at least 10-unbroken meters while fresh)

C. For form

15/12 calories, Assault bike

5 snatches 83/52kg – 185/115lb.

15/12 calories, Assault bike

5 clean & jerks 83/52kg – 185/115lb.

 

Warm up

Sets of 1 snatch + 2 clean & jerks until 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Then,

9/7 calories,  Assault bike @ fast pace

5 snatches

9/7 calories, Assault bike @ fast pace

5 clean & jerks

Goal & Movements

Move fast and efficiently with the barbell in each set under metabolic fatigue caused by the assault bike. Be explosive and push fast through the calories on the bike each time, 75+ for men | 65+ for women. Ideally go the snatches and clean jerks with touch & go sets. If possible, go with power snatches/cleans, break the high rep set of the 1st portion (A) into a big and 1-2 smaller touch & go sets and as reps decrease and weight increases in each portion go for 2 sets (B) and 1 unbroken set (C). If you are not comfortable with barbells, go with single lifts at a fast tempo or even switch to squat snatches/cleans in order to keep moving efficiently.

Options

A. 61/43kg – 135/95lb. —> 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

B. 70/47kg – 155/105lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

C. 83/52kg – 185/115lb. —>  70/47kg – 155/105lb.

Goal & Pace

Run a total distance of 7000 meters. When competing against others its important to be able to finish strong after a long run and that is what we want to work in this one. Increase pace in each 1000 meter distance (select a pace depending on your level), progressively increase your heart and respiratory rate and when you reach the last 1000 meters push your limits, near the VO2 max, and go for the strong finish.
