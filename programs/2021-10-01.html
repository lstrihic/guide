Improve strength and confidence. Go for a lift at 100%, warm up properly with an empty barbell and continue with solid single lifts until the Max. Start a new set every 60 seconds.

Goal & Movements

This is a triplet of ascending reps. As reps increase with each round try to continue going unbroken the ring muscle-ups and double-unders and maintain a fast equal tempo on the wall-walks. The more you are able to keep your tempo equal through rounds the better your performance will be. The ring muscle-ups might get challenging after the wall-walks, if you find it needed break into 2-3 manageable sets that will allow you to keep moving efficiently.

Engine- Rowing

A. Row 2500 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 16 seconds

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. Row 2500 meters @ 22 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 14 seconds

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

C. Row 2500 meters @ 24 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 12 seconds

 

Warm up

1000 meters @ light pressure warm up routines (0-250 meters only arm pulls, 250-500 only leg pulls, 500-1000 regular rowing).

Goal

Be consistent in each set maintaining steady strokes and pace for each 2500-meter distance. In part A keep steady 20 strokes and try to achieve a 2km PR pace +16 seconds, in part B, 22 strokes and 2km PR pace + 14 seconds and in part C, 24 strokes and 2km PR pace + 12 seconds.

Cool down

4 minutes @ very light pressure.
