Power Snatch

EMOM 10 Minutes of

1 rep @ 85-90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

5 snatch pulls + 5 hang power snatches + 5 overhead presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 power snatches until 70%.

Sets of 2 power snatches until 85%.

Goal

Improve power and consistency. Complete 1 solid power snatch in each minute and rest until the next minute starts. Work for 10 minutes in total. If you feel comfortable and confident increase percentages within the given range.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull and a solid overhead receiving position.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

30 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

20 power cleans 43/30kg – 20/14lb.

10 chest to bar pull-ups

Then

3 rounds of

15 front squats 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

10 strict handstand push-ups

5 bar muscle-ups

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

15 wall-balls

10 power cleans (lightweight barbell)

5 chest to bar

9 front squats (lightweight barbell)

6 strict handstand push-ups

3 bar muscle-ups

Goal

Be consistent, move steadily throughout the session. Avoid pushing too hard too soon, try to go each exercise unbroken and perform smooth transitions between them.

Movements

Go the wall-balls and the chest to bar pull-ups unbroken, alternatively break the wall-balls into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. Go the power cleans with big unbroken touch & go sets. Go the front squats and the bar muscle ups unbroken and break the strict handstand push ups into manageable sets.

Options

Strict —> kipping handstand push-ups

Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

Gymnastics Conditioning

For time

10 rounds of

Ski-erg, 10/8 calories

Handstand walk, 10 meters

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Warm up

2 sets of

10/8 calories, Ski-erg

10 shoulder taps

then

2 sets of

7/5 calories, Ski-erg

Handstand walk, 5 meters

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal

Maintain a fast yet sustainable pace on the ski-erg throughout the session and go the handstand walk fast and efficiently each time. Be consistent, the more you manage to keep the score of the last round equal to that of the first rounds the better your performance will be.

Options

Engine

12 sets of

AMRAP 60 Seconds of

15/12 calories, Assault-bike or 10/8 calories, Echo-bike

Max Reps, Bar-facing burpees over

Rest 60 seconds between sets

 

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Focus on consistency, don’t rush the first rounds and try to achieve equal performance on each round throughout the session.

Movements & Tempo

Push fast and explosively through the calories then go straight to the bar-facing burpees over, move on the burpees at a fast yet sustaiable tempo that will allow you to breathe (heavily but still breathing).
