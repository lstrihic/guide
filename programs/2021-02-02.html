Squat Clean + Jerk

1×3+2 @ 60%

2×2+2 @ 65%

2×2+1 @ 70%

1×2+1 @ 80%

1×1+1 @ 85%

3×1+1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2 squat cleans + 2 jerks until you reach 60%. The lifts at 60% are 1 set of 3 squat cleans + 2 jerks, at 70% are 2 sets of 2 squat cleans + 1 jerk. Focus on a fast drive under the bar, an explosive jerk and solid receiving positions. Go with single lifts on the squat cleans. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between squat cleans.

—

 

Clean Pulls

3×3 @ 95% of Max Squat Clean

 

Notes

Continue to the clean pulls from where you left the squat clean + jerks. Work on pulling strength. Focus on a slow, strong starting pull and an explosive shoulder shrug. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 5 seconds between lifts.

12 thrusters 52/37kg – 115/75lb

C. For time

3 rounds of

9 bar muscle-ups

9 thrusters 61/43kg – 135/95lb

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each part (couplet).

Same applies in every part. Ideally got both exercises unbroken with smooth transitions to the thrusters and faster transition from the thrusters to the other exercise. Alternatively prefer to break the exercise that you struggle more with (depending on whether you are comfortable with weights or gymnastics) into a big and smaller set with minimum rest between. In any case, if you need to break, it is better to break the toes to bar than the thrusters, it is less taxing to reposition.

Options

Snatch Cycling

3 sets of

5 hang power snatches @ 80%

5 power snatches

5 squat snatches

Rest as needed between sets

 

Notes

The goal is to move efficiently with the barbell. Ideally, go the hang power snatches unbroken, drop, go for a set of 5 touch & go power snatches, drop, increase weight and go for 5 touch & go squat snatches. Alternatively break the hang power snatches and/or power snatches into touch & go sets (3-2 reps) and go the squat snatches with single lifts with minimum rest between.

5 squat snatches

Options

80 → 75 → 70% of Max Hang Power Snatch

Gymnastics

For form

6 rounds of

7 calories, Assault bike

15 meters, Handstand walk

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and efficiency. Move explosively on the bike completing the 7 calories fast, then take a breath and go for a 15-meter unbroken distance of handstand walk. Move fast on your hands with short and quick steps and a solid handstand position (hollow, feet straight and extended upwards). Alternatively break the handstand walk into 2×7,5-meter or 3×5-meter unbroken distances depending on your level.

Options

15 → 10 → 5 meters* of

Handstand walk → 2 wall-walks

*Choose an option that you can perform at least 1 set unbroken when fresh.

Engine

A. Run, 15 minutes @ easy pace

 

B. 4 sets of

Run, 1500 meters @ increasing pace

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Warm up by running 15 minutes at an easy pace. In the main session try to achieve an increasing performance. Select paces within the given time frames which you can efficiently sustain and increase with each set. Avoid starting the first set too fast and be unable to either maintain this pace in the set or continue with a faster pace in the next one.

1st set: 4:00-4:40/km

2nd set: 3:55-4:30/km

3rd set: 3:50-4:20/km

4th set: 3:45-4:10/km

Cool down by 5 minutes jogging.

Options

1500 → 1200 meters @ increasing pace
