Warm up

3 sets of

5 snatch pulls + 5 hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 power snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve power and weightlifting angles. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled first pull until the knees, a powerful second pull and a solid overhead receiving position.

Snatch High Pull

5×3 @ 90%

 

Warm up

Continue to the snatch high pull from the percentage you finished the power snatch.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and weightlifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Keep the barbell as close to your body’s vertical line as you can and pull the barbell as high as possible, keep your elbows high at the final position.

Engine

5 sets of

AMRAP 4 Minutes of

Run, 200 meters

18 bar-facing burpees over

Max reps, Double-unders

Rest 2 minutes between sets

 

Goal

Try to achieve high performance throughout, be consistent through the 4 sets as you try to complete in every set the made reps of the first set.

Movements

Run each 200-meter distance at a fast yet manageable pace that will allow you to breath well and continue straight to the burpees. On the bar-facing burpees over move at a fast pace, avoid pushing too close to your limits and keep in mind that you have to complete the couplet fast but still be able to perform well on the double-unders. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can.
