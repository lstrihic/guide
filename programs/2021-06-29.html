Warm up

3 sets of

5 clean pulls + 5 hang power cleans + 3 hang squat cleans + 3 front squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2-3 squat cleans until 50%.

 

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability at the bottom position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid front rack on the deep squat position.

Barbell Cycling

4 sets of

6 hang power snatches

6 power snatches

6 squat snatches

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

1st set @ 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

2nd set @ 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

3rd set @ 70/48kg – 155/105lb.

4th set @ 83/52kg – 180/115lb.

Warm up

3 sets of

5 hang power snatches + 5 power snatches + 5 squat snatches 20/15kg barbell

then

1 set of

Same pattern 43/34kg – 95/75lb.

Goal

The goal is to move efficiently with the barbell. Try to maintain a steady tempo throughout each set, either you go unbroken or you make sure you tempo on the squat snatches is as similar as possible to that of the power snatches.

Movements

On the lightweight percentages try to go all lifts of each round unbroken. On the heavier weights focus on going the hang power snatches unbroken and then switch to single lifts with minimum rest between for the power snatches and the squat snatches (especially the squat snatches).

Options

1st set @ 70% of Max Power Snatch

2nd @ 75%

3rd @ 80%

4th @ 85%

Engine – Running

3 sets of

1000 meters @ 3’30”-4’15”km

Rest 3 minutes between

 

Warm up

20 minutes @ progressively increased pace

Goal

Keep a steady sustainable pace. The aim is at promoting Vo2max and lactate threshold. Increase up to 90% of Max effort in each set and then pace. Recover by 3 minutes walking between.
