Conditioning

AMRAP 13 Minutes of

60 deadlifts 102/70kg – 225/155lb

60 strict handstand push-ups

60 single arm dumbbell hang clean jerk 22,5/15kg

60 meters, Handstand walk*

 

Notes

Go the deadlifts with a big touch and go set and then some smaller sets with short to minimum rest between. The strict handstand push-ups are the most challenging part of the workout, make sure you break into small manageable sets (5-8 reps)that will allow you to push smoothly through the total volume. Maintain a steady pace on the single arm dumbbell hang clean jerks, reps are alternating so focus on technical transitions mid-air for extra speed. Break the handstand walk into 10-meters unbroken distances, move fast and efficiently with short but quick steps. *Count every 2,5 meters as 1 rep.

Options

102/70kg – 225/155lb → 82/52kg → 180/115lb

Strict handstand push-ups → kipping handstand push-ups

22,5/15kg → 50/35lb.

Handstand walk → 12 wall-walks

*Choose kipping handstand push-ups if you can’t go 15 unbroken strict handstand push-ups while fresh.

Gymnastics

AMRAP 10 Minutes of

35 double-unders

3 bar muscle-ups

 

Notes

Go each exercise unbroken, perform smooth transitions between exercises that will allow you to do so. Trying to go each round EMOM is a challenging but good strategy to follow.

Options

Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups

Choose chest to bar pull-ups if you can’t perform 3 unbroken reps of bar muscle-ups while fresh.

Engine

4 sets of

180 double-unders

60/45 calories, Assault bike

30 burpee box jump overs 60/50cm – 24/20”

Start a new set every 10 Minutes

 

Notes

This workout has a total duration of 40 minutes. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible, then go straight to the bike. Maintain on both bike and burpee box jump overs a fast yet sustainable pace. Rest the remaining time until the next 10-minute set starts again.

Options

Start a new set every 12 minutes if you don’t manage to finish the previous set within 9 minutes.
