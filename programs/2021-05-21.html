Squat Snatch

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2 reps until you reach 70%. Improve confidence and stability. Focus on a powerful pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Snatch Pull

5×1 @ 100% of Max Squat Snatch

 

Notes

Continue to the snatch pull from where you stopped the squat snatch. Improve pulling power. Rest no less than 60-90 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

AMRAP 15 Minutes of

Buy in: Run, 800 meters

Then,

4 double dumbbell thrusters 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

8 toes to bar

Notes

Start the workout with an 800-meter distance and continue with as many reps as you can of the triplet. Run at a steady manageable pace. Go each exercise unbroken, start with smooth transitions and push harder as you reach the finish.

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

5 rounds of

12 strict handstand push-ups

6 burpee step-overs 32,5/22,5kg – 70/50lb, 61/50cm – 24/20”

3 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Ideally go the strict handstand push ups of each round unbroken, alternatively break into a big and 1-2 smaller sets with short rest between. Practice the burpee step overs and try to move consistently at a steady tempo. On the rope climb move technically with proper leg assistance (solid footing and squatting on the rope) and descent smoothly by sliding down and jumping on the ground. Perform smooth transitions that will allow you to move efficiently.

Options

Strict handstand push-ups —> pike push-ups – elevated feed

32,5/22,5kg – 70/50lb —> 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Engine

A1. Row 4 Minutes using the warm up routines

 

A2. 2 sets of

Row,  500 meters @ 20 strokes

Rest 2 minutes between sets

 

B1. Row, 3000 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

Rest 3 minutes

 

B2. Row, 2000 meters @ 22 strokes/minute

Rest 3 minutes

 

B3. Row, 1000 meters @ 24 strokes/minute

Pace:

 

B1: 2km PR pace +18 seconds

B2: 2km PR pace +16 seconds

B3: 2km PR pace + 10 seconds

 

Notes

Warm up for 4 minutes at a comfortable pace, row the 1st minute using only your arms, the 2nd minute only your legs, the 3rd minute using legs and arms with super focus on coordination and the last minute do the full movement, then go for 2 minutes at steady 20 strokes to prime for the main session. In the main session, focus on consistency, the goal is to row the given distances at exactly the stroke/minute and time/distance paces with priority on the strokes. Row 2 minutes at super light pressure as cool down.
