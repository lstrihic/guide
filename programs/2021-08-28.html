Power Clean & Jerk

2×2 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 80%

4×1 @ 85%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power cleans + 2 jerks until 60%.

Goal

Improve power and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled first pull until the knees, a powerful second pull and solid receiving positions. Go with power jerks, ideally touch and go on the shoulders at the lower percentages.

Clean High Pull

2×2 @ 90% of Max Power Clean

1×2 @ 95%

1×2 @ 100%

 

Warm up

Continue from the percentage you left the power clean & jerk.

Goal

Improve strength. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled pull until the barbell is over the knees, then increase speed and finish with an explosive shoulder shrug slightly bending your arms to allow a bit more height to the barbell.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

16 double dumbbell shoulders to overhead 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

12 ring muscle-ups

8 squat cleans 83/61kg – 185/135lb.

4 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

Build up to a 83/61kg – 185/135lb. squat clean

Then,

8 double dumbbell shoulders to overhead 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

6 ring muscle-ups

4 squat cleans 83/61kg – 185/135lb.

2 rope climbs

Goal

Move steadily through the first 2 rounds with smooth transitions between exercises and push the last round hard. Use protection on the calves so you can work efficiently on the rope.

Movements

Ideally go the double dumbbell shoulders to overhead and the ring muscle-ups unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. On the squat cleans, if you are comfortable with the barbell start with a touch & go set and continue with single lifts, if you are not, go with single lifts from the start. Move fast on the rope, find solid footings and use your legs to assist your upper body, descend smoothly by sliding down and jumping on the ground.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

83/61kg – 185/135lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Push Press

Daily Max

Then

3×8 @ 65%

Rest 60 seconds between sets

 

Warm up

3 sets of

8 strict presses + 5 push presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Go with sets of 3-4 reps < 75%, 2 reps < 85% and a few single reps until you find the heavy lift of the day. Then drop down to 65% and perform the given sets to increase muscle mass and quality.

Movements

Use your legs and hips to create momentum and assist your arms and shoulders send the barbell overhead.

Max Effort

For time

45 meters, Handstand walk

30 chest to bar pull-ups

15 meters, Double dumbbell overhead lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Cap: 5 minutes

 

Warm up

Shoulder mobility

Then,

2 rounds of

5 meters, Double dumbbell overhead lunges

10 meters, Handstand walk

15 chest to bar pull-ups

Goal & Movements

Go the handstand walk by 7,5-meter unbroken distances. On the chest to bar pull-ups ideally go unbroken, alternatively break into a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with minimum rest between. The challenge is the overhead lunges, your shoulders and cardio will be taxed so you have to give your best to find a solid overhead position and then push smoothly and unbroken for as long as you can through the distances.

Options

Handstand walk —> 5 wall-walks

Chest to bar pull-ups —> pull-ups

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb. —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.

Engine – Running

2000 meters @ 4’30”-5’20”/km

1000 meters @ 4’00”-5’00”/km

 

Warm up

10 minutes running drills

Goal & Pace

This is a total distance of 6000 meters. The distance is broken into 3 portions and each portion is at a faster pace than the previous one. Select a pace of the given time frames which you can efficiently maintain in each portion and increase accordingly in each next portion.

Cool down

5 minutes @ very easy pace
