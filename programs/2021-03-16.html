Jerk

2×2 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

Notes

Warm with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight until you reach 60%. Ideally work on split jerks. Improve stability and confidence. Focus on an explosive push, a fast drive under the bar and a solid overhead receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Conditioning

AMRAP 20 Minutes of

5 push press 52/37kg – 115/80lb

10 handstand push-ups

15 deadlifts 52/37kg – 115/80lb

 

Notes

Move fast yet steadily for the biggest part of the workout, 15+ minutes, then start pushing harder by shortening your transitions. Ideally go each exercise unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. If you need to break prefer to break the deadlifts and focus on going the push presses and handstand push-ups unbroken.

Barbell Cycling

For form

3 rounds of

15 power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb

12 bar-facing burpees over

Then

2 rounds of

15 power snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb

12 bar-facing burpees over

Then

1 round of

15 power snatches 82/55kg – 180/120lb

12 bar-facing burpees over

 

Notes

This workout consists of 3 consecutive parts, in each part the load on the bar increases. Go the bar facing burpees at a fast yet steady pace throughout the workout. Ideally go the lightweight snatches unbroken with touch & go technique, alternatively break into a big and a smaller touch and go set. On the under heavy snatches, if you are comfortable with barbells, start with a touch and go set and if you break switch to singles, if not prefer to go with single lifts from the start. On the heavy lifts prefer to go with solid singles at a steady tempo. Move fast from the barbell to the burpees, take a breath before the lifts.

Options

61/43kg – 135/95lb → 52/37kg – 155/80lb

82/55kg – 180/120lb → 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Gymnastics

4 sets of

AMRAP 4 Minutes of

100 double-unders

15/12 calories, Assault bike

Max reps, Ring muscle-ups

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Notes

Although your performance will drop with each set, try to put high effort in all sets of the session. Push fast and efficiently through the couplet, go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible and push explosively through the calories on the assault bike. Take a breath before you start with the muscle-ups and go for a big unbroken set and some smaller sets with short to minimum rest between. Don’t hesitate to go with single reps of muscle-ups if needed.

Options

Ring muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups
