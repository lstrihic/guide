2×3 @ 60% of Max Power Snatch

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

3×1 @ 75%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 snatch pulls + 3 hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 deadlifts until 10cm below the knees + 1 power snatch until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

First deadlift the barbell until 10cm below the knees, hold for 2 seconds remaining still, and then continue the movement until the barbell is overhead. Focus on a solid hold position below the knees and an explosive pull. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees, shoulders tight and elbows locked out.

3 hang squat snatches + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat snatches + 2 overhead squats until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. The lifts at 60% are 2 times of 2 squat snatches + 1 overhead squat. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position, once you have established a slow overhead position continue with slow controlled overhead squats.

B. For form

12.9.6 reps of

Strict deficit handstand push-ups

Strict weighted ring dips 12/8kg – 26/18lb. kettlebell

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

6 strict handstand push-ups

6 strict ring dips

3 strict deficit handstand push-ups

3 strict weighted ring dips

Goal

Move steadily with focus on proper form and full range of motion.

Movements

Ideally, go unbroken in each round for as long as proper form allows, then catch a short breath and continue until you complete the reps with smaller sets. Always keep in mind to stop 2-3 reps before failure so you can focus on form and keep moving efficiently. Alternatively, break from the start into manageable sets.

Options

A. Strict handstand push-ups —> Pike push-ups (elevated feet)

Strict ring dips —> jumping ring dips

B. Strict deficit handstand push-ups —> pike deficit handstand push-ups

Strict weighted ring dips —> Weighted ring push-ups

Engine

5 rounds of

Row, 800 meters

Run, 400 meters

Cap: 30 minutes

 

Goal

The goal in this one is A. the total time it took you to complete the 5 rounds and B. the slowest recorded pace on the rower. That means you have two scores, so you have to complete the session fast but really focus on maintaining a steady pace on the rower, sustainable for the whole session. So for example, a pace of 1’45”/500m through the whole session yet with an instant decrease at 2’00”/500m will lead to a score of 2’00”/500m.

Movements

Select a pace on the rower that you can sustain for the whole session and moreover set a higher limit and make sure you won’t exceed it at any point.
