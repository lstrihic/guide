Squat Snatch

3×1 @  70%

3×1 @  75%

2×1 @  80%

2×1 @ 85%

1×1 @  90%

1×1 @ 95%

1×1 @ 100%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2 reps until you reach 70%. Improve consistency and confidence. Go the squat snatches in EMOM style performing 1 lift per minute. Load the bar on your own.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

15 toes to bar

15 overhead squats* 43/30kg – 95/65lb

Then,

3 rounds of

12 toes to bar

12 overhead squats* 52/37kg – 115/80lb

Then,

3 rounds of

9 toes to bar

9 overhead squats* 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

This workout consists of 3 consecutive couplets of toes to bar and overhead squats. The load on the barbell increases in each part yet reps decrease. Ideally go each exercise unbroken and perform smooth transitions between them that will allow you to achieve that. Alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between. If you need to break prefer to break the overhead squats and not the toes to bars since the effort to reposition on the overhead squats is bigger. *Each time lift the barbell from the ground.

Strength/Gymnastics

For time

50 push-ups hand release

40 toes to bar

30 double dumbbell step-ups over 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

25 push-ups hand release

20 toes to bar

15 double dumbbell step-ups over 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

 

Notes

This workout has two tempos. It is a repeated triplet with the second time having half the reps of the first time. In the 1st triplet go with a big set and some smaller sets with short rest between. On the second triplet try to go unbroken for as long as possible, break 1-2 reps before you feel like reaching muscle failure, take minimum rest and continue.

Engine

A1. Row 4 Minutes @ easy pace

 

Rest 1 minute

 

A2. Row 2 Minutes @ 2km PR pace + 16 seconds

 

B1. Row 3000 meters @ 2km PR pace + 16 seconds

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B2. Row 1500 meters @ 2km PR pace + 13 seconds

 

Rest 2 minutes

 

B3. Row 750 meters @ 2km PR pace + 7 seconds

 

Rest 1 minute

 

B4. Row 375 meters @ Max effort

 

Notes

Row 4 minutes at an easy pace and another 2 minutes at the main session’s starting pace as warm-up. In the main session, the goal is to work on consistency by maintaining the given stroke rates and paces and finish with a max effort set. As distance decreases, pace and strokes increase.

The stroke rate for each distance is:

3000 meters @ 22/min

1500 meters @ 24/min

750 meters @ 28/min

375 meters @ free

Row 4 minutes at very light pressure as cool down.
