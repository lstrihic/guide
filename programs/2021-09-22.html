Power Snatch

Daily Max

Then

5×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

2 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 4 half squat snatch balances @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Build up to a heavy lift for the day by progressively increasing weight with sets of 5 reps < 50%, 3 reps < 70%, 2 reps < 80% and a few solid single lifts until the max.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull and a solid overhead receiving position. Don’ t be afraid to receive the barbell at a half squat position, keep you feet width narrow when receiving.

5 power cleans 102/70kg – 225/155lb

50 double dumbbell shoulders to overhead 22,5/15kg

5 power cleans 102/70kg – 225/155lb

50 box-jumps over 61/50cm – 24/20”

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Warm up

Build up to 102/70kg – 225/155lb power clean

Then,

15 box step-ups

20 shoulder taps

25 push-ups

20 double dumbbell push presses 12,5/5kg – 27,5/12,5lb.

15 box jump overs

Goal

The goal is to move efficiently through the total volume. Be smart about breaking the exercises and achieve solid power cleans at a steady tempo.

Movements

Go the first box-jump overs at a steady tempo and break the shoulder to overhead and the handstand push-ups into a big (> 15 reps) and some smaller sets with short to minimum rest between. On the second sequence of exercises try to maintain the same or faster tempo than the first one. Follow a steady tempo on the power cleans that will ensure solid successfully made lifts.

Options

102/70kg – 225/155lb —> 83/61kg – 185/135lb.

Front Squat

2×5 @ 70%

2×3 @ 75%

2×2 @ 80%

3×1 @ 85%

1×10 @ 60%

 

Warm up

Sets of 4-5 reps until you reach 70%.

Goal

Improve form. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets. On the high-rep set make sure you breathe well and keep a steady tempo.

Movements

Focus on a straight back, a solid front rack position and slow controlled squats.

Gymnastics Conditioning

AMRAP 8 Minutes of

1.2.3..etc reps of

Rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

14 single-leg squats

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

6 rope pull-ups

12 narrow squats

1 rope climb

8 goblet narrow squats 24/16kg

Goal & Movements

Move efficiently on the rope finding solid footings to squat and assist your upper body. Try to reach full range of motion in each extension on the rope and reach the top with as few extensions as possible. Descend smoothly by sliding down, extending and jumping on the ground. Go the single-leg squats at a steady tempo.

Options

Single-leg squats —> goblet narrow squats 24/16kg

Engine

A. Perform @ 75-80%

Bike-erg, 3km

300 double-unders

Ski-erg, 1,5km

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B.  Perform @ 80-85%

Bike-erg, 2km

200 double-unders

Ski-erg, 1km

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

C.  Perform @ 85-90%

Bike-erg, 1km

100 double-unders

Ski-erg, 0,5km

 

Goal & Movements

Try to achieve high performance in each portion. Maintain a steady sustainable pace on the bike erg, a slightly faster pace on the ski-erg and go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible. In each portion the work volume decreases allowing the body to work inside different energy zones. Go part A at 75-80% of VO2MAX , part B at 80-85% of VO2MAX and part C at 85-90% of VO2MAX. The use of a heart monitor is recommended.
