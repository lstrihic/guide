Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Prefer to work on split jerks. Focus on an explosive push and a deep split receiving position. Repositioning the barbell smoothly on the shoulders is also important, make sure you keep in control. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Shoulders Press

3×10 @ 60%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight with sets of 5 reps until you reach 50%. Improve strength and muscle quality. Focus on a strict push without utilising your hips and legs. In order to push through each set efficiently you need to have a proper front rack position with the barbell resting on the shoulders and not on the arms. Rest no more than 90 seconds between sets.

C. For time

3 rounds of

Row, 10 calories

8 toes to bar

4 snatches 83/61kg – 180/135lb.

Cap: 8 minutes

 

Notes

This session consists of 3 portions, try to achieve maximum performance in each one of them. Reps seem to decrease with each portion yet rounds increase so volume is the same with emphasis given to the lifts. Move fast and explosively every time you are on the rower and go the toes to bar unbroken. The challenge is the snatches. Go the lightweight snatches with a big set of touch and go reps and continue with fast singles (minimum rest between). On the heavier loads prefer to go with single lifts at a fast tempo by lifting the bar as soon as it stabilizes on the ground.

Barbell Cycling

For time

50/35 calories, Assault bike

5 clean and jerks @ 70% of Max Power Clean

4 clean and jerks @ 75%

3 clean and jerks @ 80%

2 clean and jerks @ 85%

1 clean and jerk @ 90%

 

Notes

The goal is to move efficiently with the barbell under metabolic fatigue. Start the session by completing 50 for men/35 for women calories on the bike, maintain a fast pace, 68+/55+ RPM then go straight to the lifts. It is recommended that you push through the lifts with drop & go technique. Perform single lifts with minimum rest between. Focus on 2 things, 1st maintain a steady tempo throughout, resting time of heavier lifts should be as equal as possible to that of lighter lifts, 2nd perform fast weight increases, make sure it doesn’t get you too long to continue to the heavier weight.

Gymnastics Complex

3 sets of

1.2.3..etc reps of

Strict ring pull-ups

Strict ring muscle-ups

Strict ring dips

Rest as needed betweens sets

 

Notes

Each set is a complex which you have to go unbroken. Focus on form and full range of motion. Start with 1 strict ring pull-up + 1 strict ring muscle-up + 1 strict ring dip + 2 + 2 + 2 + 3 +3 + …… until failure, rest as needed and start the next set in the same way. Take adequate rest to perform well in each next set.

Options

Strict —> kipping option

*go with kipping if you can’t perform at least 2 strict ring muscle-ups.

**skip muscle-ups and work on strength if you still cant perform any ring muscle-up.
