60 calories, Row

Build up to 61/34kg – 135/95kg of power clean

Then,

8.6.4 reps of

Down ups

Hand release push-ups

Ground to overhead20/10kg – 45/25lb. plate

Goal & Movements

Maintain a steady manageable pace on the rower that will allow you to prime. Pace the bar-facing burpees over and the box-jumps over and try to breathe well. Break the power cleans into touch and go sets and the bar muscle-ups into a big and 1-2 smaller manageable sets. The hardest part is the wall-walks, push steadily through the 10 reps with focus on full range of motion and short rest between reps


Options

Scale down to chest to bar if you cant perform at least 5 unbroken reps of bar muscle-ups while fresh.

Warm up

15 meters, Bear crawl

6 sandbag deadlifts70/50kg – 150/110lb.

9 handstand shoulder taps

12 hand release push-ups

3 sandbag ground to shoulders

5 meters, Handstand walk

9 strict hand stand push-ups


Goal

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Perform smooth transitions between exercises that will allow you to focus on quality of movements.


Movements

For those that are good on gymnastics the challenge will be to maintain a fast yet steady tempo on the sandbag ground to shoulders. For those who are heavier and not that good on gymnastics will be to push efficiently through the handstand push-ups on parallettes. Go hard on the exercise that you are good at and try to push steadily through the other. Break the handstand walk into 10-meter unbroken distances. focus on taking one-breath rest between these distances.


Options

Handstand walk —> 5 wall-walks

*Go with wall-walks if you cant perform at least 5 unbroken meters of Hs walk while fresh.

Parallettes —> plates —> regular handstand push-ups

**Scale down to an option that you can easily perform 5 unbroken reps while fresh.
