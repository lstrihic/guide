Squat Clean & Jerk

2×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve maximal strength and stability. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell, a solid deep receiving position and an explosive split jerk. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

Row, 1500 meters

Then,

3 rounds of

24 chest to bar pull-ups

12 devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Cap: 11 minutes

 

Notes

Row at a fast yet sustainable pace, 1’45”-1’52” for men | 1’58”-2’05” for women, then go straight to the couplet. Ideally go the chest to bar pull-ups unbroken with butterfly technique and push steadily through the devils presses with focus on dumbbell mechanics. Alternatively, break the chest to bar pull-ups into a big and a smaller set (kipping or butterfly) with minimum rest between.

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb. —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.

Gymnastics

4 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

Buy in: 100 double-unders

Max reps, Burpee ring muscle-ups

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve maximum performance in each set. Start with the double-unders, go unbroken for as long as possible, then go straight to the burpee ring muscle-ups. The burpee ring muscle-up is an exercise that can be performed by tempo so try to follow a fast yet sustainable tempo that will ensure no failed attempts. Increase effort with each set.

Options

Burpee ring muscle-ups —> burpee chest to rings pull-ups
