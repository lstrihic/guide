Squat Snatch

3×1 @ 70%

2×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

1×1 @ 100%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight until you reach 70%. Improve maximal strength and confidence. There is a total of 10 sets, go with a set every minute in EMOM style. Complete the lift and then wait until the next minute starts to continue with the next lift. Focus on o fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position.

—

 

Snatch Pull

5×1 @ 100% of Max Squat Snatch

 

Notes

Go straight to the snatch pull from the weight that you left the squat snatch. Improve pulling power. Rest no less than 90 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

40 GHD sit-ups

60 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

20 toes to bar

40 meters, Single arm dumbbell overhead lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

20 toes to bar

60 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

40 GHD sit-ups

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Notes

This workout has two sequences of the same exercises in an inverse order. Move at two tempos, maintain a steady pace until you complete the 40 meters of lunges and the start pushing harder to go for the strong finish. Go the GHD sit-ups unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take minimum rest and continue.  Ideally go the wall-balls and the toes to bar unbroken, alternatively break from the start into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. On the single arm dumbbell overhead lunges break into 10-meter unbroken distances with focus on a solid overhead position and steady tempo.

Then

5.10.15 reps of

Double kettlebell front squats 24/16kg – 53/35kg

Notes

Focus on proper form and technique. Perform the kettlebell exercises unbroken with focus on movement mechanics and go straight to the box-jump overs moving at a steady sustainable tempo. On the deficit handstand push ups go unbroken for as long as proper form allows, if you feel your midline breaks down or you are reaching failure, stop, take short rest and continue.

Engine

A. Row 1000m

*using the warm up routines. Change every 250 meters

 

B. Main session

B1. Row 2500 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 16 seconds

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B2. Row 2500 meters @ 22 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 14 seconds

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B2. Row 2500 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

Pace: 2km PR pace + 12 seconds

 

Notes

Row 1000 meters at light pressure using the warm up routines as warm up (0-250 meters only arm pulls, 250-500 only leg pulls, 500-1000 regular rowing). The goal in the main session is to be consistent in each set maintaining steady strokes and pace for each 2500-meter distance. In part B1 keep steady 24 strokes and try to achieve a 2km PR pace +16 seconds, in the B2 part 22 strokes and 2km PR pace + 14 seconds and in the B3 part 20 strokes and 2km PR pace + 12 seconds. Cool down by rowing 4 minutes at very light pressure.
