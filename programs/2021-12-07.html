Power Snatch

3×2 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

1×3 @ 80%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

4 snatch pulls + 3 hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power snatches until 75%.

Goal

Improve confidence and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull and a steady receiving position. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees, shoulders tight and elbows locked out.

—

 

Snatch High Pull

1×3 @ 80% of Max Power Snatch

1×3 @ 90%

3×3 @ 100%

 

Warm up

Continue to the snatch high pull from the percentage that you stopped the power snatch.

Goal

Improve pulling strength and angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell as high as possible. Do not force a pull with your arms yet make sure you drive the barbell as high and close as you can to your shoulders and as near as you can to your body’s vertical line.

Conditioning

For time

4 rounds of

Run, 400 meters

3 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

15 meters, Front rack lunges 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

400 meters, Run @ easy pace

Then,

2 rounds of

4 rope pull-ups

10 meters, Bodyweight lunges

2 rounds of

1 rope climbs

5 meters, Front rack lunges 43/25kg – 95/55lb.

Goal

Try to push steadily through each round with smooth transitions and technical rope climbs, and give all you got in the last round.

Movements

Run at a steady sustainable pace throughout the session that will allow you to breathe and perform well on the other exercises. On the rope climbs, be technical, find solid footings and squat on the rope to assist your upper body when ascending. Descend smoothly by sliding down, extending your body and jumping on the ground. Go the front rack lunges by 7,5-meter unbroken distances with focus on a solid front rack position.

Options

52/37kg – 115/80lb. —> 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

Strength Stamina

3 sets of

9 clean and jerks 83/52kg – 185/115lb.

7 squat cleans 83/52kg – 185/115lb.

5 thrusters 83/52kg – 185/115lb.

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Warm up

Sets of

3 clean and jerks + 2 squat cleans + 1 thrusters until 83/52kg – 185/115lb.

Goal & Movements

Move fast and efficiently with the barbell in each set. Ideally start the clean & jerks with a touch & go set, the size of the set depends on your comfort with the barbell, and switch to solid single lifts once you notice that touch and go technique is not efficient anymore or impedes your performance. Continue to the squat cleans with single lifts at the same steady tempo and once you complete the squat cleans catch a short breath and go for an unbroken set of thrusters. Alternatively, go from the start with single lifts and break the thrusters into no more than 2-3 sets with minimum rest between.

Options

83/52kg – 185/115lb. —>70/43kg – 155/95lb.
