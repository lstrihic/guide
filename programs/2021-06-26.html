Shoulder Press

EMOM 5 Minutes of

6 reps @ 70%

 

Notes

Warm up

3×8-10 shoulder presses @ 20/15kg Barbell

Then,

Sets of 5-6 shoulder presses until 70%.

Goal

Improve muscle quality. Complete 5 shoulder presses in each minute and rest until the next minute starts.

Movements

Focus on a strict press with your hips and legs tight and still, utilise only your shoulders.
