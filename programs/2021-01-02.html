Notes

With a running clock complete 20 double-unders and 1 squat snatch every 90 seconds. Once you complete the DUs and the snatch, add weight on the bar and rest until the next 90-second window start (1:30, 3:00, 4:30, 6:00 …etc). Perform the DUs fast and unbroken, then boost your confidence and go for a solid and successfully made lift.

Strongman

For form

15 reps of

Heavy tyre flips

 

Notes

Work with a heavy tyre. Focus on proper form and technique. Rest as needed between reps in order to work on the quality of the movement. First establish a solid grip, perform a deadlift and load the tyre on your thighs, then with an explosive pull bring it near your chest and push it forward. Remember that the pull is not vertical, it is vertical and forward.

Strength/Gymnastics

For time

60 meters, Double kettlebell front rack lunges 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

60 meters, Handstand walk

Cap: 10 minutes

 

Notes

Mark a 10-meter distance and go back and forth until you complete the total distance. Try to go the 10 meters unbroken, move steadily with a proper and solid front rack position on the lunges and move fast with short and quick steps on the handstand walk. Take short yet adequate rest between distances that will allow you to move efficiently and attempt the unbroken.

Options

24/16kg – 53/35lb → 20/12kg – 44/26lb

Handstand walk → 10 wall-walks
