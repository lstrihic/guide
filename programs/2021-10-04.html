Power Snatch

1×3 @ 70%

2×2 @ 80%

2×2 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

3×1 @ 95%

1×1 @ 85%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

4 snatch pulls + 3 hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 power snatches until 70%.

Goal

Improve confidence and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull and a steady receiving position. Keep the barbell close to your body’s vertical line. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees, shoulders tight and elbows locked out.

—

 

Snatch High Pull

2×3 @ 90% of Max Power Snatch

2×2 @ 95%

2×2 @ 100%

 

Warm up

Continue to the snatch high pull from the percentage that you left the power snatch.

Goal

Improve technique and weightlifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled pull until the barbell is over the knees, then increase speed and drive the barbell as high as possible.

Conditioning

For time

15 GHD sit-ups

3 squat cleans 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

1 legless rope climb 4,6m – 15ft.

3 clean & jerks 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

Rest 60 seconds between sets

 

Warm up

Build up to 70/52kg – 155/115lb. of squat clean & jerk

Then,

3.5.7 reps of

V sit-ups

Rope pull-ups

Goal

Try to achieve high and equal performance in each set. Move efficiently through the session with a steady tempo and smooth transitions between exercises.

Movements

Go the GHD sit-ups unbroken, prefer to go the squat cleans with single lifts and minimum rest between them and the clean & jerks unbroken with touch and go technique. On the rope climbs be technical, ascend fast using your legs to assist your upper body (kick or kipping technique) and descend smoothly by sliding down, extending your body and jumping on the ground.

Options

70/52kg – 155/115lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Legless —> regular rope climb 4,6m – 15ft.

Gymnastics Skill

Practice free handstand hold

 

Goal

Work for 10-15 minutes on free handstand hold. Mark a 1-square meter distance and work inside this area.

Movements

Focus on a straight body with your arms and legs straight and extended, your shoulders tight and toes pointing upwards. You can use your arms to find balance but ideally remain still at the handstand position for as long as you can.

Goal & pace

Try to achieve maximum performance in each part. Move at a steady tempo on the burpees over rower throughout the session without any tempo variations.
