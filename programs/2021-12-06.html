Warm up

3×8 shoulders presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3 reps until 70%.

Goal

Improve strength. Rest no less than 60 seconds between sets.

Movements

Keep your legs and hips straight and still and focus on using only your shoulders to push the barbell overhead. On the last set of 3 reps at 70% follow a certain tempo, go with a slow push of 3 seconds, a fast recovery on the shoulders, and 1 second rest on the shoulders.

Then,

2 rounds of

8 down ups

6 hang power cleans @ 43/25kg – 95/55lb.

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set. First complete the distance on the bike and then continue with as many reps as you can of the couplet within the 4-minute time frame.

Movements

Maintain a sustainable pace on the bike-erg that will allow you to prime and perform well on the couplet. Start the power cleans with touch and go technique and don’t hesitate to switch to drop and go as you push through the session and reps increase with each round. Go the handstand push-ups unbroken for as long as you can, then start breaking into manageable sets in order to keep moving efficiently.

Gymnastics Skill

Practice free handstand push-ups

 

Goal

Work for 10-15 minutes on free handstand push ups. Draw 3 marks on the ground where your head and hands should touch as they form a triangle base and try to work using these marks as guide to position your body during reps.

Movements

Establish a solid base (form of triangle) with your hands and head on the ground and then use your hips and legs to kip and push your body upwards, once you establish the handstand position with your body straight and extended return to the triangle base on the ground.

Options

Practice Handstand push-ups with face against wall

Engine

10 rounds of

Row, 16/13 calories

8 burpees over rower – lateral

Row, 16/13 calories

64 double-unders

 

Goal

Try to maintain a steady pace throughout the 10 rounds at approximately 80-85% of VO2Max. The more you are able to maintain your transitions from the first to the last round equal, the better your performance will be.

Pace & Movements

Start explosively on the rower each time and quickly reach a pace 1350+ for men | 1050+ for women. Go the burpees at a fast steady pace throughout the session and the double-unders unbroken. Your transitions should be smooth and equal throughout, getting smoothly on and off the rower will surely make a difference.
