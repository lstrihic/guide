Daily Max

Warm up

3 sets of

4 shoulder presses + 3 push presses + 2 split jerks @ 20/15kg barbell


Goal

Build up to the heavy lift for the day with sets of 3 reps < 75%, 2 reps < 85% and a few sets of single lifts > 85%. Don’t force a max weight lift, make sure you lift the heaviest weight comfortably. Once you find the daily max drop down to 75% and work on technique and explosiveness.


Movements

Focus on an explosive push, a deep and fast drive under the barbell and a solid reception. Keep your mind on your foot work and the stability of your shoulders.

Warm up

3×6-8 deadlifts @ 20/15kg barbell


Goal

Build up to a comfortable heavy lift for the day with sets of 5 reps < 60%, 3 reps < 80% and a few sets of single lifts > 80%. Once you find the daily max drop down to 70% and work on form and tempo.


Movements
