Jerk

2×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

1×1 @ 97%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 4-5 reps until you reach 70%. Improve confidence and stability. Focus on an explosive push and a solid deep receiving position. If you have put work and are comfortable with split jerk go with that, if not go with push jerk. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets. Use racks or high blocks.

—

 

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

15 toes to bar

15 overhead squats 43/30kg – 95/65lb

Then,

3 rounds of

15 chest to bar pull-ups

15 power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb

Cap: 11 minutes

 

Notes

This workout consists of 2 consecutive couplets. In the first couplet, ideally go each exercise unbroken, alternatively break the toes to bar into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between. Prefer to go the overhead squats unbroken at a steady, sustainable pace with controlled squats. In the second couplet, go each exercise unbroken with touch and go reps on the snatches. If you need to break choose either the chest to bar pull-ups or the snatches depending on your comfort with each exercise.

Strength Stamina

Deadlifts

30 unbroken reps @ 60%

20 unbroken reps @ 70%

10 unbroken reps @ 80%

 

Notes

The goal is to go unbroken for as long as possible. Do your best to complete each percentage unbroken with touch and go reps. Focus on proper form, utilise your legs to decompress your back and rest at the top of the deadlift if needed. You may have someone increasing weight on the bar for you. The use of weightlifting straps is highly recommended!

Gymnastics

A. For form

60 meters, Handstand walk

20 ring muscle ups

 

Rest 5 minutes

 

B. For form

20 ring muscle ups

60 meters, Handstand walk

 

Notes

Work on efficiency and adopt to the difficulty of each movement when it is the exercise that follows. Go the handstand walk by 10-meter unbroken distances, move fast with quick steps and proper handstand form (toes pointing upwards, solid midline). On the ring muscle ups go unbroken for as long as proper form allows, stop 2-3 reps before you feel like reaching failure rest shortly and continue with the remaining reps.

Options

60 → 45 → 30 meters of

Handstand walk → 10 wall-walks

*Go with wall walks  if you can’t perform at least 7,5 unbroken meters handstand walk while fresh

20 → 15 → 10 reps of

Ring muscle-ups → chest to ring pull-ups

**Choose a number of reps that you can perform unbroken while fresh. If you can’t do at least 5 unbroken reps then switch to chest to rings pull-ups

Engine

A. 15 minutes @ easy pace

 

B. Every 3 Minutes for 36 Minutes of (12 sets)

400 meters @ 1’21”-1’36”

Notes

Run 15 minutes (warm-up) and 5 minutes (cool down) at a comfortable pace of your choice. Perform 30-meter distances of front swings, side cross swings, front leg raises, toe taps …etc and finish with 3×50 meter sprint. Each time complete the sprint and rest until the next 3 minute mark. Focus on consistency, aim to achieve high performance in all sets working up to 93% of Vo2 Max. Try to continuously increase the effort.

Options

12 → 10 → 8 sets of

400 meters @ 1’21”-1’36”
