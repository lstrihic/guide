Conditioning

AMRAP 20 Minutes of

2 ring muscle-ups

4 devils presses 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

8 single-leg squats

 

Notes

The goal is to move efficiently and continuously through the total volume. Pace the biggest part of the workout, 15+ minutes, and then push harder for the strong finish. Go each exercise unbroken, the key is the transitions. The more you keep your transitions fast and equal throughout the session the better your performance will be. Ideally, as you reach the finish pick up your pace and speed up your transitions.

For time

Chest to bar pull-ups

10.8.6.4.2 reps of

Bench presses 90/55kg – 200/120lb

 

Notes

Focus on kipping technique and efficiency. Ideally go each exercise unbroken for as long as proper form allows, alternatively break each exercise into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. Go with smooth transitions that will allow you to perform the exercises efficiently. Descending reps give you the psychological advantage so make sure you go for it.
