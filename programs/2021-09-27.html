Warm up

3 sets of

2 snatch pulls + 3 hang snatch high pulls + 4 hip power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hip power snatches until 60%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

First pull the barbell to hip height and establish a solid position, then slightly bend your knees and explosively extend your body with a powerful shoulder shrug to drive the barbell overhead. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to the hip crease.

2×3 @ 70% of Max Power Snatch

1×2 @ 80%

1×2 @ 90%

3×2 @ 95%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

2 pulls to low hang + 3 low hang snatch high pulls @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 low hang snatch high pulls until 70%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Start with a slow controlled pull of the barbell until 10cm below the knees, then focus on an explosive and technical high pull keeping the barbell always near your body’s vertical line. Drive the barbell smoothly back to the starting position of the movement (below the knees).

Gymnastics Skill

Practice free handstand push-ups

 

Goal

Work for 10-15 minutes on free handstand push ups. Draw 3 marks on the ground where your head and hands should touch as they form a triangle base and try to work using these marks as guide to position your body during reps.

Movements

Establish a solid base (form of triangle) with your hands and head on the ground and then use your hips and legs to push your body upwards. Once you establish the handstand position with your body straight and extended return to the triangle base on the ground.

Options

Practice handstand push-ups with face against wall

100/80 calories, Assault bike

200 double-unders

Max reps, Bar-facing burpees over

Rest 4 minutes between sets

 

Goal & pace

Try to achieve maximum performance in each set. Move steadily on the bike at a pace 65+ for men | 58+ for women and go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take a breath and immediately continue with the remaining reps. On the bar-facing burpees over maintain a pace that you can efficiently sustain for the whole remaining time of the set.
