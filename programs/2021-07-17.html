Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat

2×2+2 @ 60% of Max Snatch Balance

1×2+2 @ 65%

1×1+2 @ 70%

1×1+2 @ 75%

1×1+1 @ 80%

3×1+1 @ 85%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

2 back presses + 3 push presses + 4 back jerks + 3 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats until 60%.

Goal

Improve technique and stability. The lifts at 60% are 2 times of 2 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive push press, a fast drive under the barbell and slow controlled overhead squats. Try to find the ideal for you snatch width grip.

Conditioning

AMRAP 15 Minutes of

15 deadlifts 102/70kg – 225/155lb.

10 meters, Handstand walk

15 double dumbbell hang power cleans 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

10 meters, Double dumbbell shoulders rack lunges  22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

 

Warm up

Build up to 102/70kg – 225/155lb.

Then,

2-3 rounds of

8 deadlifts

5 meters, Handstand walk

8 double dumbbell hang power cleans

5 meters, Double dumbbell shoulders rack lunges

Goal

Pace and move steadily through the biggest part of the workout, 10+ minutes, then start pushing each exercise harder and shorten your transitions to go for the strong finish.

Movements

Go the deadlifts with a big and a smaller set with short rest between. On the double dumbbell hang power cleans ideally go unbroken, alternatively by no more than 2-3 sets, focus on proper mechanics. Each 10 meter distance should be performed divided into 5 meter unbroken distances, 2,5 meters = 1 rep.

Options

102/70kg – 225/155lb.

5 meters of Handstand walk —> 1 wall walk

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb —> 15/10kg – 35/25lb.

Shoulders Press

1×5 @ 70%

1×3 @ 80%

2×3 @ 85%

2×2 @ 90%

2×1 @ 95%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

6-8 shoulder presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 3-4 reps until 70%.

Goal

Improve pushing strength. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a strong push using only your shoulders and smooth rebounding of the barbell on them on the way down. Avoid utilising your lower body, hips and leg should remain straight and still.

Max Effort

For time

30 box-jumps over 61/50cm – 24/20”

20 power clean 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

10 thrusters 61/43 – 135/95lb.

Cap: 5 minutes

 

Warm up

3 sets of

10 box-jump overs

8 power cleans

6 thrusters

Rest 60-90 seconds between sets

Goal & Movements

Move steadily through the box-jumps over at a steady sustainable pace. Ideally go the power cleans with no more than 2-3 unbroken touch and go sets, alternatively go with a big set and continue with single lifts with minimum rest between. Go the thrusters unbroken for as long as you can.

Engine – Running

4 sets of

1200 meters @ 3’40”-4’20”/km

Rest 3 minutes between

 

Options

1200 → 1000 → 800 meters @ 3’45”-4”30”/km

Warm up

10 minutes @ easy pace

Goal

The 1200 meters is a distance that can easily gas you out and leave you out of breath. Make sure you select a pace manageable enough to increase in each set or maintain for the whole volume (4 sets).

Cool down

5 minutes @ very easy pace
