Warm up

3 sets of

3 back presses + 2 back jerks + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

then

Sets of 2 back jerks + 2 overhead squats until 60%.

Goal

Improve technique and stability. The lifts at 60% are 2 times of 2 back jerks + 1 overhead squat. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a an explosive push, a solid overhead reception and slow controlled squats.

30 meters, Handstand walk

60 handstand push-ups

5 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

30 meters, Handstand walk

10 devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

3 rounds

3 devils press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

5-10 meters, Handstand walk

1 rope climb

6 handstand push-ups

Goal

There are two identical portions divided by a high volume of handstand push-ups. Move efficiently through the total volume, pace until you are over the 60 handstand push-ups and then start increasing your effort tp a max effort as you reach the finish.

Movements

Move steadily on the devils presses, break the handstand walk into 10 meter distances and go the handstand push-ups with a big and as fewer smaller sets as possible. Move fast and efficiently on the rope by establishing solid footings and squatting to assist your upper body, descent smoothly.

Options

Handstand walk —> 6 wall-walks

Engine – Rowing

A. Row, 3000 meters @ 20 strokes/minute

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. Row, 2000 meters @ 22 strokes/minute

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

C. Row, 1000 meters @ 24 strokes/minute

 

Notes

Pace

A: 2km PR pace +18 seconds

B: 2km PR pace +16 seconds

C: 2km PR pace + 10 seconds

Warm up

Row 4 minutes using the warm up routines

(1st minute only arm pulls, 2nd only leg pulls, 3rd and 4th minute regular rowing)

then

2 sets of

Row,  500 meters @ 20 strokes

Rest 2 minutes between sets

Goal

The goal in the main session is to efficiently increase your strokes and pace with each part. In the B1 part keep steady 20 strokes and try to achieve a 2km PR pace +18 seconds, in the B2 part 22 strokes and 2km PR pace +16 seconds and in the B3 part 24 strokes and 2km PR pace + 10 seconds.

Cool down

Row 4 minutes at very light pressure.
