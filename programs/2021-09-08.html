Conditioning

A. AMRAP 7 Minutes of

3 bar muscle-ups

10 power snatches 34/25kg – 75/55lb.

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. AMRAP 7 Minutes of

6 toes to bar

10 overhead squats 34/25kg – 75/55lb.

 

Warm up

2 rounds of

6 toes to bar

10 overhead squats

3 bar muscle-ups

10 power snatches

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in both parts. In each part move steadily for the biggest part of the workout, 5+ minutes, and then start pushing harder to go for the strong finish.

Movements

Go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between that will allow you to do so, as you reach the finish shorten your transitions to the minimum. Focus on a solid overhead position on the overhead squats and efficient touch and go technique on the snatches.

Options

A. Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

Gymnastics Conditioning

AMRAP 10 Minutes of

12 GHD sit-ups

1 pegboard ascent

 

Warm up

3 pegboard pull-ups

6 GHD sit-ups

6 pegboard pull-ups

12 GHD sit-ups

2 pegboard ascents

Goal & Movements

Focus on tempo and technical pegboard ascents. Go the GHD sit-ups unbroken at a steady tempo, on the peg-board focus on fast end efficient ascents using your whole body to create balance. Push your legs against the board for stability and perform swift arm movements with purpose to stay on the pegboard as less time as possible.

Options

Pegboard ascent —> half pegboard ascent —> 1 leg-less rope climb if there is no peg-board

Engine

A. 4 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

20/15 calories, Ski-erg

10 burpee box jump-overs 61/50cm – 24/20”

Max calories, Bike-erg

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. 4 sets of

AMRAP 3 Minutes of

20/15 calories, Bike-erg

10 burpee box jump-overs 61/50cm – 24/20”

Max calories, Ski-erg

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Goal & Movements

Try to achieve high performance in both parts. In each part, push fast through the couplet and then, in the remaining time, maintain a steady sustainable pace on the bike-erg/ski-erg. Complete the calories of the couplet fast and explosively, start very hard and create momentum, then maintain it until the completion of the calories. On the burpee box jump-overs maintain a steady, sustainable pace throughout the session. On the max calories maintain a fast yet sustainable pace depending on the remaining time you have left after the couplet.
