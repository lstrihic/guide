Squat clean and Jerk

1×2 @ 55%

1×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 55%. Improve explosiveness and technique. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell and a solid, deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

2 rounds of

50 toes to bar

50 hang power cleans 43/30kg – 95/65lb

50 box-jumps 60/50cm – 24/20”

50 push presses 43/30kg – 95/65lb

 

Notes

Go the first round fast yet steadily at a pace that will allow you to push the second round even faster. In the first round break each exercise into a big and some smaller manageable sets with short rest between. In particular prioritise to break the exercises in this order: toes to bars -> cleans-> push presses, from the one that is the most efficient to be broken to the less efficient. In the second round, ideally, go each exercise unbroken for as long as possible, meaning stop 2-3 reps before you feel like reaching muscle failure and try to shorten your transitions to the minimum. You might need to go with small, manageable sets, then have in mind that the better you manage to keep your in-between rest short the better you will perform.

Engine

2 sets of

Row, 5000 meters

Rest 10 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Row 1500 meters, 1000 meters at light pressure and 500 meters at a progressively increasing pressure, as warm up. Try to be consistent in 24-26 strokes/min in the first set and reach maximum performance in the second set.  Strokes is the main goal in first set. Time is the goal in second (free strokes rate). Row 500 meters at very light pressure as cool down.
