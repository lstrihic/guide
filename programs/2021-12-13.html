Hang Power Snatch

2×2 @ 65% of Max Hang Power Snatch

2×2 @ 70%

2×2 @ 75%

2×2 @ 80%

2×2 @ 85%

Warm up

3 sets of

2 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 4 half squat snatch balances @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power snatches until 65%.


Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

First pull the barbell to hip height, then drive the barbell to a position over the knees and pull explosively with the barbel close to your body into a solid receiving position. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to the hip crease and avoid spending too much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position. In each set reposition swiftly and continue to the second rep.

2×1 @ 85%

Warm up

2 sets of

1 hang squat snatch + 2 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang squat snatches until 65%.


Goal

Improve explosiveness and confidence. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.


Movements

Focus on a solid pull until the barbell is at hip height, then drive the barbell to a position over the knees, pull explosively with the barbel close to your body (proper lifting angles) and focus on a fast drive into a deep receiving position.

Conditioning

A. AMRAP 10 Minutes of

30 double-unders

15 power snatches 35/25kg – 75/55lb.

Rest 5 minutes

B. AMRAP 10 Minutes of

6 bar-facing burpees over

12 overhead squats 35/25kg – 75/55lb.


Warm up

3 rounds of

20 double-unders

10/8 power snatches 20/15kg empty barbell

5 burpees

10/8 overhead squats 20/15kg


Goal

Move steadily through the biggest part of each AMRAP, 6+ minutes, then start pushing harder to reach the finish strong.

Movements

Ideally go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between them that will allow you to keep moving efficiently. In part A use touch and go technique on the snatches and try to go unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest. In part B maintain a steady sustainable pace on the burpees and go the overhead squats unbroken.
