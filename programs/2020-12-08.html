Conditioning

AMRAP 20 Minutes of

30 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb

20 toes to bar

30 box-jumps 60/50cm – 24/20”

20 deadlifts 124/80kg – 275/175lb

30 handstand push-ups

20 chest to bar pull-ups

 

Notes

The goal is to move steadily through the 20 minutes. Pace the biggest part of the workout (about 15 minutes) and then start pushing harder shortening the transitions between exercises and go for the strong finish. Break each exercise into no more than 2-3 manageable sets with short rest between.

Options

124/80kg – 275/175lb → 102/70kg – 225/155lb

Handstand stand push-ups → push-ups hand release

Chest to bar pull-ups → pull-ups

Barbell Cycling

A. For form

30 power snatches @ 60% (10×3)

 

B. For form

20 power snatches @ 75% (10×2)

 

C. For form

10 power snatches @ 90% (Singles)

 

Notes

The goal is to work efficiently with the bar. Move steadily with focus on proper form and breathing. Go the 60% lifts with touch & go sets of 3 reps, the 75% lifts with touch and go sets of 2 reps and the 90% lifts with singles. Increase load on the bar between portions and rest while you do that. Focus on short and equal rest between sets or lifts throughout each portion.

Gymnastics

For form

10.8.6.4.2 reps of

Bar muscle-ups

20 meters, Handstand walk

 

Notes

Focus on quality of movement and technique. Break the handstand walk into 2×10 meter distances. Ideally go the bar muscle-ups unbroken and each 10-meter distance unbroken as well. Alternatively, break the muscle-ups of each round into a big and a smaller set. Take short rest between sets yet adequate enough to allow you move efficiently.

Options

10.8.6.4.2 → 5.4.3.2.1 reps of (scale to 5.4.3.2.1 reps if you can’t perform at least 5 unbroken reps while fresh)

Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups

20 → 15 → 10 meters of

Handstand walk → 2 wall-walks (go with wall-walks if you can’t perform at least 5 unbroken meters while fresh)
