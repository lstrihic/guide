Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Snatch Pull

3×3 @ 95% of Max Squat Snatch

 

Warm up

Continue from the percentage you left the squat snatch.

Goal

Improve pulling strength and weightlifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell over hip height.

Gymnastics Skill

Practice free handstand hold

 

Goal & Movements

Work for 10-15 minutes on free handstand hold. Mark a square meter area on the floor and practice the position inside this area. Ideally once you place your hands on the spot stay there with your body tight, extended and still. Sightly walking to reposition is allowed yet inside the marked area.

Engine

Work @ 75% of VO2Max*

Row, 40/30 calories

80 double-unders

12.14.16.18.20 reps of

Burpees over rower

 

Goal & pace

Move steadily throughout the session. It consists of 5 rounds of decreasing reps of burpees over rower. Maintain a steady sustainable pace on the rower 1250+ for men | 950+ for women that will allow you to breathe and go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take a short breath and immediately continue with the remaining reps. On the burpees over rower try to be just a bit faster in each next round than the previous one. *The use of a heart monitor is recommended.
