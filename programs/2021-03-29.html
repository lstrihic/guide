Hang Power Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve power and hang position. Focus on a solid, hang position and a powerful pull with an explosive shoulder shrug. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

—

 

Snatch High Pulls

2×3 @ 90%

2×2 @ 100%

 

Notes

Continue to the snatch high pulls from where you left the hang power snatches. Improve technique and explosiveness. Focus on proper weightlifting angles and full range of motion (elbows pointing high, barbell as high as possible). Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Front Squat

ΕΜΟΜ 10 Minutes

3 reps @ 70%

 

Notes

Build up to 70% with sets of 2-3 reps. Perform 3 front squats in every minute for a total duration of 10 minutes. Work on form and consistency. Focus on a straight back, a solid front rack and a steady tempo.

Gymnastics

Ring Muscle-ups

Max unbroken reps

Rest 3 minutes

Max unbroken reps

Rest 3 minutes

Max unbroken reps

 

Notes

This workout is 3 max-rep sets with 3 minutes rest between. Make sure you go each set unbroken until failure, your performance will drop each set but the goal is to put maximum effort each time.

Options

Ring muscle-ups → chest to rings pull-ups

*Go with chest to rings pull-ups if you can’t perform at least 8/6 unbroken ring muscle-ups while fresh.
