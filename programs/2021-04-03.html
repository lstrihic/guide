Power Snatch + Hang Power Snatch

Daily Max

 

Notes

Build up to a heavy lift for the day with sets of 2 times of 1 snatch + 1 hang power snatch < 85% and 1 time of 1 snatch + 1 hang power snatch > 85%. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid overhead position. Consider that the less you stay on the hang position the less tension you put on your back and grips. Rest no less than 90 seconds between complexes.

—

 

Power Clean + Hang Power Clean + Jerk

Daily Max

 

Notes

Build up to a heavy lift for the day with sets of 2 times of 1 power clean + 1 hang power clean + 1 jerk < 85% and 1 time of 1 power clean + 1 hang power clean + 1 jerk > 85%. Focus on an powerful pull and an explosive, technical jerk. Preferably go with push jerk and consider that the less you stay on the hang position the less tension you put on your back and grips. Rest no less than 90 seconds between complexes.

Conditioning

For time

Row, 60 calories

50 wall-ball shots 9/6kg – 20/14lb

40 toes to bar

30 metes, Front rack lunges 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Row, 30 calories

25 wall-ball shots 9/6kg – 20/14lb

20 toes to bar

15 meters, Front rack lunges 61/43kg – 135/95lb

 

Notes

This workout consists of 2 tempos. Start by pacing the first section and push the fewer reps second section really hard. Row at 1350+ for men / 1100+ for women, then go the wall-ball shots unbroken for as long as you can breaking 2-3 reps before you reach failure. Prefer to break the toes to bar into a big and some smaller sets with short to minimum rest between. Go the front rack lunges by 7,5-meter unbroken distances, focus on proper and solid repetitions. On the lower-rep section try to maintain equal pace on the rower and give all you got to go all other exercises unbroken.

Options

9/6kg – 20/14lb → 7/4 – 16/10lb

Toes to bar → knees to elbows

61/43kg – 135/95lb → 50/34kg – 110/75lb

Notes

Build up to 50% with sets of 5-6 reps. Alternate between high-rep sets at a steady 50% percentage and single reps with increasing load up to 90% of max. Max Effort

For time

3 legless rope climbs

2 thrusters 70/45kg – 155/100lb

2 legless rope climbs

4 thrusters 70/45kg – 155/100lb

1 legless rope climb

6 thrusters 70/45kg – 155/100lb

 

Notes

Move fast from the start and keep pushing hard until the finish. On the legless rope climbs ascend fast with swift, solid grasps and coordinated leg movements. Even bigger role play the descents, make sure you are technical and go with sliding, extending body and letting go of the rope. Ideally, go the thrusters unbroken for as long as possible with a short pause at the top position if you need to momentarily catch your breath. Alternatively break the 6 or 4-rep round into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between.

Options

Leg-less → regular rope climbs

70/45kg – 155/100lb → 61/43kg – 135/95lb
