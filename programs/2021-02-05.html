Squat Snatch

1×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×2 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Improve explosiveness and stability. Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Snatch Pulls

5×2 @ 95% of Max Squat Snatch

 

Notes

Continue to the snatch pulls from where you left the squat snatches. Work on pulling strength. Focus on a slow, strong starting pull and an explosive finish. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 5 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

30 meters, Handstand walk

75 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

12 ring muscle-ups

50 handstand push-ups

12 ring muscle-ups

75 wall-balls 9/6kg

30 meters, Handstand walk

Cap: 21 minutes

 

Notes

In this workout work at two tempo. Move at a fast yet manageable tempo until you complete the handstand push-ups, then start pushing harder to go for the strong finish. Go the handstand walk by 10 meter unbroken distances, move fast with quick step and proper hollow position. Break the first wall ball shots into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. Ideally go the ring-muscle up unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set. Go the handstand push ups with no more than 2-3 big sets with short rest between. Once you are done with the handstand push ups start pushing harder and push each exercise unbroken for as long as possible if you break take minimum rest and continue.

Strength/Gymnastics

For time

2 rounds of

20 calories, Assault bike

10 double dumbbell step-up overs 30/20kg – 65/45lb

20 strict handstand push-ups

10 double dumbbell power cleans 30/20kg – 65/45lb

 

Notes

Move fast in the first round, give it all you got in the second round. Push fast and explosively through the 20 calories on the assault bike. Prefer to break the step overs and power cleans of the first round into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between, go the same exercises in the second round unbroken for as long as possible. The strict handstand push-ups is the challenge, go the first round with a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with short rest between and push through the second round with small manageable sets with minimum rest between.

Engine

A. Row 4 minutes @ easy pace

 

A2. Row 300 meters @ 2km PR pace + 2 seconds

 

B. 5 sets of

Row, 1000 meters @ 26 strokes/minute

Rest 2 minutes between sets

Pace : 2km PR pace + 10 seconds

 

Notes

Warm up by rowing 4 minutes at an easy pace and light pressure and 300 meters at 2km PR pace + 2 seconds. In the main session try to achieve high performance in each set. Row 5×1000-meter distances at steady 26 strokes and a goal pace of km PR pace + 10 seconds. Focus primarily on steady strokes and secondarily on pace. Cool down by rowing 4 minutes at a very light pressure.
