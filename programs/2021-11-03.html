Power Snatch

2×2 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

4 snatch pulls + 3 hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power snatches until 60%.

Goal

Improve confidence and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull and a steady receiving position. Keep the barbell close to your body’s vertical line. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees, shoulders tight and elbows locked out.

Power Clean & Jerk

2×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power cleans & jerk until 65%.

Goal

Improve power and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled first pull until the knees, a powerful second pull and solid receiving positions. Prefer to go with split jerks in order to improve shoulder to overhead efficiency on heavy weights.

Conditioning

A. For time

150 double-unders

Then

20.15.10.5 reps of

Hang power cleans 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Bar-facing burpees over

Chest to bar pull-ups

Cap: 11 minutes

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. For time

150 double-unders

Then

15.10.5 reps of

Hang squat cleans 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Bar-facing burpees over

Bar muscle-ups

Cap: 11 minutes

 

Warm up

Build up to 61/43kg – 135/95lb. of hang squat clean

Then,

2 rounds of

30 double-unders

10 burpees

5 hang power cleans

15 double-Unders

6 burpees

3 hang squat cleans

Goal & Movements

This session consists of 2 portions, in the 2nd portion the volume decreases yet the difficulty of the exercises increases. Go the double-unders unbroken, if you break catch a short breath and immediately continue with the remaining reps. In both portions, try to go the gymnastic exercises unbroken and prefer to break the high-rep rounds of the lifts into a big and 1-2 smaller sets with short rest between and push the low-rep rounds harder. On the bar-facing burpees over maintain a steady solid tempo throughout the session.

Options

A. Chest to bar pull-ups —> pull-ups

*Go with pull-ups if you cant perform at least 10 unbroken chest to bar while fresh

B. Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

*Go with chest to bar if you cant perform at least 5 unbroken bar muscle-ups while fresh

Back Squat

1×3 @ 70%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 90%

1×3 @ 75%

1×2 @ 85%

1×1 @ 95%

1×3 @ 80%

1×2 @ 90%

1×1 @ 100%

 

Warm up

Sets of 3-4 reps until you reach 70%.

Goal

Improve form. Repetitions decrease and percentages increase every three sets in a repeating pattern and after the 3 sets the starting percentages of the pattern are slightly higher in order to vary the weight stimulus. Rest no less than 60 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a straight back and slow controlled squats at a steady tempo.

Gymnastics Conditioning

AMRAP 10 Minutes of

1 leg-less rope climb 4,6m – 15ft.

10 meters, Handstand walk

2 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

20 single leg squats

 

Warm up

3.6.9.12 reps of

Rope pull-ups

Hand release push-ups

Narrow squats

15 seconds, Handstand hold between rounds

Goal & Movements

Move fast and efficiently on the rope with proper coordination of upper and lower body, kicks or kipping, and descend smoothly by sliding down, extending your body and jumping on the ground.  On the regular rope climbs make sure your are extra technical, find solid footings to squat on the rope to assist your upper body. Go the 10-meters handstand walk fast and unbroken and move steadily on the single leg squats at a sustainable tempo.

Options

Leg-less —> 6 rope pull-ups

Handstand walk —> 2 wall-walks
