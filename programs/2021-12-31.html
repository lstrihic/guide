Hang Power Snatch + Hang Squat Snatch

1×2+1 @ 55% of Max Power Snatch

1×2+1 @ 60%

1×2+1 @ 65%

1×1+2 @ 70%

1×1+2 @ 75%

3×2+1 @ 80%


Warm up

2 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 overhead squats + 3 hang squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power snatches + 2 hang squat snatches until 55%.


Goal

Improve explosiveness and technique. The lifts at 55% are 2 hang power snatches + 1 hang squat snatch, at 75% are 1 hang power snatch + 2 hang squat snatches. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.


Movements

Start with a snatch deadlift, drive the barbell to the hang position and continue with explosive pulls. Focus on smooth driving of the barbell to the hang position and try to spend minimum time there.

Engine – Rowing

7 sets of

500 meters @ free rate

Rest 1’30” between sets

Pace : 2km PR pace + 4 seconds


Warm up

1000 meters @ easy pace

Rest 4 minutes

2 sets of

125 meters @ high rate (28+ strokes)

Rest 1 minute between sets


Goal

Perform 7 sets with 90 seconds rest between, follow the given pace and a free stroke rate to achieve that pace. Keep in mind that more strokes mean higher cardiovascular tax, fewer strokes mean the need of powerful strokes.
