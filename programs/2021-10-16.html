Goal & Movements

Move steadily through the biggest part of the session, 12+ minutes, and then push harder by shortening your transitions. Shoulders Press

Daily Max

Then

2×6 @ 70%

 

Warm up

3×8 shoulder presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Build up to a comfortable heavy lift for the day with sets of 5 reps < 60%, 3 reps < 75% and single lifts > 75%. Then drop down to 70% and work on consistency.

Movements

Focus on keeping your legs straight and stable using solely your shoulders to push the barbell overhead.

Max Effort

For time

3 rounds of

6 squat cleans 83/61kg – 185/135lb.

10 bar-facing burpees over

Cap: 4 minutes

 

Warm up

Build up to 83/61kg – 185/135lb. of squat clean

Then,

3 sets of

2 squat cleans

4 bar-facing burpees over

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal & Movements

Go the squat cleans from the start with single lifts at a fast, solid tempo with minimum rest between reps. On the  bar-facing burpees over maintain a steady, near max effort pace with focus on breathing well and give everything you got in the last round. The more you are able to keep your tempo on the lifts steady, the better your performance will be.

Options

83/61kg – 185/135lb. —> 70/52kg – 155/80lb.
