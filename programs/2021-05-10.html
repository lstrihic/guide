Notes

Warm up with sets of 2-3 reps until your reach 70%. Work for a total of 15 minutes. Complete the given number of lifts in each minute and wait until the next minute to start the next set. Increase weight during the 5th and the 10th minute. Go with single lifts at a steady tempo and focus on solid successfully made lifts, ideally finish within 20 seconds in each minute.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

Run, 400 meters

30 GHD sit-ups

12 overhead squats 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

 

Notes

Pace the first 2 rounds and push the last round harder. Run at a steady pace throughout and avoid any significant pace variances. Ideally, go the GHD sit-ups unbroken, alternatively break the first rounds into a big and a smaller set and go the last round unbroken for as long as possible. The most challenging part is the overhead squats and this is where the difference will be made. If you are comfortable with weightlifting, snatch and continue with solid overhead squats, if not prefer to clean and jerk the barbell to a solid overhead position. In any case the goal is to complete all reps unbroken without losing time and puting effort on re-lifting the barbell overhead.

Options

GHD sit-ups —> toes to rings

61/43kg – 135/95lb. —> 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

Gymnastics

For form

12 deficit handstand push-ups 10/5cm – 4/2”

4 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

12 deficit handstand push-ups 10/5cm – 4/2”

3 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

12 deficit handstand push-ups 10/5cm – 4/2″

2 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

12 deficit handstand push-ups 10/5cm – 4/2”

1 legless rope climb 4,6m – 15ft.

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. If 12 deficit handstand push-ups is an achievable set for you, then go unbroken for as long as proper form allows, if you find that your form is breaking or you getting significantly slower break, take a breath, and continue with the remaining reps. Alternatively, break from the start into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. On the rope make sure you use your legs, kicks or kipping to assist your upper body, descend smoothly by sliding down (rope between thighs), extending and jumping on the ground.

Options

Deficit —> regular handstand push-ups

Leg-less —> regular rope climbs

Engine

AMRAP 20 Minutes of

Row, 30/23 calories

20 bar-facing burpees over

100 double-unders

 

Notes

Move steadily through the biggest part of the workout, 15+ minutes, then start pushing harder to a max effort finish. Row at 1350+ for men | 1000+ for women with focus on technique and proper breathing, then go straight on the burpees. On the burpees maintain a fast yet sustainable pace throughout the session, increase only if are near the finish. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible, when you break take minimum rest and keep pushing through the remaining reps. Go with smooth transitions.
