Hang Power Snatch

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

2×2 @ 75%

2×2 @ 80%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 65%. Improve explosiveness and the hang position. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid overhead reception. Avoid staying too long at the hang as it taxes your arms and grips unnecessarily. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

—

 

Squat Snatch

1×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

2×1 @ 85%

2×2 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight until you reach 65%. Improve power and stability. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Notes

This workout consists of 3 portions, each one is a 10-minute timeframe. Complete the couplet of each portion and then wait until the timer reaches the 10:00 and 20:00 minute mark before you start the next portion. Ideally, go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between. Alternatively break the high rep rounds into a big and as few smaller sets as possible and try to go the low-rep sets unbroken for as long as you can. Prefer to break the gymnastics and push the barbell as it is less taxing and time consuming.

Options

B. Chest to bar pull-ups —> pull-ups

*Go with pull-ups if you can’t perform at least 10 unbroken chest to bar pull-ups

C. Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

*Go with chest to bar pull-ups if you can’t go at least 7 unbroken bar muscle-ups with fresh.

Back Squat

2×5 @ 70%

2×3 @ 75%

2×2 @ 80%

3×1 @ 85%

1×10 @ 60%

 

Notes

Build up with sets of 3-4 reps until you reach 70%. Focus on proper form and a strict bottom position (no bounce). Go the high-rep set at a steady tempo. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Gymnastics

For form

11.13.15 reps of

Strict handstand push-ups

15 meters, Handstand walk

Then,

15.13.11 reps of

Strict ring dips

15 meters, Hand stand walk

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and quality. Ideally go the strict exercises unbroken for as long as proper form allows, break when you feel that you don’t execute with proper form and quality. Alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. Go the handstand walk with 7,5-meter distances, walk fast with quick hand movements and a straight tight handstand position.

Notes

Start with 4000 meters on the rower at a steady sustainable pace and continue with as many reps as you can of the couplet.
