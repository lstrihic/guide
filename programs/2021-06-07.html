Snatch Balance

Daily Max

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and reach the heavy lift of the day with sets of 4 reps < 75%, 2 reps < 85% and single reps > 85%. Focus on solid lifts with focus on an explosive drive and a solid overhead position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Squat Snatch

2×3 @ 70%

2×2 @ 80%

2×2 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 70%. Improve stability and confidence. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

C. AMRAP 7 Minutes of

6 deadlifts 83/57kg – 180/125lb.

12 box-jumps 61/50cm – 24/20”

3 shoulders to overhead 83/57kg – 180/125lb.

12 chest bar pull-ups

 

Notes

This workout consists of 3 portions, in each portion barbell reps decrease while load increases. Try to achieve maximum performance in each one of them. Start by maintaining a fast steady pace, go each exercise unbroken with smooth transitions between and focus on breathing well, as you reach the end shorten your transitions and increase your pace to go for the strong finish. If going the barbell unbroken is not possible, prefer to break from the start into a big and a smaller set.
