Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 50%. Work on explosiveness. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

30 meters, Overhead lunges 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

3 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

21 thrusters 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

3 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

21 thrusters 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

3 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

30 meters, Overhead lunges 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

 

Notes

Go the overhead lunges by 10-meter unbroken distances, focus on a solid overhead position and steady tempo. On the thrusters, ideally, go unbroken, alternatively, break into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between. Move fast on the rope, find solid footings and squat to assist your upper body, descend by sliding down and jumping on the ground. Try to maintain a steady tempo throughout the session taking equal rest between rope climbs as you push through the total volume.

Gymnastics Conditioning

For form

8.12.16 reps of

Deficit handstand push-ups 10/5cm – 4/2”

16.24.32 reps of

Single-leg squats

75 double-unders between sets

 

Notes

Focus on form and full range of motion. Go with 8 deficit handstand push-ups + 16 single-leg squats + 75 double-unders + 12 + 24 + 75 + 16 + 32. Ideally, go the deficit handstand push-ups unbroken for as long as possible, if you feel that your midline breaks down, you are reaching muscle failure or your feet start being too wide, prefer to break, rest shortly and continue. Alternatively, break each round from the start into a big and a smaller set that will allow you to push efficiently through the reps. Go the single-leg squats at a steady sustainable tempo with focus on good reps.

Options

Deficit —> regular handstand push-ups
