Squat Clean

Daily Max

Then

2×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 3 front squats + 3 squat cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Build up to a heavy lift for the day with sets of 3-4 reps < 70%, 2 reps < 85 and single lifts > 85%. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets. Once you find the daily max drop down to 90% and work on consistency on heavy lifts.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position.

3×2 @ 90% of Max Squat Clean

2×2 @ 95%

2×1 @ 100%

 

Warm up

Continue from the percentage you left the squat clean.

Goal

Work on strength. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a slow pull at proper angles and finish with an explosive shoulder shrug. Barbell should remain close to your body’s vertical line.

Conditioning

A. AMRAP 9 Minutes of

15 box-jumps 61/50cm – 24/20”

10 hang power cleans 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

5 shoulder to overhead 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

 

Immediately into

 

B. 6 Minutes to find

1 RM Jerk

Warm up

Build up to about 90% of your 1 RM Jerk

Then,

2 rounds of

10 box step-ups

5 hang power cleans 20/15kg barbell

10 push ups

5 hang power cleans 20/15kg barbell

Goal

The goal is to put maximum effort in the AMRAP of the session and still manage to achieve a heavy lift as close to your PB as possible.

Movements

Move steadily through the biggest part of the session, 7+ minutes, and then push harder by shortening your transitions. Go the box jumps at a steady sustainable pace. Ideally, go the hang power cleans and shoulder to overhead unbroken and try to regulate your breathing during the box jumps. If this is not possible prefer to go each round with 9 hang power cleans, 1 hang power clean + 5 shoulder to overhead. Once you complete the AMRAP you have 6 minutes to find a 1RM Jerk. You can use racks or high blocks.

Bench Press

Daily Max

Then

3×8 @ 70%

 

Warm up

3×8 bench presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Goal

Build up to a heavy lift for the day with sets of 3-4 reps < 60%, 2-3 reps < 80 and single lifts > 80%. Rest no less than 60 seconds between sets. Once you find the daily max drop down to 70% and work on strength.

Movements

Focus on a strong controlled push with focus on full range of motion. Have a spotter to assist you with your lifts.
