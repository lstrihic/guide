Squat clean

EMOM 5 Minutes of

3 reps @ 65%

EMOM 5 Minutes of

2 reps @ 75%

EMOM 5 Minutes of

1 rep @ 85-95%

 

Notes

Work on tempo and confidence. This is a total volume of 15 minutes, perform single lifts throughout the whole session. The goal is solid, successfully made lifts and short, equal rest between them. Minute 4, 9 and 14 is where you increase weight on the bar. Warm up with an empty bar and continue with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 75%.

Conditioning

3 sets of

AMRAP 8 Minutes of

8 chest to bar pull-ups / toes to bar

7,5 meters, Overhead lunges 43/30kg – 95/65lb

8 bar-facing burpees over

7,5 meters, Overhead lunges 43/30kg – 95/65lb

Rest 4 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve maximum performance in each set. Each set is an 8-minute AMRAP in which you have to complete as many reps of the given exercises as you can. Make sure you alternate chest to bar pull-ups with toes to bar with every new round. 1st round CH2B, 2nd T2B, 3rd CH2B ..etc Move steadily throughout by going each exercise and distance unbroken. Take short rest between exercises, focus on technical gymnastic exercises and a solid overhead position.

Options

Chest to bar pull-ups → pull-ups

Overhead lunges → front rack lunges

Strongman

EMOM 10 Minutes of

Odd: Yoke front rack carry, Max distance

Even: Rest

 

Notes

Work on yoke carry technique. Place marks and create 10 or 20 meter distances depending on available space. Move constantly going the distances back and forth within each odd minute. Yoke + 40kg for men / Empty yoke for women.

Options

Yoke → barbell front rack carry (+ banded kettlebells)

—

 

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

10 rounds of

2 leg-less rope climbs

4 double kettlebell snatches 24/16kg – 53/35lb

12 push-ups hand release

 

Notes

Focus on proper form and quality of movements. Go each exercise unbroken, move fast on the rope with coordinated arm & leg movements, descend smoothly by sliding down or extending and jumping. Take adequate rest between exercises yet make sure you keep a steady tempo throughout the 10 rounds.

Options

Leg-less → regular rope climbs

24/16kg – 53/35lb → 20/12kg – 45/26lb

Engine

4 sets of

Ski-erg, 1000m

Run, 800m

Bike-erg, 2000m

Rest 5 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Spend 2 minutes on each ergometer at an easy pace as warm-up. Maintain a steady pace in each set and increase progressively throughout the session, work at 82-90% of Max effort.

Options

Ski-erg → row

Bike-erg → 2,5km Assault bike
