Power Snatch

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

2×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

5×1 @ 90%


Warm up

5 sets of

1 snatch pulls + 3 low hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2-3 power snatches until 60%.


Goal

Improve strength and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.


Movements

Focus on an explosive pull maintaining the barbell close to your body’s vertical line.

—


Snatch High Pull

5×5 @ 90% of Power Snatch


Warm up

Continue from the percentage you left the power snatch.

Goal

Improve technique and weightlifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.


Movements

Focus on a controlled pull until the barbell is over the knees, then increase speed until the barbell is as high as possible. Keep the barbell close to your body’s vertical line.

Goal & Movements

This session consists of two identical exercise patterns divided by 60 meters handstand walk. Pace the first portion and push the second portion hard to go for the strong finish. Go the GHD sit-ups and single-leg squats unbroken at a steady tempo. Break the handstand walk into 10-meter unbroken distances.


Options

Gymnastics – Stamina

10 rounds of

1 peg-board ascent

30 double-unders


Warm up

3 rounds of

5 peg-board pull ups

15 double-Unders


Goal

Focus on technique and transitions. Try to maintain a steady tempo with equally time completion of each round throughout.


Movements

Be technical on the board, remember that you need to rotate your grip in order to safely insert the stick into the hole and push your legs against the board as a counter power to ensure balance. Go the double-unders unbroken.


Options

Peg-board ascent —> half pegboard ascent —> 5 peg-board pull-ups
