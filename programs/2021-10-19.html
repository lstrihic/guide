Hang Squat Clean

2×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

2×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

2 clean pulls + 2 hang power cleans + 2 hang squat cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang squat cleans until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and confidence. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a solid pull until the barbell is at hip height, then drive the barbell to a position over the knees, pull explosively with the barbel close to your body (proper lifting angles) and focus on a fast drive into a deep receiving position.

—

 

Clean Pull from blocks

2×3 @ 90% of Max Hang Squat Clean

2×2 @ 95%

2×1 @ 100%

 

Warm up

Continue to the clean pull from blocks at the percentage that you stopped the hang squat clean.

Goal

Improve pulling power and lifting angles. The starting position of the barbell should be approximately 10cm below the knees.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull until the barbell is at hip height and finish with an explosive shoulder shrug.

Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

Run, 600 meters

10 thrusters 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

10 box-jumps over 76/61cm – 30/24”

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Warm up

Run, 800 meters @ easy pace

Then,

2-3 rounds of

5 burpees

10 thrusters @20/15kg barbell

10 jump squats

5 box step-overs

Goal

Push steadily through the 1st and 2nd round and push the 3rd round hard.

Movements

Run at a steady sustainable pace throughout the session that will allow you to breathe and perform well on the exercises. On the box-jump overs maintain a pace that will ensure fast and efficient reps. Ideally go the thrusters unbroken, alternatively, break the thrusters of the 1st and 2nd round into a big and a smaller set and try to go the last set unbroken.

Options

70/52kg – 155/115lb. —> 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Barbell Cycling

A. For form

3 rounds of

6 hang power snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

6 overhead squats 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

 

Rest 90 seconds

 

B. For form

2 rounds of

9 hang power snatches 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

9 overhead squats 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

 

Rest 90 seconds

 

C. For form

18 hang power snatches 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

18 overhead squats 43/30kg – 95/65lb.

 

Warm up

Build up to 61/43kg – 135/95lb. of 2 hang power snatches + 2 overhead squats

Then,

2-3 sets of

12/9 calories, Assault bike @ fast pace

6 hang power snatches + 6 overhead squats 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

Rest 60 seconds between

Goal & Movements

The goal is to move steadily and efficiently with the barbell under fatigue. In part A go the hang power snatches unbroken and straight to an unbroken set of overhead squats, then drop the barbell, take a breath and continue at the same fashion. Alternatively, break into 5 unbroken hang power snatches, 1 hang power snatch + 6 overhead squats. Do the same for the part B. In part C break the hang power snatches into a big and no more than 2 smaller sets and go the overhead squats unbroken for as long as you can.

Engine – Running

10 rounds of

100 meters @ 85-95% of Max effort

300 meters @ easy pace

 

Warm up

2000 meters @ easy pace

Then,

2×30 meters of

Front swings

Side cross swings

Front leg raises

Toe taps

Then,

3×50 meters, Sprint

Goal & Pace

Start from 85% of Max effort (of 100 meters) and increase to a pace near 95% of Max effort. Avoid pushing each set to your limits. The aim is to focus on sprint mechanics and be consistent throughout. Jog for 300 meters in order to recover your breathing.

Cool down

1600 meters @ very easy pace
