Power Snatch

1×3 @ 70%

2×2 @ 80%

2×2 @ 85%

1×1 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

3×1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and increase weight progressively with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 70%. Improve power and stability. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid overhead receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

—

 

Snatch Pull

2×3 @ 90% of Max Power Snatch

2×2 @ 95%

2×2 @ 100%

 

Notes

Continue to the snatch pulls from where you left the power snatches. Improve starting strength. Start with a solid first pull and increase speed once the barbell is over the knees. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 5 seconds between lifts.

—

Conditioning

A. For time

3 rounds of

12 shoulders to overhead 61/43kg -135/95lb

21 chest to bar pull-ups

Cap: 8 minutes

 

Rest 5 minutes

 

B. For time

3 rounds of

21 shoulders to overhead 43/30kg – 95/65lb

12 bar muscle-ups

Cap: 8 minutes

 

Notes

This workout consists of 2 parts, try to achieve maximum performance in both of them. Ideally, go each exercise in each couplet unbroken with focus on the shoulders to overhead, if you need to break prefer to break a gymnastics exercise for minimum time and effort of repositioning. Alternatively break the exercise that your are not comfortable with from tha start into a big and 1-2 smaller sets with short to minimum rest between.

Options

A. 61/43kg -135/95lb → 52/37kg – 155/80lb

Chest to bar pull-ups → pull-ups

B. Bar muscle-ups → chest to bar pull-ups

1×6 @ 65%

1×4 @ 75%

5×2 @ 85%

Notes

Build up with sets of 5 reps until you reach 55%. Improve form and consistency. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets.

Gymnastics

5 sets of

AMRAP 2 Minutes of

15/12 calories, Assault bike

Max reps, Ring muscle-ups

Rest 2 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Your performance will decrease with each set yet try to put every time maximum effort. Push explosively through the calories on the assault bike, then take a breath and go for a big unbroken set of muscle-ups, continue with some smaller sets with minimum rest between. Don’t forget to take care of your arms while resting.

Options

Ring muscle-ups → chest to rings pull-ups

Engine

AMRAP 20 Minutes of

8 bar-facing burpees over

14/12 calories, Row

46 double-unders

 

Notes

Warm up rowing 4 minutes at an easy pace. In the main session move steadily through the 20 minutes at a fast yet sustainable pace. Go the double unders unbroken for as long as possible. Perform smooth transitions between exercises and try to complete each round equally fast.
