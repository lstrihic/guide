Hang Power Clean

2×3 @ 50%

2×3 @ 60%

2×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

3×1 @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 50%. Improve technique and explosiveness. Focus on a solid hang position and an explosive pull with proper angles. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

—

 

Clean High Pull

2×5 @ 85%

2×4 @ 95%

2×3 @ 105%

 

Notes

Continue from where you left the hang power cleans. Improve pulling power and angles. Pull high with the barbell on a vertical trajectory and finish with an explosive shoulder shrug. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 2 minutes between sets, no more than 5 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

AMRAP 15 Minutes of

5 double dumbbell thrusters 22,5k/15g – 50/35lb.

10 strict handstand push-ups

15 box-jumps 60/50cm – 24/20”

 

Notes

Focus on consistency, move steadily through the biggest part of the workout, 10+ minutes, and then start pushing harder increasing your tempo and speeding up your transitions between exercises. Create a working space that will make it easier for you to move from exercise to exercise. Go the double dumbbell thrusters unbroken and the box jumps at a fast, sustainable pace (ideally touch & go). Prefer to break the strict handstand push-ups from the start into sets of 6-4 or 5-5 reps that will allow you to move efficiently through the total volume. As you reach the finish go for unbroken handstand push-ups and try to minimise your transitions.

Options

22,5/15g – 50/35lb → 15/10kg – 35/25lb

Strict → kipping handstand push-ups

*go with strict if you can perform easily 10 unbroken reps while fresh

Barbell Cycling

For form

5 rounds of

30 double-unders

15 deadlifts @ 70/47,5kg – 154/105lb

30 double-unders

7 shoulders to overhead 70/47,5kg – 154/105lb

 

Notes

The goal is to move efficiently with the barbell under fatigue. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible, if you break immediately continue with the remaining reps. Ideally go the deadlifts and the shoulders to overhead unbroken, alternatively break into a big and smaller set with minimum rest between. If you are to choose between the two exercises, prefer to break the deadlifts and push the shoulder to overhead for the unbroken. Take short yet adequate rest between exercises that will allow you to move efficiently.

Options

70/47,5kg – 154/105lb → 61/43kg – 135/95lb

Gymnastics

For form

10 rounds of

1 leg-less rope climb

3 metal parallettes handstand push-ups

10 meters, Handstand walk

 

Notes

Focus on quality of movements and full range of motion. Try to move steadily and at a good tempo throughout each round, take short yet adequate rest between rounds. Go the leg-less rope climbs with proper upper-lower body coordination, preferably leg kicks or kipping. Take a moment to boost your confidence and perform 3 solid handstand push-ups, then without much hesitation continue with the handstand walk. Move fast with short and quick steps, ideally going the 10 meters unbroken, alternatively by 2×5 meters unbroken.

Options

Leg-less → regular

Metal parallettes —> deficit on plates (15/10cm) → regular handstand push-ups

10 → 7,5 → 5 meters of

Handstand walk* → 2 wall-walk

*Choose an option that you can perform unbroken for a whole round while fresh.

Engine

A. Run, 15 minutes @ easy pace

 

B. 4 sets of

Run, 4×400 meters

Rest 60 seconds between portions

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Run for 15 minutes at an easy pace as warm up. The main session consists of 4 sets. Each set is 4 times of a 400-meter sprint with 60 seconds rest between. Avoid pushing too hard from the start, select a sustainable pace for each set and increase this pace progressively with each set. eg: 1st set all sprints at 1:25-1:45. 2nd set 1:20-1:40, 3rd set 1:15-1:35, 4th set 1:10-1:30. Jog for 5 minutes as cool down.

Options

4 → 3 sets of

Run, 4×400 meters
