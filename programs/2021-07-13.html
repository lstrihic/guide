Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 3 front squats + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat cleans + 2 jerks until 60%.

Goal

Improve technique and explosiveness. The lifts at 60% are 2 times of  2 squat cleans + 2 jerks. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between squat cleans.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull, a fast drive under the barbell and solid deep receiving positions on both cleans and jerks. Work and build confidence on split jerks since the percentages get near max heavy.

Barbell Cycling

5 sets of

Ski-erg, 21 calories

15 power snatches 35/25kg – 75/55lb.

9 hang squat snatches 35/25kg – 75/55lb.

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 overhead squats + 3 hang squat snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

2 rounds of

10 Calories, Ski-erg @ fast pace

5 power snatches

3 hang squat snatches

Goal

The goal is to achieve high performance in each set by moving efficiently with the barbell. Push fast and explosively through the calories on the ski-erg and go straight to the barbell. Don’t hesitate to push each set hard.

Movements

The weight on the barbell will allow you to attempt going each set unbroken, yet if you struggle, prefer to go the power snatches touch & go and the squat snatches with single lifts.
