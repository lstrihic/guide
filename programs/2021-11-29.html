Power Snatch with 2” pause below knees

2×3 @ 60% of Max Power Snatch

2×2 @ 65%

2×2 @ 70%

3×1 @ 75%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch pulls until below knees + 3 hang power snatches below knees + 3 half squat snatch balances @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power snatches with 2” pause below knees until 60%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the pull pause position. The position of the barbell is 10cm below the knees. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Movements

First deadlift the barbell to a height 10cm below your knees, pause for 2 seconds at a solid position and continue with an explosive pull.

Squat Snatch + Overhead Squat

1×3+1 @ 55% of Max Squat Snatch

2×2+1 @ 60%

1×2+1 @ 65%

1×2+1 @ 70%

1×1+1 @ 75%

5×1+1 @ 80%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

1 snatch pull + 2 hang power snatches + 3 overhead squats @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat snatches + 1 overhead squat until 55%.

Goal

Improve technique and stability. The lifts at 55% are 3 squat snatches + 1 overhead squat, at 60% are 2 times 2 squat snatches + 1 overhead squat. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Once you have established the overhead position continue with slow controlled overhead squats.

Goal & Movements

Move steadily through the biggest part of each AMRAP, 5+ minutes, and then start pushing harder by shortening your transitions.

Movements

Move steadily on the ergs at a sustainable pace and push them harder only if you are near the finish. Go all other exercises unbroken with smooth transitions between them. On the wall walks make sure you perform proper reps with full range of motion. Single arm dumbbell devils presses are alternating, switch arms once the dumbbell is on the ground. Break the single arm dumbbell overhead squats into 6 left arm | 6 right arm.

Back Squat

5 Rep Max

Then

EMOM 5 Minute of

1 reps @ 5RM load – explosive

 

Warm up

Sets of 3 reps < 80% and 5 reps > 80%.

Goal

Improve strength. Build up to the maximum load with which you can complete 5 reps. Once you find the max load continue for 5 minutes (EMOM style) performing 1 rep at the established 5-rep max load. Once you complete each set rest until the next minute starts.

Movements

Go the singles with focus on an explosive squat from the deep position to the standing position.

Gymnastics Skill

Practice Free Handstand Hold

 

Goal & Movements

Work for 15 minutes on free handstand hold. Try to establish a proper position with your body straight (hollow) your shoulders tight and your legs straight with your toes pointing upwards. Ideally, stay still where you stand, alternatively work within a square meter.

Engine

Work @ 80-85% of VO2Max

Row, 2000 meters

60 calories, Assault bike

300 double-unders

60 calories, Assault bike

Row, 2000 meters

 

Goal & pace

It’s all about pace, maintain a steady sustainable pace on the row and assault bike prior to the double-unders that will allow you to breathe well, and move efficiently on the double-unders. The challenge for those that are not comfortable with them is the double unders. If you feel confident, go unbroken for as long as you can, if not, prefer to break into big sets. Once you complete the double-unders start pushing harder, try to maintain the same or ideally faster pace than that of the 1st portion.
