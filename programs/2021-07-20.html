Power Clean + Jerk

1×3+2 @ 60% of Max Power Clean

2×2+2 @ 65%

2×2+2 @ 70%

1×2+2 @ 80%

1×1+2 @ 85%

3×1+2 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power cleans + 2 jerks until 60%.

Goal

Improve power and stability. The lifts at 60% are 3 power cleans + 2 jerks. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between power cleans.

Movements

Focus on a controlled first pull until the knees, a powerful second pull and solid receiving positions. Work and build confidence on split jerks since this is the most appropriate jerk technique when it comes to heavy weights.

—

 

Clean Pull

3×3 @ 95% of Max Power Clean

 

Warm up

Continue to the clean pull from the percentage you finished the power clean.

Goal

Improve pulling strength. Go with single lifts, rest no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Keep the barbell as close to your body’s vertical line as you can and finish the movement with a body extension and an explosive shoulder shrug..

Strength Stamina

10 rounds of

Row, 12 calories

3 deadlifts @ 80% – dead stop

 

Warm up

Row 40 calories

Then,

Sets of 2-3 deadlifts until 80%.

Then,

2-3 sets of

10 calories, Row @ fast pace

2 deadlifts 70-80%.

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal

The goal is to push efficiently through the 10 rounds at a steady tempo with solid lifts and equal transitions between rounds.

Movements

Row at a fast yet sustainable pace throughout the session, then take a breath and go for deadlifts. On the deadlifts, go with single lifts without losing contact (your grip) with the barbell, you should not go touch & go but not take your grip off the barbell either.

Gymnastics Strength

For form

10 sets of

AMRAP 60 seconds of

1 legless rope climb 4,6m – 15ft.

Max reps, Strict ring dips

*Vest 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

Rest 60 seconds between sets

 

Warm up

3 sets of

1 legless rope climb

8-10 strict ring dips

Rest 60 seconds between sets

*Start with no vest and then add it

Goal & Movements

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Complete a fast rope climb using your legs and hips (kicks or keeping technique), to assist your upper body on your ascent and descent smoothly by sliding down, then go straight to the strict ring dips. Break into sets that will allow you to keep working for the whole 60-second window.

Options

Leg-less —> regular rope climbs

*Go leg-less for as far as possible then switch to regular

Strict ring dips —> ring push-ups

*Go with ring push-ups if you can’t perform at least 5 unbroken reps of strict ring dips while fresh
