Snatch Balance + Overhead Squat

3×1+3 @ 70% of Max Snatch Balance

2×1+2 @ 75%

1×1+1 @ 80%

1×1+1 @ 85%

1×1+1 @ 90%

1×1+1 @ 95%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2 snatch balance + 2 overhead squats until you reach 70%. The lifts at 70% are 3 times of 1 snatch balance + 3 overhead squats, at 80% are 1 snatch balance + 1 overhead squat. Work on technique and stability. Focus on a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Power Snatch

Every 2 Minutes for 10 Minutes (5 sets)

3 reps @ 85%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with 3 reps < 70% and 2 reps < 85%. Complete 3 power snatches within a 2-minute time frame and rest until the next 2-minute window starts. Continue in this fashion for 10 minutes in total.

Conditioning

For time

Row, 2000 meters

60 meters, Handstand walk

5 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

Row, 1000 meters

40 meters, Handstand walk

4 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

Row, 500 meters

20 meters, Handstand walk

3 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

Cap: 25 minutes

 

Notes

Row at a fast steady pace, 1:50.0-1:55.0/500m for men | 1:57.0-2:03/500m for women and go straight to the handstand walk (5-meter distances). The challenge in short distances is to consistently dive quickly into each next distance without hesitation so make sure you perform super fast transitions. On the rope climbs, ascend fast using your legs to assist your upper body (kicks or kipping) and descend smoothly by sliding down extending your body and jumping on the ground. Try to breath well on the rower and increase effort as distance decreases.

Options

Handstand walk —> wall-walks 12.8.4 reps (1 wall-walk for each 5-meter portion of handstand walk )

Leg-less —> regular rope climbs

Engine

4 sets of

AMRAP 6 Minutes of

50/35 calories, Assault bike

150 double-unders

Max reps, Bar-facing burpees over

Rest 3 minute between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Move fast through the calories on the assault bike, start explosively and try to maintain the momentum for as long as possible. Go the double-unders unbroken, if you break immediately continue with the remaining reps. Go the bar-facing burpees over at a fast sustainable pace for the remaining time until the 6 minute mark.
