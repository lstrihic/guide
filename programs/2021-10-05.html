Conditioning

For time

3 rounds of

Ski-erg, 600 meters

15 bar muscle-ups

9 double kettlebell shoulders to overhead 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Warm up

Ski-erg, 600 meters

Then,

4.6.8

Kipping pull-ups

Push ups

Parallel dips

Goal

Push steadily through the total volume. Try to move in the last round at the same pace as in the first round.

Movements

Maintain a steady pace on the ski-erg throughout the session. On the bar muscle-ups ideally go unbroken, alternatively break into a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with minimum rest between. Try to go the shoulder to overhead unbroken effectively using your body to drive the kettlebells to the overhead position.

Options

24/16kg – 53/35lb. —> 16/12kg – 35/26lb.

Strength Stamina

For form

3 rounds of

40 heavy double-unders or 80 double-unders

5 snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

2 rounds of

40 heavy double-unders or 80 double-unders

5 snatches 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

1 round of

40 heavy double-unders or 80 double-unders

5 snatches 83/61kg – 185/135lb.

 

Warm up

Build up to 83/61kg – 185/135lb. of snatch.

Then,

3 sets of

12 heavy double-unders or 25 double-unders

2 snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Rest 60 seconds between sets

Goal & Movements

Move efficiently and steadily with the barbell for the whole duration of the session. As rounds of the couplet decrease, the weight of the snatch increases. If you are comfortable with heavy double-unders and have the appropriate jump rope then go for them. Try to go the heavy double-unders or double-unders unbroken each time, if you break take a breath and immediately continue with the remaining reps. On the snatches, ideally start the lightweight rounds by going unbroken with touch and go reps and switch to solid single lifts on the rounds where the barbell gets heavier. Alternatively, go all the snatches from the start with single lifts at a steady tempo.
