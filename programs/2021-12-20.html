1×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

4×1 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 snatch high pulls + 3 hang power snatches + 3 half squat snatch balances @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 hang power snatches until 60%.

Goal

Work on explosiveness and confidence on the hang position. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

First deadlift the barbell to hip height, then focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Avoid spending much time under tension holding the barbell at the hang position.

Low Hang Snatch High Pulls

2×2 @ 90% of Max Power Snatch

2×2 @ 95%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

2 pulls to low hang + 3 low hang snatch high pulls @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 low hang snatch high pulls until 90%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Start with a slow controlled pull of the barbell until 10cm below the knees, then focus on an explosive and technical high pull keeping the barbell always near your body’s vertical line. Drive the barbell smoothly back to the ground and reposition (below the knees).

2×4 @ 75%

3×3 @ 85%

 

Warm up

Sets of 2-3 reps until 85%.

Goal

Improve form and efficiency. Push steadily through each set at a solid tempo. Rest no less than 75 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on proper form. Maintain a straight back, a solid front rack position and strict motion at the bottom position (no bounce).

Gymnastics Skill

Forward roll to ring muscle-up

 

Goal

Work for 10-15 minutes on forward roll to ring muscle-up. Get on the rings with a strict ring muscle, then roll forward slowly and under control with focus on solid tension on the arms. Keep your arms contracted and continue rolling until you once again get yourself over the rings.

Options

Practice ring muscle-up drills

Engine

10 sets of

AMRAP 90 seconds of

15/12 calories, Assault bike

9 burpees to target*

Max reps, Double-unders

Rest 30 seconds between sets

 

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Maintain a sustainable tempo that will allow you to move efficiently in each set without significant variances on your pace.

Pace & Movements

Push fast and explosively on the assault bike, then move steadily on the burpees at a tempo that will allow you to breath and achieve proper reps (*target 10cm over your extended arms). Take a breath and go unbroken on the double-unders for as long as you can. Go for the achieved double-Unders of the 1st round in each next round.
