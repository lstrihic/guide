Squat Clean & Jerk

1×2 @ 60%

1×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 70%

1×1 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

3×1 @ 85%

 

Warm up

2 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 3 hang squat cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat cleans & jerk until 60%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and solid receiving positions. Work and build confidence on split jerks since this is the most appropriate jerk technique when it comes to heavy weights.

—

 

Push Press

5×5 @ 80%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

5 shoulder presses + 5 push presses @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Few sets of 2-3 push presses until 80%.

Goal

Work on power and explosiveness. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Go with powerful push presses using your legs and hips to drive the barbell overhead. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to a steady front rack position on the shoulders so that you are ready to go for the next rep.

Row, 60/45 calories

Cap: 25 minutes

 

Warm up

50 calories, Row

Then,

3 rounds of

10 GHD sit-ups

8 wall-balls

Goal & Movements

This conditioning workout is all about pace. Go the first calories at a fast yet manageable pace that will allow you to push efficiently through the exercises. Ideally go the GHD sit-ups and wall-balls unbroken at a steady tempo, alternatively, break the first round of wall-balls into a big and no more than 1-2 smaller sets with short rest between and go the last set of wall-balls unbroken for as long as you can. Push the last row hard, try to be keep an equal or or faster pace than that of the first cals of the workout.

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

5 pegboard ascents

10 double kettlebell shoulders to overhead 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

4 pegboard ascents

10 double kettlebell shoulders to overhead 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

3 pegboard ascents

10 double kettlebell shoulders to overhead 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

2 pegboard ascents

10 doublekettlebell shoulders to overhead 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

1 pegboard ascents

10 double kettlebell shoulders to overhead 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

 

Warm up

15 seconds, Hang from the pegboard

20 shoulder taps

12 ring rows

15 push ups

10 pegboard pull-ups

10 double kettlebell presses 12/8kg – 26/18lb.

Goal

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Perform smooth transitions between exercises that will allow you to focus on quality of movements.

Movements

Move fast on the pegboard, push your feet against the board to create stability, ascend and descend with swifts arm movements. The less you stay on the board the better. On the double kettlebell shoulders to overhead make sure you jerk the kettlebell, use your body to push and get under the weights so as to preserve your upper body as much as possible.

Options

Pegboard ascents —> leg-less rope climbs 4,6m -15ft.
