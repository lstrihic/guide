Power Clean

1×2 @ 65%

1×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

1×2 @ 85%

1×2 @ 90%

1×1 @ 95%

1×5 @ 75%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 65%. Improve power. Focus on a powerful pull and a solid receiving position. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Strongman/Gymnastics

For time

4 rounds of

20 meters, Sandbag bear hug carry 70/50kg – 155/110lb

7 ring muscle-ups

 

Notes

Move fast and efficiently. Find a comfortable hug position and go the 20 meters of sandbag carry unbroken. On the ring muscle-ups ideally go each round unbroken, alternatively prefer to start by breaking into a big and a smaller set with short rest between in order to keep moving and push harder the last 1-2 rounds for the unbroken.

Options

7 → 5 → 3 reps of

Ring muscle-ups → chest to rings pull-ups

Engine

8 rounds of

Run, 800 meters @ easy pace

Run, 200 meters @ fast pace*

 

Notes

Run a total distance of 8000 meters. The format is an 800-meter easy pace run and then a 200 meter sprint repeated 8 times. The easy pace run is your recovering period, breath well and relax your arms and legs. On the sprints make sure you start at a manageable pace 55 seconds and increase with each round reaching sub 40 seconds. Jog 400 meters as a cool down.

Options
8 → 7 → 6 rounds of

Run, 800 meters @ easy pace

Run, 200 meters @ fast pace*
