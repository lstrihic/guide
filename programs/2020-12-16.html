Conditioning

For time

60 meters, Single-arm dumbbell overhead lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

60 single-arm dumbbell snatches 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

60 handstand push-ups

60 meters, Handstand walk

Cap: 14 minutes

 

Notes

Place two marks to create a 10-meter distance. Go this distance back and forth to perform the overhead lunges and the handstand walk. Try to move as efficiently as possible with the dumbbell. Focus on movement mechanics and technique, make sure you keep your arm locked out on the lunges and move the dumbbell smoothly on the snatches. The less taxed you are after the dumbbells the better you will cope with the handstand. Break the handstand push-ups from the start into small, manageable sets of 5-8 reps, don’t hesitate to go with single reps if you struggle with doubles or more. On the handstand walk try to move fast and go for unbroken 10-meter distances. Every 2,5 meters is 1 rep.

Options

22,5/15kg – 50/35lb → 15/10kg – 35/25lb

Handstand push-ups → push-ups hand release

Handstand walk → 10 wall-walks

Engine

15 sets of

AMRAP 2 Minutes of

Assault bike, 12/9 calories

8 burpees to target*

Ski-erg, Max calories

Rest 1 minute between sets

 

Notes

Try to achieve high performance in each set. Push fast through the couplet assault bike-burpees, start explosively on the bike and then try to maintain the momentum. On the burpees go at a fast, steady tempo. *Mark a target 10cm over you, standing tall extending your arms overhead. Transitions will make the difference, make sure you move fast from exercise to exercise. On the Max calories maintain a fast, sustainable pace until the last second. Breathe when resting and try to regulate your cardio intensity.
