Engine

2 sets of

AMRAP 12 Minutes of

300 double-unders

75 bar-facing burpees over

Max calories, Assault or Echo bike

Rest 3 minute between sets

 

Notes

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each set. The 2nd set should ideally be as equal as possible to the 1st one. Try to breathe well throughout.

Movements & Tempo

Go the double Unders unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take a breath and continue with the remaining reps. On the bar facing burpees maintain a fast yet sustainable tempo, make sure every rep takes the same time and is identical to the previous one. Ideally work on an echo bike, alternatively an assault bike. Start steadily on the bike and increase your pace as you push throughout the cals, less time means faster increase, more time steadier increase.
