Conditioning

OPEN 21.0

100 double-unders

15 clean jerks @ 43/30kg – 95/65lb

100 double-unders

12 clean jerks @ 61/43kg – 135/95lb

100 double-unders

9 clean jerks @ 83/52kg – 185/115lb

100 double-unders

7 clean jerks @ 102/61kg – 225/135lb

100 double-unders

5 clean jerks @ 120/70kg – 265/155lb

Cap: 15 minutes

Notes

This is one of our Open style workouts, consider it as the real deal and give it your best shot! The challenge is the heavier lifts, you may be able to reach the heaviest load or a lighter load depending on your clean & jerk PR, in any case, try to move fast until that point. Go the double-unders unbroken or unbroken for as long as possible, then catch a breath and continue with the clean & jerks. Go with smooth transitions from DUs to lifts, fast transitions from lifts to DUs. Go the lighter weights (rounds of 15 & 12 reps) with a big touch & go set and then fast single lifts. On the heavier loads (rounds of 9,7 & 5 reps) go with single lifts at a solid tempo. Perform the clean & jerks anyhow you want, yet we recommend you start with powers cleans & touch & go power jerks and then switch to squat cleans and split jerks on the heavier loads

If you know that a given load is higher than your PR note down the time you finished the lifts of the previous load.

Strength/Gymnastics

For time

60 meters, Sled push + 20/15kg – 45/35lb

60 meters, Handstand walk

60 meters, Sled push + 40/30kg – 90/70lb

Notes

Use two marks to designate a 10-meter back and forth distance. Try to go each distance unbroken at a steady pace and perform fast transitions between distances and on the weight increase. Since the load is relatively light prefer to use the bent arms, sled near the chest pushing technique.

Options

Sled push → box drag

Handstand walk → 12 wall-walks

Notes

This is a total work volume of 8000 meters, complete 2km run + 2km row + 2km run + 2km row. *Use a vest in the first round, remove the vest in the second round.
