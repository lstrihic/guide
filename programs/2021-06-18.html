Conditioning

For time

Run, 400 meters

Then,

3 rounds of

21 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

21 toes bar

7 power cleans 83/61kg – 180/135lb.

7 bar muscle-ups

Then,

Run, 400 meters

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Notes

Start the workout with 400 meters run at a steady pace with focus on breathing well. Continue to the 3 rounds by moving steadily through the exercises. Go the wall-balls unbroken, break the toes to bar into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. Prefer to perform solid single lifts at a fast tempo and attempt unbroken bar muscle-up sets. On the last run give everything you got.

Options

83/61kg – 180/135lb. —> 70/52kg – 155/115lb.

Bar muscle-ups —> chest to bar pull-ups

*Go with chest to bar pull-ups if you can’t perform at least 5 unbroken bar muscle-ups while fresh.

Strength/Gymnastics Conditioning

For form

30 ring muscle-ups

30 meters, Shoulders rack lunges 2×22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

300 double-unders

20 ring muscle-ups

20 meters, Shoulders rack lunges 2×22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

200 double-unders

10 ring muscle-ups

10 meters, Shoulders rack lunges 2×22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

100 double-unders

Cap: 30 minutes

 

Notes

The goal is to push efficiently through the muscle-ups with sets that will tackle a failed attempt and allow you to push through the total volume without excessive rest between breaks. Go the muscle-ups with manageable sets, > 5 reps. Break the shoulders rack lunges into 10-meter unbroken distances, focus on a solid position and steady lunges throughout the distances. Go the double-unders unbroken for as long as possible, if you break immediately continue with the remaining reps. Instead of pushing harder with each round, prefer to be consistent through the session and push steadily without long breaks.

Options

Ring muscle-ups —> ring dips —> ring push-ups

*Choose an option that you can easily perform 10 unbroken reps while fresh.

Engine – Rowing

A1. Row 1000 meters @ 22-24 strokes. 

 

A2. Row 3x250m  @ Time Trial pace

Rest 90 seconds between

 

B. Time trial

Row, 2000 meters

 

Notes

Warm up by rowing 1000 meters at a low stroke rate and prime by completing 3 sprints at the intended time trial pace prior to the main session. In the main session go for a 2000 meter official time trial. Go with free strokes and try to achieve maximum performance. This is an important session as it designates the reference point of the given paces that you work with through your training cycles.
