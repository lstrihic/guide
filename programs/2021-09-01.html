Power Clean

2×3 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×1 @ 80%

5×1 @ 85%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2-3 power cleans until 60%.

Goal

Improve power. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between power cleans.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull and a solid receiving position. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees and the barbell shoulder rest at a solid front rack positions.

—

 

Push Press + Jerk

2×3+1 @ 60% of Max Push Press

2×2+1 @ 70%

1×1+1 @ 80%

1×1+2 @ 85%

3×1+2 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

1 power clean + 4 presses + 3 push presses + 2 push jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 push presses + 2 jerks until 60%.

Goal

Work on power and explosiveness. The lifts at 60% are 2 times of 3 push presses + 1 jerk. Prefer to work on push jerks. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Go with powerful push presses using your legs and hips to drive the barbell overhead and continue with explosive jerks with focus on a solid deep receiving position. Make sure you drive smoothly and under control the barbell back to a steady front rack position on the shoulders so that you are ready to go for the next rep.

Conditioning

A. For time

40 meters, Handstand walk

30 toes to bar

20 double dumbbell devil’s press 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

30 toes to bar

40 meters, Handstand walk

Cap: 12 minutes

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. For time

40 meters, Double dumbbell front rack lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

30 GHD sit-ups

20 bar muscle-ups

30 GHD sit-ups

40 meters, Double dumbbell front rack lunges 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb.

Cap: 12 minutes

 

Warm up

10 meters, Handstand walk

8 toes to bar

3 double dumbbell devil’s presses

10 double dumbbell front rack lunges

8 GHD sit-ups

3 bar muscle-ups

Goal

Try to achieve high performance in each part. Each part consists of 2 portions of consecutive exercises in reverse order divided by either double dumbbell devil’s press or bar muscle-ups . The goal is to pace the first portion until you complete these exercises and then start pushing harder for a strong finish.

Movements

Break the handstand walk and the double dumbbell front rack lunges into 10-meter unbroken distances. Prefer to go the toes to bar with manageable sets and go the GHD sit-ups unbroken for as long as you can at a steady sustainable tempo. Break the bar muscle ups into a big and no more than 2-3 smaller sets and move constantly and steadily on the double dumbbell devil’s presses.

Back Squat

ΕΜΟΜ 10 Minutes of

Odd: 2 reps @ 70% (explosive eccentric phase)

Even: 8 reps @ 60%

 

Warm up

Sets of 6 reps until you reach 60%.

Goal

Improve consistency. Work for 10 minutes, every odd minute perform 2 lifts at 70% and every even minute remove some weight from the barbell so that you perform 8 reps at 60%. Once you complete each set rest until the next minute starts.

Movements

Go the doubles with focus on an explosive squat from the deep position to the standing position (eccentric phase) and maintain a steady tempo on the 8-rep sets.

Gymnastics

For form

4 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

8 strict deficit handstand push-ups 20/15cm – 8/6”

3 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

8 strict deficit handstand push-ups 20/15cm – 8/6”

2 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

8 strict deficit handstand push-ups 20/15cm – 8/6”

1 legless rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

8 strict deficit handstand push-ups 20/15cm – 8/6”

 

Warm up

3 rounds of

6 rope pull-ups

6 pike push-ups

Goal & Movements

Focus on proper form and full range of motion. Move fast on the rope by kicking with your legs to assist your upper body, descend smoothly by sliding down, extending your body and jumping on the ground. Ideally go the strict deficit handstand push-ups unbroken, alternatively break into a big and a smaller set with short rest between.

Options

Legless —> regular rope climbs

Deficit —> 10/5cm – 4/2” —> regular strict handstand push-ups —> pike push-ups (elevated feet)

Goal & Movements
