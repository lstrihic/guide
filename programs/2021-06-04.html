Hang Squat Snatch

1×3 @ 60%

1×2 @ 70%

1×2 @ 75%

1×2 @ 80%

1×1 @ 85%

3×1 @ 90%

 

Notes

Warm up with an empty barbell and progressively increase weight with sets of 2-3 reps until you reach 60%. Work on explosiveness and consistency at the hang position. Focus on an explosive pull and a super fast drive under the barbell.  Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

—

 

Snatch Pull

5×1 @ 100% of Max Squat Snatch

 

Notes

Start the snatch pull from where you left the hang squat snatch, continue from that point with sets of 2 pulls until you reach 100% of Max Squat Snatch. Work on pulling strength. Rest no less than 90 seconds between lifts.

Conditioning

For time

30 meters, Handstand walk

5 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

150 double-unders

75 wall-balls 9/6kg – 20/14lb.

150 double-unders

5 rope climbs 4,6m – 15ft.

30 meters, Handstand walk

Cap: 20 minutes

 

Notes

This workout has two sequences of the same exercises in an inverse order. Move at two tempos, maintain a steady pace until you complete the 75 wall-balls and then start pushing harder to go for the strong finish. Ideally go the wall-balls unbroken for as long as you can, if you break take minimum rest and continue. Alternatively, break into a big and a smaller set. Break the handstand walk into 10-meter unbroken distances and go the double-unders unbroken for as long as you can, if you break immediately continue with the remaining reps. On the rope climb move fast by finding solid footing and squatting on the rope to assist your upper body, descend smoothly by sliding down and jumping on the ground.

Options

Handstand Walk —> 6 wall walks

Strength/Gymnastics

For form

5 rounds of

20 GHD sit-ups

15 double ketllebell swings 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

10 strict handstand push-ups

5 double ketllebell thrusters 24/16kg – 53/35lb.

 

Notes

Focus on quality and full range of motion. Go the GHD sit-ups unbroken for as long as you can. Ideally, go the kettlebell exercises unbroken as well, alternatively break the swings in each round into a big and a smaller set with short rest between. If you are comfortable with strict handstand push-ups attempt to complete each round unbroken and then break into more manageable sets, if not, prefer to break each round from the start into a big and smaller set. Perform smooth transitions that will allow you move efficiently throughout.

Options

24/16kg – 53/35lb. —> 20/12kg – 44/26lb.

Strict hspu —> pike push-ups (elevated feet)

B. Main Session

7 sets of

500 meters @ free rate

Rest 90 seconds between sets

Pace : 2km PR pace + 4 seconds

 

Notes

Row 1000 meters at an easy pace as warm and continue with 2 short distance sprints at a high stroke rate, 28+ strokes, to prep your cardiovascular for the main session’ s sprints. In the main session perform 7 sets with 90 seconds rest between, follow the given pace and a free stroke rate to achieve that pace. Keep in mind that more strokes mean higher cardiovascular tax, fewer strokes mean need of powerful strokes. Row 800 meters at very light pressure as cool down.
