Warm up

2 sets of

4 snatch pulls + 3 hang power snatches @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power snatches until 55%.

Goal

Improve confidence and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on a powerful pull and a steady receiving position. Hip crease should be over 90 degrees, shoulders tight and elbows locked out.

1×2 @ 60%

1×1 @ 80%

1×2 @ 85%

3×2 @ 90%

 

Warm up

3 sets of

3 clean pulls + 3 hang power cleans + 2 presses + 2 push presses + 2 jerks @ 20/15kg barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 power cleans + 2 jerks until 50%.

Goal

Improve power and stability. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 15 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled first pull until the knees, a powerful second pull and solid receiving positions. Prefer to go with split jerks in order to improve efficiency on the heavy weights.
