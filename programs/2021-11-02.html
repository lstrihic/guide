Warm up

3 sets of

2 clean pulls + 2 hang power cleans cleans + 2 front squats @ 20/15kg  barbell

Then,

Sets of 2 squat cleans until 50%.

Goal

Improve explosiveness and confidence. Rest no less than 90 seconds between sets.

Movements

Focus on an explosive pull, a fast drive under the barbell and a solid deep receiving position.

—

 

Clean Pull

3×3 @ 95% of Max Squat Clean

 

Warm up

Continue to the clean pull from the percentage that you stopped the squat clean.

Goal

Improve pulling strength and weightlifting angles. Go with single lifts, rest no less than 90 seconds between sets, no more than 10 seconds between lifts.

Movements

Focus on a controlled starting pull until the barbell is just over the knees and then increase speed as you pull the barbell over hip height.

Conditioning

For time

4 rounds of

21/16 calories, Ski-erg

21 GHD sit-ups with med-ball 6/4kg – 14/10lb.

Run, 200 meters

Cap: 15 minutes

 

Warm up

500 meters, Run @ easy pace

Then,

2-3 sets of

9 GHD sit-ups

7 calories, Ski-erg @ fast pace

5 GHD sit-ups with med-ball

Rest 30 seconds between sets

Goal & Movements

The goal is to start at a solid pace and push harder as you reach the end. Move fast on the ski explosively pushing through the given calories. On the GHD sit-ups try to go unbroken at a steady sustainable tempo, if this is not possible break from the start into a big and a smaller set with minimum rest between. Run at a steady pace throughout the session that will allow you to breathe and go the last run as fast as you can reaching a max effort pace.

Barbell Cycling

A. For form

5 rounds of

7 hang power snatches 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

5 overhead squats 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

3 squat snatches 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

 

Rest 3 minutes

 

B. For form

5 rounds of

9 hang power snatches 34/25kg – 75/55lb.

7 overhead squats 34/25kg – 75/55lb.

5 squat snatches 34/25kg – 75/55lb.

 

Warm up

Sets of 3 hang power snatches + 2 overhead squats + 1 squat snatch until you reach 52/37kg – 115/80lb.

Goal & Movements

The goal is to move steadily and efficiently with the barbell. Go the hang power snatches and overhead squats unbroken at a steady tempo without dropping the barbell on the ground. Then prefer to go the squat snatches with fast single lifts and, as you do that, try to recover your breathing as much as you can. Once you finish the round catch a short breath and continue to the next round in the same fashion.

Engine  – Running

3 sets of

1000 meters @ 3’30”-4’15”km

Rest 3 minutes between sets

 

Warm up

15 minutes @ progressively increased pace

Goal & Pace

The aim is at promoting Vo2max and lactate threshold. Push your pace up to 90% of Max effort in each set. Recover by 3 minutes walking between sets.

Cool down

10 minutes @ very easy pace
