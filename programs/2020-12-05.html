Power clean + Jerk

1×2 + 1 @ 50% of power clean

1×2 + 1 @ 60%

1×1 + 1 @ 70%

1×1 + 1 @ 75%

5×1 + 2 @ 80%

 

Notes

The lifts at 50% are 1 time of 2 power cleans + 1 jerk, at 80% are 5 times of 1 power clean + 2 jerks. Focus on powerful pulls and solid, technical power or split jerks. Warm up with an empty barbell and continue with sets of 2 power cleans + 2 jerks until you reach 50%. Rest no less than 2 minutes between sets (times).

Conditioning    

For time

20 bar-facing burpees

10.8.6.4.2 reps of

Squat snatches

43.61.83.90.102kg – 95.135.155.200.225lb (men)

30.43.52.57.61kg – 65.95.115.125.135lb (women)

Cap: 20 Minutes

 

Notes

Complete 20 bar-facing burpees + 10 squat snatches + 20 bar-facing burpees + 8 squat snatches …. + 20 bar-facing burpees + 2 squat snatches as fast as possible. Increase the load on the barbell in each round as prescribed above. Maintain a steady, sustainable pace on the burpees throughout the workout with focus on breathing well. The goal is to push efficiently through the squat snatches at a fast, solid tempo. Go all the squat snatches with solid, single lifts, the more consistently you keep you rest between lifts short and equal as load increases, the better your performance will be. Move fast from lifts to burpees, smoothly from burpees to lifts.

Strongman

5 sets of

20 meters, Sled arm-over-arm pull

Rest 2 minutes between sets

 

Notes

Depending on the available space, work on 20 meters or 2×10 meters with a 180-degree turn of the sled on the transition. Start with a sled + 20/15kg – 45/35lb plate and add weight progressively until you reach the 5th round with a heavy, challenging weight for the 20 meters. Stand at a half squat position, load your weight backwards and work with your arms.

Strength/Gymnastics

For time

10 rounds of

2 strict ring muscle-ups

4 strict deficit handstand push-ups (10/5cm height)

6 double dumbbell step-ups over 22,5/15kg – 50/35lb

 

Notes

Go with fast reps and focus on full range of motion. Push steadily through the biggest part of the workout (7-8 rounds) with smooth transitions between exercises that will allow you to keep moving efficiently. As you reach the finish, if you feel comfortable enough, push harder by shortening your transitions to the minimum.

Options

Strict muscle-ups → false grip strict chest to rings pull-ups

Deficit → regular strict handstand push-ups
