Notes

The goal is to reach efficiently past the 100 overhead squats and then start pushing harder as you once again return to the starting exercise. The most challenging part is the overhead squats, find a solid overhead position and try to move consistently throughout the total reps. Ideally go all other exercises unbroken for as long as possible, take minimum rest and continue. Alternatively, break the shoulders to overhead and/or deadlifts into a big and a smaller set with short to minimum rest between.

Barbell Cycling

AMRAP 10 Minutes of

10 snatches 61/43kg – 135/95lb.

Rest 1 minute

10 snatches 83/52kg  – 180/115lb.

Rest 1 minute

10 snatches 102/61kg – 225/135lb.

Rest 1 minute

Max snatches 111/70kg – 245/155lb.

 

Notes

The difficulty is increasing as weight is added on the barbell and tax is accumulating. It is recommended that you start with power snatches and then switch to squat snatches as the barbell gets heavier. Ideally, go the lightweight snatches unbroken with touch and go power snatches, alternatively break into no more than 2-3 touch and go sets. Go the second part unbroken for as long as possible and continue with single lifts with minimum rest between. Go the third part with solid single lifts at a steady tempo, take adequate rest in order to ensure successfully made snatches. When/If you reach the last part, gather your strength build your confidence and attempt solid successfully made lifts.

Gymnastics Complex

3 sets of

5 toes to bar

5 pull-ups

5 toes to bar

5 chest to bar pull-ups

5 toes to bar

5 bar muscle-ups

Rest as needed between sets

 

Notes

Each set is a complex, try to go all movements unbroken. Focus on smooth transitions from kipping (toes to bar) to butterfly technique (pull-up, CH2B and muscle-up). The more technical you are and the better your transitions the more efficiently you will push through each set.

Options

5 —> 4 —> 3 reps of each movement

Chest to bar pull-ups instead of bar muscle-ups if you struggle with muscle-ups.

Engine – Running

A. 10 minutes @ easy pace

B. Every 3 Minutes for 30 Minutes of (10 sets)

400 meters @ 1’21”-1’36”

Notes

Warm up by running 10 minutes prior to the main session and cool down for 5 minutes afterwards at a comfortable pace of your choice. Perform 30-meter distances of front swings, side cross swings, front leg raise toe taps etc and finish with 3 sets of 50 meter sprints. Each time complete the sprint and rest until the next 3 minute mark. Focus on consistency, aim to achieve high performance in all sets working up to 93% of Vo2 Max. Try to continuously increase effort.
